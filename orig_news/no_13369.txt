Donald Trump released his first campaign ad of the general election on Friday, titled, "Two Americas: Immigration. "[The ad will air in Ohio, Pennsylvania, North Carolina and Florida over the next ten days. The campaign plans to spend $4. 8 million on the ad buy.  The 30 second ad shows a contrast between Trump’s immigration policy, which would provide "change that makes America safe again," versus Clinton’s immigration policy where "the system stays rigged against Americans" and "it’s more of the same, but worse. "
