Tuesday in New York City at a meeting of evangelical Christian leaders, presumptive Republican presidential nominee Donald Trump said there were questions about his general election opponent presumptive Democratic nominee Hillary Clinton’s religion.  Trump said, "We don’t know anything about Hillary in terms of religion. She’s been in the public eye for years and years, and yet there’s no  —   there’s nothing out there. There’s like nothing out there. It’s going to be an extension of Obama but it’s going to be worse, because with Obama you had your guard up. With Hillary you don’t. " He added, "We can’t be again politically correct and say we pray for all your leaders because all your leaders are selling Christianity down the tubes, selling the evangelicals down the tube.
