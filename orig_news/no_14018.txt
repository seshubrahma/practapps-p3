THE WHITE HOUSE, Office of the Press Secretary, FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE, January 19, 2017,   DAILY GUIDANCE AND PRESS SCHEDULE FOR, FRIDAY, JANUARY 20, 2017,   In the morning, the President and the First Lady will welcome   Trump and Mrs. Trump at the North Portico.  This event will be pooled press.   Later in the morning, the President and the First Lady will host a tea and coffee reception with   Trump and Mrs. Trump in the Blue Room.  This event will be closed press.   Afterward, the President and the First Lady will depart the White House en route the U. S. Capitol with   Trump and Mrs. Trump.  This event will be covered by the   travel pool.   In the afternoon, the President will attend the Inaugural   Ceremony for   Trump.  The Vice President, the First Lady, Dr. Biden, Vice   Pence, and Mrs. Pence will also attend.  This event at the U. S. Capitol will be open to   media.   Later in the afternoon, President Obama and Mrs. Obama will depart the U. S. Capitol en route Joint Base Andrews.  While at Joint Base Andrews, President Obama will deliver remarks at a farewell event with staff.  The departure at the U. S. Capitol will be open to   media, and the arrival and remarks at Joint Base Andrews will be pooled press.   Afterward, President Obama and Mrs. Obama will depart Joint Base Andrews en route Palm Springs, California.  The departure from Joint Base Andrews will be pooled press.
