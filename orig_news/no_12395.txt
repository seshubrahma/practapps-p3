Former Mexican president Felipe Calderón said Mexico won’t be paying for a wall to be built along America’s southern border as GOP frontrunner Donald Trump has vowed to build at the expense of Mexico. ["We are not going to pay any single cent for such a stupid wall! And it’s going to be completely useless," Calderón said.  During an interview with CNBC’s Hadley Gamble, he said it’s "incredible" that the United States  —   an "admirable society"  —   has presidential candidates such as Trump. Calderón responded to Trump’s tough stance on immigration, saying, "The first loser of such a policy would be the United States. If this guy pretends that closing the borders to anywhere, either for trade [or] for people, is going to provide prosperity to the United States, he is completely crazy.,
