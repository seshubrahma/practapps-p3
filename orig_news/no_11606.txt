Sunday on CBS’s "Face the Nation," presumptive Republican presidential nominee Donald Trump said political correctness was killing Americans and although he dislikes the concept, the United States is going to have to look seriously at profiling terror suspects.  Trump said, "Everybody wants to be so politically correct and they want to do what is right and afraid to do anything. the words are killing us, political correctness. " When asked about profiling Trump said, "Well, I think profiling is something that we’re going to have to start thinking about as a country and other countries do it. You look at Israel you look at others, they do it. They do it successfully. I hate the concept of profiling, but we have to start using common sense and we have to use  —   use our heads  …     We really have to look at profiling. We have to look at is seriously.
