Politico first reported on the security discussion at the GOP meeting. Last month’s retreat in Philadelphia with House and Senate Republicans drew hundreds of protesters, mostly there to voice opposition to Trump, who was invited to address the members.   Several members told CNN that after a series of protests in cities across the country following the inauguration they were being advised to brief staff on how to deal with groups that may show up at their offices. Virginia GOP Rep. Dave Brat blamed professional advocates from outside his district for interfering with his office’s work.
