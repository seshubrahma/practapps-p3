Bill Clinton’s foundation began largely to support the building of his presidential library in Little Rock, Arkansas. As his   House ambitions grew, so did the foundation, ballooning into a $2 billion charity focused on global health, climate change and other international efforts. The former president has leveraged his contacts to fill the foundation’s coffers and traveled the world to meet with people helped by its work.
