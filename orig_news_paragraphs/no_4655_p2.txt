The plaintiff states in the original TRO lawsuit —  a growing list that currently includes Washington, Minnesota, and Oregon —   asked Robart to rule that the TRO blocking EO 13769 likewise applies to the new EO. Robart released an order Friday stating that none of the parties have properly filed new motions in that litigation   —   and that he will reserve judgment until there is a relevant motion and the legal issues have been fully briefed before his court.
