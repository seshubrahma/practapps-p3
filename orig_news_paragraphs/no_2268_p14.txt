Ben S. in New York, reacting to an article about rifts on many college campuses between Jewish students and others protesting Israel’s treatment of Palestinians. 6. I have withheld my donations from the University of Maryland over their whitewashing of the history of our campus and the removal of [Harry] Byrd (the man who built the stadium) from our football stadium. I’m liberal and a woman, but this trend to strip all history from our   institutions bugs the heck out of me.
