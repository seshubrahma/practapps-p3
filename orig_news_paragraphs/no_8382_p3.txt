Now conditions in China threaten to derail the U. S. recovery. Beijing’s statisticians report China’s growth slowed to  6. 9 percent in 2015, down from double digits a few years ago. Western estimates are as low as 4 percent, and much of that is decadent. Building apartments and office complexes that attract no tenants, and entire ghost cities, count in China’s GDP tally but add little to productivity.
