Respondents were asked if they agreed or disagreed with this statement: "For years, the political elites have governed America for their own benefit —   and to the detriment of the American people. This election is the best chance in our lives to take back our government and change course. " In response, 43 percent said they strongly agreed, 17 percent somewhat agreed, 14 percent somewhat disagreed, 20 percent strongly disagreed and 6 percent didn’t know.
