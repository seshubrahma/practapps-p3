He routinely excoriated other politicians for   while repeatedly hogging center stage, his remarks interminable  —   after his Iowa victory, for example, or when he presumptuously introduced Carly Fiorina as his running mate  —   and his pauses so theatrically drawn out that you could watch the entirety of "The Revenant" during some of them.
