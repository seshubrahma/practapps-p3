And as Presidents Reagan and Bush realized, there are ways to package   tax cuts with rhetoric and targeted sweeteners so that working class conservatives believe they will be sharing a piece of this economic pie. Even though there is a lot of grumbling among Republicans about the impact on the deficits and proposals such as eliminating the deductions for state and local taxes, which will hit New York and California residents hard, Reagan also had trouble moving his tax bill in 1981. After the assassination attempt on his life, Reagan came back and mounted a   public relations blitz that was effective. Most Republicans who opposed him backed off.
