The militants recently released a video vowing to step up attacks against Christians, whom they regard as "infidels" empowering the West against Muslims. Egypt has struggled to combat a wave of Islamic militancy since the 2013 military overthrow of an elected Islamist president. The   IS affiliate has mainly attacked police and soldiers, but has also claimed bombings that killed civilians, including the downing of a Russian passenger jetliner in the Sinai in 2015, which killed all 224 people aboard and devastated Egypt’s tourism industry. Egypt’s Copts are one of the oldest Christian communities in the Middle East.
