Los Angeles Times "Black Twitter" correspondent Dexter Thomas has a New Year’s resolution for white people: dump Donald Trump. ["Trump is not a Republican problem. He’s a   problem," writes Thomas, in a provocative column entitled, "In 2016, white people must take responsibility for Donald Trump. "   In Thomas’s view, Trump is not only racist, but appeals to the nascent racism of white people in general —  and ignoring him is not an option.
