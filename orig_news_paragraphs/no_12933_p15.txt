Finally, the aforementioned shortfall of antibiotics has a larger echo in the overall shortfall of drug production, for both new and existing drugs.   While the drug shortage is not as severe as it was a few years ago, it’s still severe: There are notable shortages, for example, of pain medications, sedatives, electrolyte solutions, and antidotes to undo the ill effects of anesthesia.
