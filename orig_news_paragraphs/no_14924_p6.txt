The most remarkable thing about the Trump presidency may be our expectation that he would be any different. Trump first demonstrated his penchant for distortion and deception as a young man when he bragged about his accomplishments before he had any. Despite four massive corporate bankruptcies, each involving complex enterprises that required real executive skills, Trump insisted he was a great business leader.   What he was, in fact, was a successful entrepreneur who led   companies that he could direct like a monarch.
