Tapes of a 1979 meeting between Mr. Shenker and two mob figures  —    Joey (The Clown ) Lombardo and Allen Dorfman, a   liaison between the mob and Hoffa’s Teamsters union   —   revealed Mr. Dorfman complaining that Mr. Shenker had failed to pay him kickbacks on millions in loans that Mr. Shenker had procured from the Teamsters.
