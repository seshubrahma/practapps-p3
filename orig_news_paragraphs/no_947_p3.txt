Mr. Trump threatened to punish China over its yawning trade surplus with the United States. Last year, the United States’ deficit in trade in goods with China reached $347 billion, and Mr. Trump has promised to close that gap, which he has also attributed to what he calls China’s rigged currency exchange policies. Many economists say that claim is an oversimplification. Mr. Trump also suggested he might abandon longstanding American policy on Taiwan, the   island that China treats as an illegitimate breakaway territory.
