Florida Senator Marco Rubio praised Donald Trump for empowering the Cuban people by reversing some of former President Barack Obama’s diplomatic concessions with the Castro brothers. ["More than anything else this change empowers the people of Cuba    …     America is prepared to outstretch its hand and work with the people of Cuba, but we will not empower their oppressors," Rubio said during an event in Florida to highlight Trump’s policy changes with Cuba.   Rubio joined Trump for an event in Florida to highlight Trump’s policy changes with Cuba, at a packed room at the Manuel Artime Theater in Miami.
