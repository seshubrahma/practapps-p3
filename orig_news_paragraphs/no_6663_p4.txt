Tomas Yarrington and Eugenio Hernandez, both former governors of Tamaulipas for the PRI are current fugitives of the U. S. Department of Justice on money laundering charges in connection with cartel bribes from the Los Zetas and the Gulf Cartel both politicians had been receiving   bodyguards while on the run.   Yarrington is also wanted on drug trafficking charges for allegedly taking part in drug trafficking conspiracies involving the Gulf Cartel, Los Zetas and the Beltran Leyva Cartel.
