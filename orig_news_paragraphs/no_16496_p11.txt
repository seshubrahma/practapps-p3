A Monmouth poll this month found that Trump only picked up 10% of   voters. Some Republicans, including pollster Whit Ayres, have said Trump’s outreach to   voters has more to do with bolstering his image among white voters who are uncomfortable with his campaign.   " After 15 months of denigrating every nonwhite minority in sight, it’s hard to believe that he can actually do significantly better among nonwhites," Ayres recently told National Public Radio.
