A new survey reveals that most voters are not knowledgeable on key election issues, as the results show voters gave an incorrect answer 52 percent of the time when questioned about key policies. [Just Facts questioned 700 people that said they vote in "most" elections about key election issues such as: healthcare, education, taxes, government spending, pollution, the national debt, global warming, Social Security, hunger, and energy.   Voters only gave the right answer 35 percent of the time, according to the results. "
