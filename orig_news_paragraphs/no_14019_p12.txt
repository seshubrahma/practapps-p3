When you look at the word inaugural    where does this word really come from, what does it really mean or stand for?     you see that its meaning is embedded deep in its etymology ’augur’ in ancient Rome, was the word for omen, for foreboding. It turns out that an inauguration was actually a situation where you would ask for a sign from the gods that you were doing something right.
