It is worthwhile reading if only because you’ll see that it was written specifically for the headline to be "out there" rather than making any legitimate criticisms. The sum total of it, if you don’t have time, amounts to: Walls make us feel safer than they actually make us 13th Century walls weren’t very good and people will try and find alternative routes anyway. Leaving aside the first two risible arguments, the latter is tantamount to saying, "Don’t lock your car door, because people will just smash the window". The point of border walls is not to make it impossible, but rather, as difficult as possible, for illegal border crossings.
