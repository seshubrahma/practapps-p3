He once erroneously wrote on Twitter that Shariah, or Islamic law, was in danger of taking over the country. Both Mr. Bush and President Obama believed that such assertions inflamed   sentiment, served as a recruiting tool for terrorists and antagonized countries in the Middle East that the United States needed as allies in the fight against violent extremism.
