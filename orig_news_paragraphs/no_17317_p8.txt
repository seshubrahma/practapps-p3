Antagonistic rhetoric toward China was a feature of Trump’s campaign rallies through both the Republican primary and general election.   He has accused China of manipulating the value of its currency in order to give its products price advantages in the American market.   Trump has also called for much steeper tariffs on products imported from China    and then criticized US companies, including Boeing, which have pointed out that that could set off a trade war with Beijing that could close its market to   products.   Cornerstone of diplomacy, Trump’s comments Sunday made clear he intends to force a new deal with China.
