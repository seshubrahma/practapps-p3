"La La Land," made by Lionsgate, also won Globes for song, score, screenplay and actor. The bliss felt by the "La La Land" cast and crew was likely mirrored by the disappointment of those involved with "Moonlight," the night’s    film. Despite six nominations, "Moonlight," about a young black man growing up in Miami, received a lone trophy   —   albeit an important one: best drama.
