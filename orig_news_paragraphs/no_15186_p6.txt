Before becoming secretary, Price represented Georgia’s 6th district in Congress and he happened to be visiting on the day his seat was up for grabs in what was seen as a litmus test on the Trump presidency. Tuesday’s unexpectedly close election unfolded in a district that has elected a Republican since 1979, including Newt Gingrich.   This week, it gave the most votes to a Democrat, newcomer John Ossoff, whose lawn signs urge people to "Vote your Ossoff. "
