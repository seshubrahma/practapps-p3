After Trump wins the election, he shakes up his transition team, demoting Christie to a supporting role and selecting Vice   Mike Pence to take Christie’s place as chair.     December 6, 2016    A Quinnipiac University Poll indicates that 19% of voters approve of Christie’s job performance as governor and 77% disapprove. That’s the lowest score for a governor in 20 years of Quinnipiac’s polls of 11 different states.
