The    Massive Ordnance Air Blast Bomb (MOAB) nicknamed the "mother of all bombs" for its extraordinary force, was dropped at 7:32 p. m. local time Thursday, according to four US military officials with direct knowledge of the mission.   The strike targeted a network of fortified underground tunnels that ISIS had been using to stage attacks on government forces in Nangarhar province, near the Pakistan border.
