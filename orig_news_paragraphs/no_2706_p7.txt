For now, Polish leaders have a   strategy to praise Mr. Trump’s victory as a continuation of their own victories, focus on areas of agreement with the new American president and play down, as much as possible, disagreements over things like how to deal with Moscow. Poland’s prime minister, Beata Szydlo, joined other   leaders in declaring Mr. Trump’s victory a validation of the rise of a more nationalistic and muscular form of government with a strong executive and resurgent patriotism. " A certain era in world politics ends, an era when politics focused on elites and dealing with issues of the elites," she said.
