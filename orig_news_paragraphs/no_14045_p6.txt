That said, Congress has not always been welcoming to infrastructure initiatives    particularly under President Obama. Obstructionists blocked his $50 billion "roads, rails and runways" proposal in 2010 and his American Jobs Act in 2011. They blocked his proposals for an infrastructure bank, a national   rail network and the GROW AMERICA Act (Generating Renewal, Opportunity, and Work with Accelerated Mobility, Efficiency and Rebuilding of Infrastructure and Communities throughout America).
