 (CNN) The Trump administration’s decision to repeal guidelines put in place by the Obama administration concerning transgender students’ bathroom access both misunderstands the gravity of the civil rights issues at stake for transgender youth (as well as transgender school staff and visitors) and underestimates how easy the   transgender bathroom problem would be to solve. Many people assume that   bathrooms are necessary, but they are not, even in schools. Our social constipation regarding bodily waste elimination leads many to dismiss or trivialize the political importance of public bathroom access.
