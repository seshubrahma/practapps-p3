U. S. Army Garrison Wiesbaden, Germany, sent out a memo on Feb. 21 that said it would temporarily cease operations of   programs at child development centers on March 1. The memos went viral, and multiple outlets reported that Trump’s order was causing the shutdown of some military childcare programs. " President Trump’s federal hiring freeze has forced two major Army bases to shutter some child care programs," reported The Hill on Feb. 22.
