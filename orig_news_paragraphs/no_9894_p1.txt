Trump was endorsed by 88 former Generals and Admirals in September and that number has now grown to more than 200. Retired Army Brigadier General Ernie Audino is a Senior Military Fellow at the London Center for Policy Research. He is the only U. S. Army General to have served a year as a combat advisor embedded in a Kurdish Peshmerga brigade. He explained what would have happened to him or someone in his command if they had been "extremely reckless" handling classified information.
