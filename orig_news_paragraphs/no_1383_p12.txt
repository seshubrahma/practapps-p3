This, of course, misreads the extent to which antifashion is fashion. As an aesthetic, antifashion as fashion is annoying and alienating, as many people who are over 40, not particularly slender or less prestigiously schooled can attest when visiting a Warby Parker outlet. There is democracy in a relatively low price, but a sense of exclusion is woven into the gestalt. Are you really smart enough to be shopping at Warby Parker?
