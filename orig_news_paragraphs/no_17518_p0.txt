 (CNN) Illegal immigration was not the debate that 2016 Republicans wanted to have right now as they begin an already tough battle to regain the White House. Perhaps no issue has bedeviled Republicans in recent election cycles more than illegal immigration. GOP candidates have been snared, over and over again, by the difficulty of appealing to conservative GOP primary voters who dominate the primary process while not alienating Latino voters, whose influence has grown in battleground states with each presidential cycle.
