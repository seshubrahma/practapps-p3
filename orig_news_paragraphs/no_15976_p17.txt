Trump’s inability  —   and apparent lack of interest   —   in toning down his rhetoric going into the general election season has deeply troubled the party. There is widespread concern that without a dramatic course change, Trump will continue to be significantly outmatched by presumptive Democratic nominee Hillary Clinton on many fronts, including organization, communications and fundraising. The news, which was first reported by The New York Times, comes amid deep internal tensions that have roiled the Trump campaign.
