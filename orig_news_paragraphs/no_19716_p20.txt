NASA has been on a very public trajectory to Mars for nearly seven years —  and Congress, in an unusual show of bipartisanship, wants to keep it that way. Earlier this month, the Senate unanimously passed legislation that amends current law by adding human exploration of Mars to one of NASA’s objectives.
