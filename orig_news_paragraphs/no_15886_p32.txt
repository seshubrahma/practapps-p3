When Kathy Griffin holds up violent images of the president and Eric Trump says Democrats are "not even people," we must acknowledge that the tone of our rhetoric has risen far outside the norm. Both parties are at fault, as are outside groups that spend millions of dollars villainizing and attacking political opponents. We can’t change the past —  but we can put our best foot forward today and act to tone down our political speech.   Some will say that the current tone of political dialogue had nothing to do with this shooting.
