" Johnson’s political strategy is simple. With just $35, 000 in his campaign coffers at the end of March, he doesn’t have the money for TV ads,   messaging, or a paid presence in battleground states where Trump and likely Democratic nominee Hillary Clinton are already beginning to invest resources. Johnson is instead relying on an intensifying schedule of media appearances to boost his name recognition in an effort to reach the necessary 15 percent threshold to qualify for the presidential debates this fall.
