Sarah Palin and billionaire Donald Trump come from completely different backgrounds and coasts. But Palin explained to Breitbart News    Alex Marlow why Trump is resonating with the same   Americans who viewed her as their champion when she unrelentingly challenged the established political order.   In an interview on Breitbart News Sunday, which airs on Sirius XM Patriot channel 125 from 7 PM to 10 PM EST, Palin said Trump’s successful business record and his celebrity enabled him to be the perfect messenger this election cycle who was able to stand against the interests of the bipartisan political class open borders. Palin told Marlow that Trump’s "candidness," ability to call "it like he sees it" and willingness to fight to "win for America" are the virtues that the "common man, everyday average     American want in a presidential candidate.
