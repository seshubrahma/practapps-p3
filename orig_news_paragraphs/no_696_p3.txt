The evening begins with a guided tour of two exhibitions: "The Window and the Breaking of the Window," with art from the Black Lives Matter era, and "Circa 1970," whose focus is on the sociopolitical and cultural issues of the ’70s. The shows further affirm that, in the age of the   news cycle and endless social media feeds, there’s no escape from news of horrific acts of hate, social injustices and inequalities.
