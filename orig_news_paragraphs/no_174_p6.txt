And Lithuania on Monday said it plans to use European Union funding to build a fence on the border with Russia’s highly militarised Kaliningrad exclave to increase security and prevent smuggling, according to Agence  . Construction of the   $32 million fence will start this spring and will be finished by the end of the year, said Interior Minister Eimutis Misiunas. " The reasons are both economic to prevent smuggling and geopolitical to strengthen the E. U.’s external border," Mr. Misiunas said.
