She has been closely studying the vernacular of first lady dress. In an architectural    white crepe column with a thin burgundy ribbon as a belt, a high slit and a gazar wave curving from sleeve to hip and then down the skirt, she looked   —   especially when compared with the younger generation of Trump women, most of whom opted for   sparkling princess gowns  —   understated and adult.
