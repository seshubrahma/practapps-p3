Astana, Kazakhstan (CNN) On a cold and clear Tuesday, you can feel the competing ambitions battling on Astana’s   Konaev Street.   It’s the final day of the Syrian peace negotiations, and the latest moves toward ending the bloody,   civil war    or at least consolidating a fragile ceasefire    are being announced.   On one side of the city’s main thoroughfare, the Rixos President Hotel bustles with shabby journalists, dapper diplomats and wealthy patrons in mink coats.
