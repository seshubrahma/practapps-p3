The entire debacle was set up shortly after the 2012 Republican National Convention, when the State Executive Committee of the Tennessee Republican Party changed the bylaws that established the process for the allocation of these 14 delegates it appointed today. Instead of calling for the appointments to be made with the "advice and consent" of the relevant campaigns, the bylaws were changed so that the appointments are now made merely with the "advice" of the campaigns. Neither the Trump nor the Cruz campaigns, apparently, consented to the delegate slate approved today, but, due to the rules change, their consent is not needed.
