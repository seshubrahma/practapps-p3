The debate was streamed on various online channels including YouTube and Facebook, and on Twitter in partnership with Bloomberg Politics. Monday’s debate broke the viewership record set in 1980, when Republican Ronald Reagan and Democrat Jimmy Carter squared off in front of an audience of 80 million people. On the broadcast networks, NBC led the pack with 18. 2 million total viewers, followed by ABC (11. 3 million) and then CBS (11 million). NBC’s Lester Holt moderated the debate, which was broadcast live from Hofstra University in New York.
