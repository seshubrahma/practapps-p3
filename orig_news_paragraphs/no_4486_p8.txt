Neil Gorsuch was sworn in as the 101st associate justice of the Supreme Court on April 10, 2017. He was, therefore, able to participate in the Court’s   April sitting for the final oral arguments of the term, quickly establishing himself as an active —  and humorous —  addition to the bench. From first to last, every detail of Trump’s successful appointment of Gorsuch to the Supreme Court was an unmitigated victory. Even Saturday Night Live could not deny that reality.
