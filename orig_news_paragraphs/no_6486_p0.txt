Two million Americans withdrew from Obamacare insurance plans since signing up on a federal exchange in January. [On Monday, the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services (CMS) published two reports detailing that nearly two million Americans had not paid their insurance premiums to maintain health insurance coverage.   The Effectuated Enrollment Report explains that 12. 2 million Americans selected an Obamacare plan at the end of the open enrollment period. However, only 10. 3 million decided to pay the required premiums to maintain health insurance coverage as of March 15th.
