 (CNN) Reports are swirling over   Donald Trump’s apparent decision to bring a private security detail with him to the White House.   Given America’s checkered experience with private military forces in Iraq and Afghanistan, Trump’s   rhetoric, and his apparent comfort with commingling state and commercial power, the last thing we need is a Blackwater on the Potomac.   Though the analogy to Blackwater is hardly perfect, we do have a recent and sordid history of private security contractors advancing sensitive government responsibilities.
