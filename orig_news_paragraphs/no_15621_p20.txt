" ’States need time to plan’ The delay in funding has been a problem for states. Most state legislatures have wrapped up for the year, and it’s hard to plan programs when states don’t know what the budgets will be.   Some states may be able to fund CHIP through 2018, regardless of federal funds, according to the National Governors Association, but all states will exhaust their federal allotments at some point in fiscal year 2018.
