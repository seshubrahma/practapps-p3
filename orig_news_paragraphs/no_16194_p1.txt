With senseless tragedy at our backs and broken spirits moving forward, many are asking "why?"   Not only that, a lot of Americans are asking the same question Iowa Sen. Joni Ernst asked last night, "Where is America’s leadership?"   The first day of the RNC answered that question and extended a simple   message: America’s leadership will be found in strength and unity. Mindful of the tragedies we’ve faced and the fellow countrymen we have lost, Rep. Michael McCaul of Texas succinctly laid out the problem we face, saying that "[O]ur city on a hill is now a city under siege."
