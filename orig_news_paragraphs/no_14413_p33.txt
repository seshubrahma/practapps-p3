June 2014   Icahn holds a 9. 4% stake in Family Dollar. July 2014   Icahn sells off shares in Family Dollar, owning a 3. 6% stake by the end of the month.     Summer 2014   Dollar Tree and Dollar General bid for ownership of Family Dollar.   August 2014   Icahn criticizes Family Dollar CEO Howard Levine and pushes for a Dollar General buyout.
