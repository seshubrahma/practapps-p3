It started with a glimpse of nudity on "NYPD Blue" back in the ’90s, and then everyone else making television had to up the ante    including   TV hosts. We went from Carson to the edgier Letterman, leaving behind the uptight Ed Sullivan, who made the Rolling Stones change "Let’s Spend the Night Together" to "Let’s Spend Some Time Together" when they performed on his show 50 years ago.
