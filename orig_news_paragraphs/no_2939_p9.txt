For his part, Mr. Putin is counting the days until Mr. Trump is in the Oval Office. Despite a failing economy, the Russian president has been pursuing for the past four years what most Western analysts see as a plan to reassert Russian power throughout the region. First came the annexation of Crimea and the shadow war in eastern Ukraine. Then came the deployment of   forces to the border of NATO countries, as Moscow, working to fracture the power structures in Germany and France and promote   parties, sent a reinvigorated military force on patrol of the coasts of the Baltics and Western European nations.
