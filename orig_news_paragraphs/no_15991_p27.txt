By Chris Isidore and Tami Luhby, CNNMoney, Trump said Clinton should be "scorned" because the nation’s trade deficit with China soared 40% while she was secretary of state. " Our trade deficit with China soared 40% during Hillary Clinton’s time as secretary of state    a disgraceful performance for which she should not be congratulated, but rather scorned," he said. Actually, the trade deficit rose only 12% if you look between 2008 and 2012, which is the most accurate way to measure what happened under her tenure, which ran from early 2009 until early 2013, according to federal trade data.
