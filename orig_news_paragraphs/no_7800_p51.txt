When eyebrows were raised about her overseas billions —  where the money was out of reach of the taxman, but still within reach of her —   Pritzker’s defense was, in effect, that everybody does it. And what was further said in her defense —  that it was her parents, not she, who had stashed the cash, and that Mitt Romney did it, too —  isn’t actually much of a defense.
