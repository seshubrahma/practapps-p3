Each of her three Goldman speeches were private, and Mrs. Clinton has rebuffed requests to release transcripts of them. And at the time, she still embraced Goldman as a partner. In 2014, she appeared at the annual meeting of the Clinton Global Initiative, an offshoot of the Clinton Foundation known as C. G. I. to highlight the 10, 000 Women program. " Thanks to Goldman Sachs and thanks to 10, 000 Women for really shining a bright spotlight on what is possible if you believe in and you provide support to women," Mrs. Clinton said.
