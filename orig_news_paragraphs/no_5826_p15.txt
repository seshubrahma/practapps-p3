Indeed, from the first moment that BOR, as he was known internally, called someone a "pinhead," the world was on notice that his show would be something different —  and something very popular. After all, O’Reilly was first and foremost a TV guy his roots were in local news and tabloid television —   also places that don’t reward complex locutions and polysyllabic words. And also, interestingly, for all his bluster, he never forgot, every night, to thank his audience for watching.
