Moscot has responded to the feverish competition by outstylizing everyone else, remodeling its Lower East Side store three years ago to look like a steampunk fantasy island. The vibe is so steeped in history and authenticity that it makes Warby Parker look like a T. G. I. Friday’s. Moscot has also expanded internationally, opening a branch in London in December. Cohen’s faces the challenge of trying to seem hip as a franchise business with 130 stores.
