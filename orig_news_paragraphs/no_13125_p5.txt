The primary contest first pitted Establishment candidates like Jeb Bush and Marco Rubio against conservatives and then split the conservative movement during a   war between Donald Trump and Ted Cruz. Some Republican consultants and activists are still pledging support for the #NeverTrump movement and vowing either not to vote or to support Hillary Clinton in the general election. But Trump has made up some ground by meeting with House Speaker Paul Ryan and Senate Majority Leader Mitch McConnell.
