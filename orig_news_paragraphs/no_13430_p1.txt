Through Thursday’s results, Trump led among men, 47%  to 36% while Clinton had a smaller, 41% % edge among women. Trump led among voters 45 and older, Clinton among those younger. Some of Trump’s strongest support comes from white voters who have not graduated from college, among whom he led 53% to 24%. Clinton, by contrast, dominates among minorities, leading 77% to 3% among blacks and 51% to 30% among Latinos.
