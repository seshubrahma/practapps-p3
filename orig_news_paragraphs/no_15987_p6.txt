How was the Leave campaign able to exploit a single issue so effectively, and leave the vote still too close to call?   Well, behind all the hysteria over immigration, many people in modern Britain have good reason to be angry.   After six years of   austerity, local services are overstretched    unable, so the Leavers claim, to cope with the growing influx of incomers from overseas.   Workers in traditional industries, who blame depressed wages and increasing rents on a glut of foreign labor, see the EU as just another agent in the machinery of corporatism and globalization.
