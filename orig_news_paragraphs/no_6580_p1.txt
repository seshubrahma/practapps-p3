Only 17 percent of respondents said they supported the Ryancare plan in a Quinnipiac poll of 1, 056 voters from March 16 to March 21. That poll showed lopsided 56 percent opposition, and 26 percent undecided. The Ryancare plan was supported by only 41 percent of declared Republicans. Independents split 14 percent positive, 58 percent negative.
