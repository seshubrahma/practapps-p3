During the second night of the 2016 Democratic National Convention, an audience of delegates and spectators signed along with him. Harkin taught the crowd how to sign "America" while drawing attention to a continued lack of employment for disabled Americans. Harkin praised the 1990 act’s successes, but noted its failings, saying, "When, 26 years later, 70% of adults with disabilities in America aren’t in the workforce, it’s time to take action. "
