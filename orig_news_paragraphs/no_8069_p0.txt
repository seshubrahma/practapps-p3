This article was written by Gideon Resnick and published by the Daily Beast: As Donald Trump descended his podium at a rally in Arkansas last week, a familiar scene of screaming fans met him with requests for autographs and selfies. Everything was gaudy in a typically Trumpian way but for the presence of one person in particular: Paula Jones. "Go ahead honey, go," Trump told Jones as she turns around for a selfie with the Republican frontrunner. When her husband introduced himself, Trump leaned over the railing and said "you got lucky man, look how beautiful," before moving along down the line.
