Instead of banquets and horses, Trump was suddenly flung into   meetings with leaders deeply skeptical of his foreign agenda. His first talks with newly elected French President Emmanuel Macron began with a lengthy handshake, each man’s face contorted in a forced smile as they gripped each other. Later, a French official revealed Trump asked for Macron’s cell phone number, and tried to insist he didn’t back Macron’s election rival Marine Le Pen, who he referred to only as "that woman. "
