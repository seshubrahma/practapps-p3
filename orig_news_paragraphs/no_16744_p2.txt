Clinton, Trump poised for historic debate battle, The new results in two battleground states underscore the closeness of the race and come as the candidates prepare to square off Monday night in their   first debate at Long Island’s Hofstra University. In both states, sharp divisions among whites by education are evident, with white college graduates choosing Clinton over Trump by 11 points in Pennsylvania and 16 points in Colorado, while whites who do not hold   degrees break in Trump’s favor by 19 points in Pennsylvania and 22 points in Colorado.
