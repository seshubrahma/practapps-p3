Debate prep, The rivals spent the day preparing for their big battle. Clinton participated in mock debates with her   former aide Philippe Reines playing Trump. Reines even wore the kind of signature red tie that Trump favors and adopted his characteristic hand gestures in a bid to fully prepare Clinton for her unpredictable foe.
