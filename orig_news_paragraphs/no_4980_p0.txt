A poll released Wednesday by the Public Policy Institute of California (PPIC) finds a solid majority of Californians support   vouchers for private and religious schools. [According to the poll, 60 percent of adults favor "providing parents with   vouchers to send their children to any public, private, or parochial school they choose. " Among registered voters, 55 percent favor vouchers, and, among likely voters, 50 percent favor vouchers.
