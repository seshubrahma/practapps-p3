" The Social Security Administration not only overstepped its mission with this regulation, it discriminated against certain Americans with disabilities who receive Social Security benefits. The agency should be focused on serving all of its beneficiaries, not picking and choosing whose Second Amendment rights to deny," said Rep. Kevin Brady,   who chairs the Ways and Means Committee. Democrats complained that repealing the rule, which was adopted in the waning days of the Obama administration, would lead to more gun violence.
