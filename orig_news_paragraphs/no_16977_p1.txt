The FBI director cleared Hillary Clinton for a second time Sunday in the probe over her private email server. But the damage to her campaign    and his own reputation    is unlikely to quickly heal. With his highly public handling of the email probe and his resulting entanglement in the political tumult, Comey accomplished the unusual feat of antagonizing each side of the partisan divide. In the aftermath, his stature is diminished and his future in a Clinton or Donald Trump administration is highly uncertain.
