The closest the show gets to big ideas about policing come when the     detective Theresa Murphy (Natalie Martinez) reminds her new boss that computers can’t replace the human element. Like "Training Day," "A. P. B. " was in the works well before Election Day (though not before Black Lives Matter and the reaction to it). The shows’ content is mainly driven by the eternal needs of broadcast drama: heroes (or, increasingly, antiheroes) villains and   stakes. You can’t make much of a crime procedural out of people not committing crimes.
