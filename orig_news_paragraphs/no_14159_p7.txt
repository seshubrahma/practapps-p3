On the other side of the border is Tijuana, a sprawling and vibrant city of 1. 3 million people.   Residents say in recent years the wait times to cross the ports of entry into the United States can take as long as 5 hours on some days. This section of the U. S.  border is one of the most heavily fortified sections where the border wall has been doubled up in some places. But here, Homeland Security agents are battling smugglers    not just above ground but below ground as well.
