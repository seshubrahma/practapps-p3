The State Legislature, after passionate debate, eventually agreed to remove the flag. " I think they should put it in the museum, let it go, respect whatever it is that you have to respect, because it was a point in time, and put it in a museum," Mr. Trump told reporters at the time. Historians say the battle flag has had shifting meanings over time: a symbol of white resistance to integration during the Civil Rights era, a more complicated but still racially charged symbol now.
