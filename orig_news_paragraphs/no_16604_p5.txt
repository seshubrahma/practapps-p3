The Geneva Conventions prohibit countries from seizing others’ property, even in war    the exact justification the United States used during its 1990 invasion of Iraq, after Iraq had seized Kuwait’s oil.   But in an interview with ABC’s on "This Week," George Stephanopoulos pressed the former New York City mayor over the legality of his claim, with Giuliani saying: "Of course it’s legal. It’s a war." "Until the war is over, anything’s legal," he said, laughing.
