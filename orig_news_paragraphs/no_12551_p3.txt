The economic deterioration of states like Pennsylvania is itself a predicate to Trump’s principal campaign message. If Trump is able to put Pennsylvania’s electoral votes in play, his message may well resonate in other   states in the upper Midwest. Not only would Ohio be safe for Republicans in November, but the party may be especially competitive in Michigan and Wisconsin this year. A competitive battleground in these states would force the Democrats to expand considerable resources shoring up states they have previously taken for granted.
