Andrew McClinton has been charged with one count of   arson of a place of worship in connection with the November 1 blaze at Hopewell Baptist Church, a predominantly black church in Greenville, the Greenville Police Department said. Authorities did not release a motive.   Firefighters quickly extinguished the blaze after a 911 call reporting the fire at the     church came in around 9:15 p. m. No one was injured.
