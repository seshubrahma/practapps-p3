The Democratic nominee retains an advantage, 41% to 38% with Libertarian candidate Gary Johnson and Green Party candidate Jill Stein included in the survey. Johnson pulls 11% support, while Stein garners 2%. Since 1964, Georgia has voted for a Democratic presidential candidate only three times    for favorite son Jimmy Carter in 1976 and 1980, and Bill Clinton in 1992.    According to the poll, Trump and Clinton are viewed unfavorably by an equal share of registered voters at 58% each.
