In 2000, he bade farewell to the White House press corps by showing a video in which he moped around the White House alone. In one scene, he ran onto the South Lawn with a brown bag lunch for his wife Hillary, who had already swept away in a limousine. In others, he watched his laundry roll around in a tumble dryer and raided a vending machine selling ice cream in the White House basement. The video contrasted with the popular image of Clinton fighting against his   status and played into questions of how this quintessential politician could ever live without the limelight of the White House.
