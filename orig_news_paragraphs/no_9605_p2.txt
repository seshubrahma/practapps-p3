According to the Recode report, Facebook investor and PayPal   Peter Thiel is intricately involved in helping organize the meeting of the minds. In the case of Google, Apple, Facebook and Microsoft, those companies poured millions into backing Clinton —  and barely anything into backing Trump. According to data compiled by the Center for Responsive Politics, employees from Alphabet Inc. —  which runs Google —  gave $1, 315, 545 to Hillary Clinton’s campaign while only donating $21, 924 to Trump. To the Hillary Victory Fund, Google employees gave $571, 327.
