" The night the wall came down, she crossed over, like so many others, and finally experienced what she calls the ’incredible gift of freedom,’ " Obama said at a State Dinner for Merkel in 2011. Two years later, Merkel poignantly pointed out the route of the wall during an Obama visit to Berlin, and told him that, trapped in the East, she used to listen to trains on the other side and dream of being free.
