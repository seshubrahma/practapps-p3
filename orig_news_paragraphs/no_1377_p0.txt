Big promises are to be expected from presidential candidates, but reality often intrudes. The elder George Bush broke the "no new taxes" pledge that helped lead to his election. And Barack Obama’s administration has yet to live up to his prediction that his nomination would go down in history as the moment "when the rise of the oceans began to slow and our planet began to heal. " Donald J. Trump’s vow to restore what he says is America’s lost luster, while perhaps not as flowery, comes with campaign promises that are equally grandiose.
