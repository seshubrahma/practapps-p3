Gallup started asking the question about trust in the media in 1972, but not regularly until 1997. Confidence in the media peaked in 1976 at 72 percent. Among Democrats and Independents there was not such a dramatic change: Democrats’ and independents ’ trust in the media has declined only marginally, with 51 percent of Democrats, compared with 55 percent last year, and 30 percent of independents, versus 33 percent last year, expressing trust.
