So other than running for the most critical   position in American politics, what does a vice president do for a presidential campaign?   More than any other personnel choice, a presidential candidate’s VP pick signals the kind of leaders with which she or he would surround themselves in the White House, as well as the substantive vision he or she would advance.   As the saying goes, we’re all judged by the company we keep.
