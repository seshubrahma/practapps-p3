She had been working for Disney just shy of ten years, when out of the blue she was called into a meeting and informed that she along with hundreds of her American colleagues were being let go and would be replaced with foreign workers, whom they would have to train. These foreign workers had been brought into the country on the controversial     visa. In the course of the interview, Moore discussed the toll this news took on her "extended family" —   her colleagues at Disney —  who could not recover from the stress of the blow.
