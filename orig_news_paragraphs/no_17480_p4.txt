How did Putin respond to the new sanctions? The Russian President’s decision to postpone any retaliation for Washington’s sanctions, despite Foreign Minister Sergey Lavrov recommending the    expulsion of US diplomats, surprised some  . In retrospect, Moscow’s tactics appeared orchestrated: first threatening to match the explosions announced by the Obama administration the previous day, then, in seeking to appear magnanimous    even extending an invitation to the children of American diplomats in Washington to a Christmas party at the Kremlin    backing off.   At the same time, Putin laid the onus of action on incoming President Donald Trump.
