But Ebell, the head of Trump’s Environmental Protection Agency transition team, likely had as much influence shaping Trump’s decision as any single individual. As director of the Competitive Enterprise Institute, a   policy center that was in favor of withdrawing from the Paris agreement, Ebell had direct and indirect influence that might have helped change the political and environmental direction of the US. Following news that Trump was waffling on whether or not to uphold his campaign promise of withdrawing the US from the pact, Ebell’s CEI put out a TV ad with video montages of Trump promising to do just that during the election.
