 (CNN) When Prime Minister Justin Trudeau of Canada goes to the White House on Monday, his greatest priority    and responsibility    will be forming an easy, effective rapport with Donald Trump.   If Trudeau can create chemistry with an unpredictable President whose agenda and ideas are different from his own, this flying visit will be a success. If they like each other, Trump will be more likely to accept the case for Canada as the most important friend of the United States, with a century and a half of shared history, geography and commerce.
