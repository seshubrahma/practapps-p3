The uninsured rate fell to 10. 9 percent at the end of last year, according to Gallup, compared with 17. 1 percent at the end of 2013. Analysis from the Commonwealth Fund concluded that the Affordable Care Act was responsible for a majority of the decline. This is misleading. Researchers at the Urban Institute estimated that roughly 2. 6 million people reported in 2013 that their plans were being canceled because of noncompliance with the A. C. A.
