Horror author Stephen King took to his Twitter account Saturday to pen a   story that mocked President Donald Trump’s claim that former President Obama wiretapped his office in Trump Tower before the 2016 presidential election. ["Not only did Obama tap Trump’s phones, he stole the strawberry ice cream out of the mess locker," King wrote in the first of three Tweets Saturday.
