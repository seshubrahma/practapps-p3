You can’t stick a landing to score a perfect "10 Out of 10" without first taking the junk.   That’s exactly what Ivan Briggs did ahead of his 90th birthday.    The World War II veteran who said age has never been a problem for him also said he had no fear about the jump, that it was pure enjoyment. Briggs’ wife of 65 years says he got lots of hugs when he was safely back on the ground.
