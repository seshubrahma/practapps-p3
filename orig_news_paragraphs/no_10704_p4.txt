As the New York Times reported earlier this month, "[o]n Sunday, Pastor Bradley Cranston stood at his pulpit in Burlington, Iowa, and, citing Exodus 18:21 ("select out of all the people able men who fear God   …     as leaders") endorsed Senator Ted Cruz of Texas, urging his parishioners to also consider voting for him. " While such outright mixing of religion and politics presumably may offend supporters of other candidates in attendance at Pastor Cranston’s church, it is an accepted practice among some pastors backing Cruz.
