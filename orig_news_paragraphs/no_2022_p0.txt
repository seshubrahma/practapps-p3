FAYETTEVILLE, N. C.  —      Donald J. Trump formally introduced Gen. James N. Mattis as his choice for defense secretary Tuesday night, appearing side by side with him during a   "thank you" rally in this heavily military community, home to the Army Special Operations Command and the 82nd Airborne Division. To a roar in the Crown Coliseum, Mr. Trump called General Mattis "one of the most effective generals that we’ve had in many, many decades" and the living embodiment of the Marine Corps motto, "semper fidelis.
