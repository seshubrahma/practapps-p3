[Just as conservative candidates for President cannot be selected if they support abortion, Obamacare or gun control, Ponnuru explains that the new litmus test of conservatism is immigration. " Conservatives are now starting to see a candidate’s position on immigration as an index of his conservatism in general," Ponnuru writes.   Ponnuru notes how the conservative creed’s adoption of immigration limits mirrors the Party’s transformation on the issue of abortion.
