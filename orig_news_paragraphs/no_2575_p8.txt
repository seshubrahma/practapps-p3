Allies of Mrs. Clinton said on Wednesday that they were confident Mr. Sanders would come around in time, and that the broad message she had outlined for the fall campaign would suffice to win over doubters on the left. Mayor Eric Garcetti of Los Angeles, a Democrat who campaigned for Mrs. Clinton in California, predicted that the urgency of fighting Mr. Trump would bring the party together and ease the hurt feelings from the nominating contest. " This is definitely a crisis moment," Mr. Garcetti said.
