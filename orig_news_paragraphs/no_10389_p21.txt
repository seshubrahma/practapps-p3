Only 42 percent of voters think Trump "cares about people like me," but only 46 percent believe the same of Clinton. A strong 65 percent of voters think Trump will "say anything to get elected," but 71 percent believe that about Clinton. Just 37 percent believe Trump has "strong moral values," but a similar 40 percent believe that about Clinton. A strong majority, 56 percent, believe Trump is running for President to benefit himself, but even more, 57 percent believe Hillary is simply running to benefit herself.
