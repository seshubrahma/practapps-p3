Class ideas are enshrined    women on horseback actually own those horses. All is respectable,   and, to some, beautiful. As the country drifted toward entering World War I by 1916, suffrage leaders offered themselves to that effort, and the whiteness took on the extra added patina of patriotism. These lovely images that purport to embody the suffrage movement, then, are sanitized pictures of a particular time and particular people.
