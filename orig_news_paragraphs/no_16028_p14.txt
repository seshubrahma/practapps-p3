Trump hardly projected presidential savvy    and he didn’t seem to care, unlike presidential nominees in previous campaigns who have been desperate to showcase   leadership skills on their own foreign trips. For instance, Trump led reporters traveling with him on a bizarre rolling news conference conducted on the tees of his picturesque links that weaves through towering coastal dunes north of Aberdeen. And he seemed keen to dance on the metaphorical grave of British Prime Minister David Cameron, who had just resigned after losing the referendum vote. The billionaire’s Scottish diversion was not the first time he had appeared to detract from his own message.
