Monday on his nationally syndicated radio show, conservative talker Rush Limbaugh commented on   Donald Trump’s departure from norms, including his use of Twitter to communicate to the American people.   According to Limbaugh, Trump’s use of social media is an example of him playing by a new set of rules, which has upset many of those in the media and that it suggests that Trump is on offense, unlike other Republicans in positions of power in the past.
