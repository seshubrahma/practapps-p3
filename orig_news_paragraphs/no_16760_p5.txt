In an interesting strategic move, she waited until the very end of the debate to throw her sharpest dart by raising his past descriptions of women "as pigs, slobs and dogs" and his criticism of weight gain by 1996 Miss Universe Alicia Machado.   Trump shook his head, demanding where she had gotten her information (it’s on tape) but then didn’t seem to have an effective response. Instead he wandered off into a digression about Rosie O’Donnell, the meanness of Clinton’s ads, and the notion that he had planned to mention Bill Clinton’s infidelities    but was holding back the instinct to say "something extremely rough to Hillary, to her family."
