Never mind that some of those listed, like Greta Garbo, were not actually at the party. The effect had been achieved. Before The Times published a "List of Those Who Were Invited to the Party at the Plaza Hotel," that distinction had been reserved mainly for White House dinners and the like. In many ways, the Black and White Ball served as a signpost pointing the way to Kardashia, that mythical land where accomplishment is largely optional and fame is an end unto itself.
