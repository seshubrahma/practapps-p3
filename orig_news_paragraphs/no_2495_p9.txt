While Mr. Obama had been planning the speech for months, Donald J. Trump’s election victory made the address all the more vital for him. The president sought to defend his legacy and provide a road map for a successor with no experience in national security  —   and warn Mr. Trump away from some of his more controversial positions and advisers.
