Then, early in the morning, my dog has taken to waking me up with a sermon of barks    something he never did before. I soon realize my       Harlem building has had its share of early morning visitors in uniform. My building happens to be mostly Muslim    brown, black, and white Muslim    and is located close to several prominent mosques, one of the reasons I’ve loved living here.
