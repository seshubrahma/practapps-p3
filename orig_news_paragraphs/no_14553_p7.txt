It is vital for the credibility of the President and a White House that things do indeed go smoothly this time around. This may be the best, last chance for the administration to establish whether it can write an executive order that can honor Trump’s goals but at the same time not fall foul of constitutional due process rights of travelers trying to get into the United States who might be covered by the ban. The White House faces a high bar in drafting the new order because its constitutional interpretation has already proven open to being challenged.
