Jack Kleinhenz, the chief economist at the National Retail Federation, does not discount the magnitude of the transformation that is occurring in retail, but cautioned that the monthly job figures are also highly subject to temporary vagaries. " One of the challenges we have at this time of the year is the quirkiness of seasonal forces," he said.
