Last week’s meeting commenced on an awkward note when Trump appeared to refuse to shake Merkel’s hand during an Oval Office  . The body language came to symbolize the vast gulf between the two leaders, which was exacerbated by memories of Trump’s campaign trail criticism of Merkel’s stance on refugees. In their meeting, Trump returned repeatedly to the defense spending issue, according to people familiar with the two leaders’ conversation. He did not, as a British newspaper reported over the weekend, present a physical invoice to Merkel for unpaid funds.
