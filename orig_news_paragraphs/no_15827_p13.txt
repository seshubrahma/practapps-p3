It is important to remember that Watergate took a long time to gestate. The scandal started with the   to the Democratic National Headquarters in June 1972 and didn’t end until President Nixon resigned in August 1974, facing impeachment. The famous "smoking gun" tape didn’t come until the very end of the process, after Congress and prosecutors had slowly built a strong case and discovered what had taken place in Nixon’s broken White House.
