—   Danny Freeman (@DannyEFreeman) March 18, 2016,  1:55: Paul Ryan met with   donors. House Speaker Paul Ryan met Thursday night at a pricey French restaurant here with some of the party’s biggest donors to assess a political landscape dominated by one vexing question: what to do about Donald Trump. The dinner was a highlight of a secretive   conclave, convened under heavy security by a donor group headed by New York   manager Paul Singer, that is being viewed as a pivotal moment for the   effort to block Trump from the Republican presidential nomination.
