And as a sign of the     flood to come, on March 8, nearly three dozen green groups filed a formal legal petition with EPA, demanding that the agency tighten the regulations on concentrated   operations (CAFO) also known as "factory farms. "   As the lead plaintiff, Wenonah Hauter, executive director of Food   Water Watch, declared,  This petition paves the way for EPA to finally regulate CAFOs as required under the Clean Water Act, and explains that allowing CAFO pollution to continue unabated by maintaining the woefully inadequate status quo would violate federal law.    That petition, we might note, is 58 pages of closely argued legalese, boasting 294 footnotes.
