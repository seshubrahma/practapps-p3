For mothers, it has long been a troubling norm of employment: a baby means an unfair bump off the management track. You may be aware that, on average, women earn 79 cents to a man’s dollar.   But mothers earn only 73 cents. And according to the National Women’s Law Center (with numbers drawn from the U. S. Census Bureau): "Compared to white,   men, mothers of color fare even worse:   mothers make 53 cents and Latina mothers 47 cents compared to white,   fathers."
