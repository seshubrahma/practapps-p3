Vandalism at Adam Yauch Park, Swastikas and the word "Go Trump" were painted on playground equipment at Adam Yauch Park in Brooklyn, according to New York police spokeswoman Annette Shelton. The park is named after the late Adam "MCA" Yauch, a founding member of the pioneering rap band Beastie Boys.    The vandalism was discovered Friday afternoon, according to Shelton. A resident reported it to police, who are investigating the incident.
