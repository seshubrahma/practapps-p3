Ill. Twinkies started rolling off the line nearly a century ago. And when Apollo and Metropoulos bought some of Hostess’s cake plants and brands out of bankruptcy in 2013, Schiller Park’s plant was one of the fortunate few to reopen. " Schiller Park, We ♥ You," read a billboard that Hostess sponsored in the town, the heart carved into the image of a   Twinkie.
