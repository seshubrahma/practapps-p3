Trump runs hot and cold with the polls. In that way, he is not unlike other politicians, who lift up the numbers they like and downplay the figures they don’t. But the new White House has broken from the usual spin cycle by promoting    sometimes repeatedly    a series of jarring, and easily disproved falsehoods. In the reality described by Trump, a federal judge who ruled to halt the administration’s travel ban would, along with the entire "court system," be responsible for a potential terrorist attack.
