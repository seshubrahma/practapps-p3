Christine Koh, founder and editor of Boston Mamas, wrote about "the way forward" for her, her family and the country in a blog post. Finding a path ahead will involve hard conversations about topics such as fear of diversity, racism and gender inequality, and then committing to work together to make things better, said Koh, who has two daughters, ages 5 and 12.
