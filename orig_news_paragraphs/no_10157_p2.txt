The senator said he has never met Tillerson, and he is aware that members of the   national security establishment such as former secretary of state Condoleeza Rice and former Pentagon chief Robert M. Gates have praised the oil man. Coons allowed he would take those endorsements under consideration. Rice’s endorsement has special meaning inside the national security community, because although she came to prominence during the Global War on Terror, her education and training was as an expert on Russia —  mentored by former secretary of state Madeleine Albright‘s father Josef Korbel.   The connection between Tillerson and Russia is crucial, Coon said.
