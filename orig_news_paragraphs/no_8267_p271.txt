With 88 of 2, 559 precincts, or 3. 4 percent, reporting as of 7:10 p. m. eastern time, the first returns posted at the Virginia Department of Election’s website show Republican nominee Donald Trump in the lead with 51 percent of the vote to Hillary Clinton’s 42 percent. Trump has 10, 354 votes, and Clinton has 8, 526 votes. Polls in Virginia closed at 7:00 pm eastern time.
