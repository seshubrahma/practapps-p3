The strike group has been at sea since January and conducted military exercises with other nations in the western Pacific, including Japan and the Republic of Korea. The carrier group is now conducting some training with Australian forces and may also do additional training with Japanese forces as it heads further north, officials said. The Navy usually does not discuss ship locations in detail before they arrive.
