In 2008, Donald Trump sold a Palm Beach, Fla. estate for $95 million, making it the most expensive single residential property ever sold in town. Now the property is about to set another record   —   as Palm Beach’s most expensive  . Backhoes and dump trucks have started crushing and carting away the    French provincial home at 515 North County Road, along with an    tennis house, a pool house and a carriage house.
