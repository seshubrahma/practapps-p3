Impressively, the show’s éminence grises are showing some of the best work of their careers. Larry Bell contributes "Pacific Red II," a majestic line of six large   volumes made from progressively lighter or darker sheets of red laminated glass that combines East Coast and West Coast Minimalism as never before. The weird symbolism in Jo Baer’s paintings fuse the grays of her early Minimalist abstractions with visions of Irish myths and monuments.
