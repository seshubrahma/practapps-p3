_ 5. The Philippines is struggling through its own devastating war on drugs. Its Senate heard explosive testimony from a   hit man who said that the country’s new president, Rodrigo Duterte, had presided over the extrajudicial killings of about 1, 000 criminals and political opponents over the years he served as mayor of Davao City. The Senate’s televised hearings have already heard that nearly 1, 800 people were killed by police officers and vigilantes in the war on drugs in Mr. Duterte’s first seven weeks as president.
