In the words of the Bible, John 3:17, we are told, "Whoever has the world’s goods, and sees his brother in need and closes his heart against him, how does the love of God abide in him? " Whatever the exact state of his scriptural knowledge, Trump seems to hold that biblical injunction close to his heart hence his pledge to guarantee healthcare coverage to all.   As he said in the South Carolina Republican debate on February 6, if we fail to be good shepherds, "There will be a certain number of people who will be on the street dying, and as a Republican, I don’t want that to happen.
