By the end of the day, any residual angst from a challenging week seemed to melt away. Surrounded by men hoisting swords in a traditional dance ahead of a palace dinner, Trump even began to sway himself. He was joined, enthusiastically, by his    commerce secretary, Wilbur Ross. Even after 10 p. m. Trump was still being ferried through a royal museum in a   golf cart, the King at his side and his wife, first lady Melania Trump, perched on the back.
