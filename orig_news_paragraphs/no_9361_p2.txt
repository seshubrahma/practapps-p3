However, it has since battled back to strength, this year launching Generation Conservative to encourage   students stifled by censorship on university campuses The think tank led a successful rebellion against the Cameron Government’s ‘ Educational Excellence Everywhere’ plans to coerce all state schools into academies, for which it received national media coverage.
