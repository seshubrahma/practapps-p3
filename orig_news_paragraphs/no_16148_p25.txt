Ginsburg has a busy summer of travel ahead of her. In the coming days she will travel to Europe and on July 27 she is expected to take part in a program with performers of a production of the Merchant of Venice which will take place in Venice, Italy.    Later in August she will provide commentary for an Opera festival in Cooperstown, New York. Chief Justice John Roberts has been critical in public about the rancor between the political branches, and in a speech in 2014 at the University of Nebraska expressed concern that the discord impeded their ability to carry out their functions.
