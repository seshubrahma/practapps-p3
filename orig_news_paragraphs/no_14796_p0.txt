Louisville, Kentucky (CNN) Vice President Mike Pence, armed with the Republican bill to overhaul the health care system, put subtle but direct pressure Saturday on Republicans thinking about bucking President Donald Trump’s administration and their leadership on Capitol Hill over the legislation. Pence cast the current debate over health care as the best chance Republicans have to repeal and replace Obamacare , President Barack Obama’s sweeping 2010 health care law, and said the administration needs all Republicans to be with them in this effort.
