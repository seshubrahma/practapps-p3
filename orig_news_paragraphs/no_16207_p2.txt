Pennsylvania (20 electoral votes) moves from lean Democrat to "battleground "         Utah (6 electoral votes) moves from "solid Republican" to "lean Republican " In total, that means 19 electoral votes have moved in Hillary Clinton’s direction from "battleground" to "lean Democrat" and 20 have moved toward Donald Trump with Pennsylvania’s new "battleground" status, according to CNN’s ratings.
