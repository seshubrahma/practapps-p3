And so the most important part of the pick is to avoid selecting someone who causes problems for the campaign. Trump can’t afford another Sarah Palin, whose disastrous media rollout only weighted down John McCain’s already sagging campaign.   Even if it doesn’t move the needle much, the vice presidential selection can help compensate for some of the problems and concerns that voters have about a nominee. In Trump’s case this is especially important, given how unprecedented and unpredictable his candidacy is.
