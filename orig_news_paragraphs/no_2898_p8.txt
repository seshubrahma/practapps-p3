" The hearing provided further evidence of Republicans’ eagerness to keep the email controversy alive as Mrs. Clinton’s general election campaign begins in earnest against Donald J. Trump. Just a day earlier, Mr. Goodlatte and Representative Jason Chaffetz, the Utah Republican who leads the House Committee on Oversight and Government Reform, had asked the Justice Department to mount a new investigation to determine whether Mrs. Clinton perjured herself last fall when she testified before Congress about her use of a private email system. They cited apparent contradictions between what Mrs. Clinton told Congress about the email server and what the F. B. I. found.
