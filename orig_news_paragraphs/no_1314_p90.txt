His campaign was a textbook case of what we now call "cognitive priming. " That is, the deep cerebral desire we all have to see what we expect to see. So when you buy a   kit, you get the tiny plastic aquarium and fitted into the sides are little magnifying lenses that enhance the tiny brine shrimp just enough, setting off a little motion picture in your head of   primates swinging to and fro by their curved tails, gamboling in a watery jungle.
