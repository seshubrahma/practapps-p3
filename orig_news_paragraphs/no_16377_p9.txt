One prominent Republican who is leaning toward Clinton is Jeb Bush’s top adviser, Sally Bradshaw. She told CNN last week she was becoming an independent, and said if the presidential race in Florida is close, she’ll vote for Hillary Clinton. And Meg Whitman, who ran for California governor, said last week that she would donate to and vote for Clinton, joining an upstate New York Republican representative in supporting the rival candidate.
