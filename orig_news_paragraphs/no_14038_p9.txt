The Senate Foreign Relations Committee is scheduled to meet Monday to vote on Rex Tillerson’s nomination for secretary of state. The Judiciary Committee will meet to consider attorney general nominee Sessions on Tuesday, but a vote is expected to be delayed for a week.   Also on Tuesday Trump’s choice to head the Health and Human Services Department, Rep. Tom Price, will have a hearing before the Senate Finance Committee. It’s his second committee hearing but the Finance panel will be the one to vote on his nomination.
