The official said that one appeal of targeting individuals with removal orders is that they can be deported "quickly," because no further court action would be necessary. Operation leads to nationwide arrests, ICE in Los Angeles said it had conducted a   operation targeting criminals and fugitives, and said that the vast majority of those arrested had criminal histories.   Seeking to push back on reports of indiscriminate raids, ICE released the results of the operation from its Los Angeles office, saying about 160 foreign nationals were arrested during the week. Of those, 150 had criminal histories, and of the remaining arrests, five had final orders of removal or were previously deported.
