In this captivating book, the reporter David Sax provides an entertaining account of how analog technologies are enjoying a spirited revival: vinyl record sales are booming,   cameras have caught on among millennials and their younger siblings, and   paper notebooks and erasable whiteboards have become a   option in many Silicon Valley offices.
