Musician John Legend attempted to put Donald Trump’s popularity in historical perspective in an interview Tuesday, appearing to compare those who support the presumptive GOP presidential nominee to people in the 19th century who allowed slavery to continue without realizing it was an evil practice. ["We’re in a moment now where we have a major candidate for office that is being very racist and divisive," Legend told Variety at the publication’s TV Summit in Beverly Hills on Tuesday.
