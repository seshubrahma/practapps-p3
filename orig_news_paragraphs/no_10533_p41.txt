Now 30 years after Ronald Reagan, who agreed to an amnesty for illegal aliens in 1986 only if it came with border security, the U. S. border with Mexico remains entirely insecure. Reagan’s border security pledge would have needed his vice president, George H. W. Bush, to follow through on it as the nation’s next president. He failed. A national security and economic risk, both President Bushes, President Clinton, and Obama and his Secretary of State Clinton left the border wide open.
