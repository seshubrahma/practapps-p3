April 13   Kobe Bryant plays his last NBA game, scoring 60 points to help the Los Angeles Lakers defeat the Utah Jazz  .   April 20    Five former New Orleans police officers plead guilty and are sentenced in connection with the shootings of six unarmed people, two fatally, on the Danziger Bridge days after Hurricane Katrina. Their sentences    from three to 12 years in prison    are remarkably less severe than the six to 65 years in prison to which they were originally sentenced back in 2012.
