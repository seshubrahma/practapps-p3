My grandmother, a Lebanese war orphan who was saved by the American Red Cross after World War I, would have   echoed Melania Trump’s sentiments. Both my immigrant grandparents adored this country and sent their sons to fight for it in World War II and Korea. In fact, because my grandmother had no birth records due to the Great War, the immigration officials at Ellis Island made July 4th her birthday   —   which has been a further source of patriotic pride for my family because our American story was "born on the Fourth of July.
