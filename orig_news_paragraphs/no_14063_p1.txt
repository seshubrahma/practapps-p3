It is in Pakistan where the majority of CIA drone strikes have occurred under Obama, resulting in the death of a minimum of 1, 904 people and perhaps as many as 3, 114, according to New America’s research. The drone program has continued in other countries outside of traditional war zones, such as Yemen and Somalia, but at relatively small levels, compared to the 122 strikes in one year that occurred in Pakistan at the height of Obama’s drone war there. On Friday, Donald Trump took the oath of office. He has a key decision to make as commander in chief: Will he   the drone strikes?
