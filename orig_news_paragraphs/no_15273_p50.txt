Michael D’Antonio: Willing to be presidential, but sometimes beneath his office, We saw the best of the Donald Trump presidency when he met with China’s President Xi Jinping and then announced that suddenly China was not a currency manipulator. This announcement, amid worsening tensions with North Korea, showed Trump was willing to be presidential and pursue the interest of the nation and the world by abandoning a campaign slogan. Likewise, Trump’s recognition that NATO is not obsolete demonstrated he could play well with others.
