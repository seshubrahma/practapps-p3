Fight over executive power, The legal drama over the immigration executive order was the first episode in what could be a series of legal challenges to Trump’s governing style and agenda and represents the first confrontation between his White House and the checks and balances of the American political system. The judges addressed each of the Justice Department’s proffered reasons for reinstating the ban in great detail and rejected them all. Perhaps the most sweeping language came concerning the government’s vast claim of executive power.
