Previous attempts to punish Milo have generated huge backlashes from conservative users, and this one  —    Twitter’s most severe clampdown on the Breitbart senior editor so far   —   may generate the biggest yet.   Supporters of Milo, who had more than 338, 000 followers on Twitter prior to his suspension, have created the #FreeMilo and #FreeNero hashtags on the platform in protest at Twitter’s latest attack on the conservative commentator.
