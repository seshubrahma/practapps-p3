All this is part of the historic transformation going on in Latin America. And while that transformation didn’t start this week, it has become increasingly clear how the people of the region (with the notable exception of Cuba) have been taking charge of their respective countries in new ways. Latin America’s revolutionary movements, a feature of the Cold War in the continent’s jungles and universities, are receding.
