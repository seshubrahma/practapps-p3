On the morning of April 11, 2017, the Attorney General toured the southern border with officers of U. S Customs and Border Protection. Addressing them and the nation, he proclaimed, "For those that continue to seek improper and illegal entry into this country, be forewarned: This is a new era. This is the Trump era. " "The catch and release practices of old are over," Sessions continued, announced that 125 additional immigration judges would be hired on expedited basis.
