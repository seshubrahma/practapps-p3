Mr. Cruz has won over many evangelical voters in Iowa through both pluck and luck. Although he has spent a year aggressively courting them, Mr. Cruz has benefited recently from the decline of Ben Carson, who briefly led Iowa polls thanks to evangelical support, then saw those voters swing to Mr. Cruz after questions arose about Mr. Carson’s grasp of foreign policy. Mr. Cruz first stirred Iowa crowds in March at a Pastors and Pews event, and later at a Christian   convention and a Faith  Freedom forum.
