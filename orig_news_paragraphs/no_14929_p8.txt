Perhaps the coup de grace came with Vice President Mike Pence’s tweet of a White House meeting with members of Congress, discussing cuts in women ’s health and maternity coverage to reduce the overall cost of the AHCA. Twitter exploded with protest over the optics of a room full of white men    and not a single woman to be seen    deciding the fate of the nation’s female population. On social media many compared the   discussion of women’s health coverage in the White House to Saudi Arabia’s creation of a Girl’s Council composed of men.
