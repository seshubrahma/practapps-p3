To be sure, most of Trump’s 2016 campaign healthcare plan is in keeping with orthodox Republicanism.   Most obviously, he urges the "full repeal" of Obamacare. Yet at the same time, Trump, marching to his own drummer, has also said, "We’re not going to let people die in squalor. "   So, whereas some Republicans would be happy repealing Obamacare and leaving it at that, Trump is determined to replace the Democrats’ national health plan with a comprehensive plan of his own.
