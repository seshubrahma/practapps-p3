After trying in 1853 to determine the origins of Valentine’s Day, The Times called it "one of those mysterious historical or antiquarian problems which are doomed never to be solved. " Here is a guide to some of the competing theories, including that it emerged from a Roman fertility rite, or possibly to honor the martyrdom of St. Valentine. Now, if you’ve received flowers, some tips on how to keep them fresh longer: Clip the ends under warm running water and keep them out of direct light.
