In the   race adding Green Party candidate Jill Stein and Libertarian candidate Gary Johnson, Clinton’s   lead over Trump is still inside the margin of error. The   race found Clinton at 42 percent, Trump at 38 percent, Johnson at six percent, and Stein at four percent. It comes as a Monmouth poll of 401 likely voters in North Carolina also has Trump at 42 percent, easily within the margin of error, just two points behind Clinton’s 44 percent. Johnson gets seven percent in the poll, while one percent name another candidate and six percent are undecided.
