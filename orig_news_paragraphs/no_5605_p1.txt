[Like the swollen tongue of a bloated battlefield corpse  —   pressing at the cuffs, seams and Sam Brownes  —    the engorged spadix of the titan arum grows until bursting into a stench that is described as a cross between rotting meat and   garbage. The odor is so repellent that it tricks flies into thinking the plant is a decaying corpse, thus its name.   This being Washington and the seat of your federal government, such a carnival of putrefaction inexplicably attracts people from all around by the thousands to take part in the jolly rotten occasion.
