She also admitted that she did not properly report on the crowd size —  and blamed an Associated Press editor for the mistake. Furthermore, I was doing the estimate after the actual event and not during, so I was doing it from memory, because I didn’t think my editor would be interested in a crowd count since what he originally wanted was for me to just talk to Ms. Conway and get an interview.
