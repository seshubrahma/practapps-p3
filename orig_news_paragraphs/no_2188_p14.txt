His support among   suburban voters in battleground states is especially low, according to polling conducted by Republican groups. Aides are discussing ways to keep Mr. Trump focused during the next few weeks. Among the options is deploying his three oldest children, who have urged him in the past to act "more presidential," as well as his    Jared Kushner, to travel with him in a rotation. At the same time, the Trump operation is relying on the Republican National Committee, as well as on several "super PACs" that support him, to handle tasks typically overseen by a presidential campaign.
