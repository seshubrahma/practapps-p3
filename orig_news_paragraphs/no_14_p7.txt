The teams play mostly in conventional gyms set up with temporary barriers. Orthodox youngsters are often introduced to the game in summer camp or by friends from their synagogue, and end up playing in one of the many youth leagues that have cropped up in Orthodox neighborhoods, before joining teams in middle school. " In some New Jersey yeshivas, floor hockey is more popular than basketball," said Yoni Stone, the varsity coach at the Yeshiva University High School for Boys.
