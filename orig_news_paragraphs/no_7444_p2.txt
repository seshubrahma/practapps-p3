The apprehension of Family Units increased by 95 percent over FY 2015 numbers to 77, 674 according to a report from U. S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP). The number of Unaccompanied Alien Children (UAC) crossing the border hit a near record total of 59, 692. More disturbingly, the numbers were dramatically increasing in the three months leading up to the election. The numbers of Family Unit apprehensions showed a constant increase from March through the fiscal year end of September.
