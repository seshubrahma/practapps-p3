Maxine Waters, Donald Trump’s most outspoken congressional opponent, presented the Best Fight Against the System award with Tracee Ellis Ross. Acceptance speeches stressed unity and diverse representation in movies and TV.    But the most significant development at this year’s awards was the debut of genderless acting awards, replacing the conventional "best actor" or "best actress" categories. Emma Watson and Millie Bobby Brown took home the two major acting awards, best actor in a movie and best actor in a TV show, respectively, for "Beauty and the Beast" and "Stranger Things.
