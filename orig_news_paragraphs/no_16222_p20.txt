Beyond the racial resentment, xenophobia and Islamaphobia that is very plainly coursing through the veins of Trump’s candidacy and his supporters’ fervor, this election is also marked by a genuine craving for radical change, new ideas and new leadership   —    a craving that cuts across partisan divides.    Hillary Clinton sells herself as a steady hand amidst this tumult, and that’s right, but she also needs to be riding the waves of change to some degree as well.
