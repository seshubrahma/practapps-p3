In an article on Buzzfeed from just two weeks ago titled, "Black Lives Matter Leaders Are Reaching Out To Silicon Valley, And It’s Paying Off" it says Deray Mckesson is: Something of a regular at the New York offices of Medium, has strong relationships with executives at Slack and Twitter, and has a fledgling relationship with top heads at Facebook. Mckesson’s friendliness with Twitter sure paid off. At his behest, Twitter banned conservative journalist Chuck Johnson for saying he wanted to "take out" the Black Lives Matter activist.
