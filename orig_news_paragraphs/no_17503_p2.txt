Maybe he just wanted to spend some time in the town where lawman Wyatt Earp grew up. Or maybe Jeb Bush wanted to serve notice he might make a stronger run for Iowa than many GOP strategists anticipate.              Pella, in Iowa’s conservative Marion County, was one of Bush’s stops as he took his campaign kickoff tour to the key early states.              Evangelicals tend to dominate the Iowa Republican caucuses, and there are a handful of GOP candidates competing hard for that niche in the GOP field.
