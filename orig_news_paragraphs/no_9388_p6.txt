Clinton logged a significant 25. 6 percent drop in California primary votes compared to her 2008 performance. In her 2008 battle against   Sen. Barack Obama, she received 2, 608, 184 California Democratic primary votes compared to a 2016 total of 1, 940, 580 against Sen. Sanders with 100 percent of precincts reporting. Sanders supporters told Breitbart News that they weren’t giving up on their candidate during a Tuesday election night party. The crowd yelled, "The media is corrupt!" and criticized the Monday evening   primary election day Associated Press report that claimed Clinton had secured enough delegates to win their party’s nomination.
