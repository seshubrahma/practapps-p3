Cecil Burrows, 23, a known gang member originally from the slums of Mumbai, has been sentenced to 18 months in prison for his role in the 2012 gang rape of a   teenage girl. Burrows decided to record the gang rape on video. Burrows came to the U. S. when he was 12 years old and promptly joined a gang. Even still, the government rewarded him with lawful permanent resident status.
