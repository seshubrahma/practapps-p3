In the age of Bernie Sanders, today’s young generation of   socialists would do well to read Friedrich Hayek’s 1944 classic broadside against socialism, The Road to Serfdom. A member of the Austrian School of economics and a winner of the Nobel Prize in economics, Hayek warned of the totalitarian realities of socialism’s conceits. As he put it elsewhere: "A claim for equality of material position can be met only by a government with totalitarian powers. " 3.
