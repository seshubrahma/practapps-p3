 (CNN) President Donald Trump has "absolutely" considered proposals that would break up the 9th Circuit Court of Appeals    the federal appeals court that ruled against his travel ban    he said Wednesday. Congress is currently considering legislation that would break up the appellate court currently that covers nine Western states plus Guam. In an interview with the Washington Examiner, Trump accused opponents of his agenda of "judge shopping" by sending cases through the 9th Circuit on appeal.
