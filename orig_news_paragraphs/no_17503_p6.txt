Bush will be busy in the week ahead, but not so much in the early caucus and primary states.              Ed O’Keefe of the Post tells us raising money is the Bush priority this week, which means some work back home in Florida but also stops in other places where the Bush campaign believes there is strong financial backing.              " The goal is to get $5 million by the end of the month to show a strong start for his campaign," said O’Keefe.
