Washington (CNN) Public opinion polls suggest a close race, but Hillary Clinton is crushing Donald Trump with a   lead in CNN’s Political Prediction Market during the week of the Republican National Convention in Cleveland. Clinton’s odds for the White House are at 67% while Trump’s odds are at 33%. Trump has climbed four points in the market since the first day of the RNC Clinton was at 71% while Trump was at 27% at the beginning of the week.
