A key factor in the race will also be how motivated the Democratic base is, which will also be a deciding factor in the 2018 midterms. Democrats are hopeful that Trump will motivate their base to turn out in the same way President Barack Obama motivated the Republican base to turn out in 2010.    But while some were looking for a big Democratic turnout to serve up a big upset in Montana, others were lowering expectations.   Matt McKenna, a Democratic strategist working with Quist, told Politico that while enthusiasm appears to be high, "the same people who tell you they know what turnout is going to be are the people who try to get you to buy a time share in Panama City, Florida.
