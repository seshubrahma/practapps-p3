There is also disaster aid package that includes funding for California, West Virginia, Louisiana, North Carolina. Talks had also stalled over a threat by Trump to cut off Obamacare subsidies paid to insurance companies to reduce the    expenses of some   users of the Affordable Care Act, but Trump backed off that demand in the face of harsh criticism from Democrats. The subsidies will stay in place as Republicans continue their   effort to repeal Obamacare, something Trump and his aides hope to revive as early as this week.
