Washington (CNN) President Donald Trump ’s seismic move to ban more than 218 million people from the United States and to deny entry to all refugees reverberated worldwide Saturday, as chaos and confusion rippled through US airports, American law enforcement agencies and foreign countries trying to grasp Washington’s new policy. Trump’s executive order bars citizens of seven   countries from entering the United States for the next 90 days and suspends the admission of all refugees for 120 days.
