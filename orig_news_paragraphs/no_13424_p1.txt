About 5 percent of voters were undecided.    Interestingly, when pollsters removed Johnson from the equation, the results changed to Clinton 23 percent, Trump 19 percent, and a whopping 31 percent undecided. However, fully 66 percent of respondents said they expected Clinton to win the election, with only 22 percent anticipating a Trump victory. The poll was conducted after the second presidential debate, which 70 percent of respondents said they had watched.
