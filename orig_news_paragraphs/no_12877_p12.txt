Until recently, the newspaper was known as The Manchester Guardian, as in Manchester, England.   And Manchester is not only a big city, population 2. 5 million, it is also a city with a fabled past: You see, Manchester was the cradle of the Industrial Revolution, which transformed England and the world. It was that city that helped create the free trade orthodoxy that is now crumbling. Yes, in the 18th and 19th centuries, Manchester was the leading manufacturing city in the world, especially for textiles.
