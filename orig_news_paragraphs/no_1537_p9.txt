Given that the spousal role often comes down to what the person makes of it, Mr. Clinton has some freedom to rethink the job, and Mrs. Clinton has signaled that she would want him to play a policy role. But the Clintons are also sensitive to not giving short shrift to the influence that first ladies have had, and advisers to the couple say that the three Clintons, as a family, will discuss ways to carry out that role. Betty Ford arguably had more   impact on American life, by easing stigmas on breast cancer and alcoholism, than her   husband did. Jackie Kennedy and Nancy Reagan defined style for many women of their generations.
