There were more pleas for help for some two million people trapped in the besieged Syrian city of Aleppo, above. Russian officials offered hope for greater aid access, saying they were near an agreement with the U. S. to combine forces against the Islamic State. U. S. officials made no comment. In Yemen, where a Saudi bombing campaign against Houthi militias has resumed, Doctors Without Borders said an airstrike on one of its hospitals had left at least 15 people dead.
