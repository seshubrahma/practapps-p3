Washington (CNN) A federal judge on Friday issued a tentative ruling to deny a bid to dismiss a lawsuit by unhappy customers of Trump University.    U. S. District Judge Gonzalo Curiel made the ruling from the bench in San Diego, California, with more motions to be heard in the coming weeks.    The case    one of two   lawsuits by former students of a real estate training program founded by Republican presidential nominee Donald Trump in California    is set for trial in November.
