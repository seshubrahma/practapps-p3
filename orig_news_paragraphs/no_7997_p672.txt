In yet another aggressive move that will thrill Trump supporters, late Tuesday morning, Jesse Rodriguez, Senior Producer of MSNBC ’s "Morning Joe,"  announced a town hall event with Republican frontrunner Donald Trump that will take place Wednesday night at 8 p. m. The night will be hosted by Joe Scarborough and Mika Brzezinski. The MSNBC town hall will compete directly with CNN’s   town hall with Trump’s two closest  competitors, Ted Cruz and Marco Rubio.
