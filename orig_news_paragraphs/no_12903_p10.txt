Christie frequently spent time in debates breaking up the fights between the freshman senators squabbling over their records of political accomplishment, and focusing on his own record as a federal prosecutor and governor. When attacked by Rubio’s Super PAC in New Hampshire, Christie retaliated against the freshman senator for having a thin political resume and living in a   "bubble" to protect him from the press. When asked about the ads, Rubio would only cite Christie’s moderate record on issues like Common Core, judicial nominations, and credit downgrades in the state of New Jersey.
