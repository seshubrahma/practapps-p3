For example, the familiar gambit of outsourcing jobs to China, or Mexico —  or 50 other countries —  and calling that "free trade" is now socially unacceptable, and politically unsustainable. Still, the broader vision of planetary freedom, including the free flow of peoples and their ideas, is always enormously appealing.   The United States, as well as the world, undoubtedly benefits from competition, from social and economic mobility —   and yes, from new blood.
