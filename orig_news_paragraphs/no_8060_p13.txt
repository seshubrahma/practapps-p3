In Florida, Sen. Bill Nelson ( ) easily defeated former Rep. Connie Mack IV 55 percent to 42 percent in 2012. Nelson is a long time political figure in Florida. Though Mack ran a very flawed campaign, unseating Nelson will be a significant —  and very expensive —  challenge for Republicans in 2018. In Pennsylvania, Sen.
