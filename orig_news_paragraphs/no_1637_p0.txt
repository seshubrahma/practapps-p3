ERIE, Pa.   —    Senator Tim Kaine of Virginia, Hillary Clinton’s running mate, challenged Donald J. Trump on Tuesday to be more forthcoming about his health, taking aim at Mr. Trump over an issue he has tried to use to undermine Mrs. Clinton. As part of a lengthy critique of Mr. Trump, Mr. Kaine mocked a   letter signed last year by a doctor for Mr. Trump, which proclaimed the candidate’s strength and stamina to be "extraordinary" and declared that he would be "the healthiest individual ever elected to the presidency.
