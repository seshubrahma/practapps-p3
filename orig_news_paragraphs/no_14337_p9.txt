If he can keep this up, he’d be following a long tradition of politicos taking parody in stride.    In 1995, House Republicans invited Chris Farley, who’d been impersonating Speaker Newt Gingrich on "SNL," to impersonate him to his face at the conference meeting while Gingrich laughed along.    After spoofing George H. W. Bush for four years, Dana Carvey was invited to the White House to meet him. After Bush lost to Bill Clinton, he had Carvey come cheer up his staff.
