The single biggest way to stop the drug crisis is to educate   people on the fact that if you do it, it’s probably going to kill   you or it’s going to ruin your life. So why don’t we all work   together to stop that? We don’t have to be fighting about some   national security policy. Let’s start talking about the things   that pull us together, and then maybe we can send a message to   the politicians and the other leaders in the business world and   the sports world.
