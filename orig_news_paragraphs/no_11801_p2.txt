Districts 2, 3, 4 and 5 voted Friday, selecting 10 officially pledged Cruz delegates. District 2 also elected two unpledged delegates that are expected to support Cruz. One of those unpledged delegates, Michael McAlpine, is listed as Cruz for President Grassroots Leader on a list of leadership team members released by the Cruz campaign. Districts 1 and 6 voted last Saturday and District 7 voted on Thursday.
