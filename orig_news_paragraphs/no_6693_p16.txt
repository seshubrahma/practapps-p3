By 2015, the Pentagon admitted that a number of "vetted" FSA members had handed their weapons over to   the Nusra Front, or merely joined their ranks. One FSA faction pledged allegiance to Islamic State Caliph Abu Bakr  . What is left of the FSA now relies on Turkey for its support, largely politically but with some reported military aid. The United States has largely shifted the focus of its support to the YPG.
