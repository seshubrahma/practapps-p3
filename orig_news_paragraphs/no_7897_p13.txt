Ryan dragged out his decision to endorse Donald Trump for weeks —  leaving voters with the distinct impression that Ryan does not consider the choice between Trump versus Clinton to be an easy one. Although Ryan has now technically endorsed Trump, he continues to be one of Trump’s most frequent public critics. In interviews about the election, Ryan seems much more aggressive in attacking Trump than he has been in going after Hillary Clinton. In this sense, some argue that Ryan has perhaps done more damage to Trump with his endorsement than he could have done by not endorsing him
