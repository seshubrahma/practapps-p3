Washington (CNN) President Donald Trump’s administration says green card holders    including those detained at airports across the United States in the wake of Trump’s ban on travel from seven nations    will be allowed into the country. Reince Priebus, Trump’s chief of staff, said on NBC’s "Meet the Press" that "as far as green card holders moving forward, it doesn’t affect them."
