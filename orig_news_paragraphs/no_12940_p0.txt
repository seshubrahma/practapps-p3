A new poll from CBS News conducted before Saturday’s GOP debate, shows Donald Trump with 42 percent support among Republicans, and a massive 22 point lead over   candidate Sen. Ted Cruz. [The three candidates vying for the "establishment lane"  —   Jeb Bush, Gov. John Kasich and Sen. Marco Rubio combine for just 30 percent support.   The poll of likely Republican primary voters, drawn from a larger sample of 1, 300 registered voters, shows Trump with 42 percent, followed by Cruz with 20 percent. Rubio is third with 15 percent, ahead of Kasich with 9 percent and of Jeb Bush with 6 percent.
