Editor and publisher of the   German newspaper Die Zeit Josef Joffe has said that "murder in the White House" could be a way to end the "Trump catastrophe" on a German television programme. [  Mr. Joffe made the remarks on an episode of "Presse club" on the public broadcaster ARD during a segment which featured calls from viewers. One female caller asked the panel if it was possible for the Americans to impeach the newly inaugurated president to end what she called the "Trump catastrophe. "
