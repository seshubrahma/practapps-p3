It seems likely that Trump sees One Belt, One Road as bearing a family resemblance to the the massive infrastructure program he would like to launch in the U. S. The combination of innovative financing with ambitious plans to build roads and railways could present a learning opportunity for Trump administration. There’s also a more practical aspect. One of the motivations behind the launch of One Belt, One Road was to give massively overbuilt domestic Chinese industries —  including construction, cement, steel and coal —  new sources of demand.
