Melania Trump’s address was warmly received by the rowdy Republican crowd, but did not include    glimpses of life in Trump Tower or other details that might offer some insight into the man behind the image. In an unusual move for a nominee, Trump appeared on a stage bathed in smoky light to introduce his wife to the sound of Queen’s rock anthem, "We are the Champions." "We are going to win, we are going to win so big. Thank you very much," Trump said in an uncharacteristically brief appearance of only one minute six seconds in the spotlight.
