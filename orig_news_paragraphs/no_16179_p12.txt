Priebus downplayed other differences between Trump and Pence, as well. Asked if the Republican ticket would have supported the Iraq War    which Pence backed in Congress and Trump has said he opposed     he said: "You’ll have to ask them."   And on Trump’s proposed temporary ban on Muslims entering the United States, Priebus insisted that Trump’s position has evolved to a more targeted ban on travel from countries that are terrorist safe havens, and has nothing to do with religion.
