Experts say too much caffeine alone can cause caffeine intoxication, leading to insomnia, anxiety, restlessness, muscle twitching.   And they add that in adolescents, more than 100 milligrams of caffeine per day, that’s about what’s in a cup of coffee, has been associated with elevated blood pressure. The American Academy of Pediatrics says kids should avoid energy drinks altogether.   It says teenagers should limit their intake of caffeine to a hundred milligrams per day.
