On his radio show Wednesday, Glenn Beck blasted Matt Drudge of the Drudge Report and Breitbart News as "nothing but shills" for GOP frontrunner Donald Trump, but Beck stated that he and his staff have "proven" themselves "not to be shills" for Ted Cruz. ["Matt Drudge and Breitbart  —   they’ll never say it, but they are nothing but shills for Trump. I will tell you this: we have proven ourselves not to be shills for Cruz," Beck said.   The Drudge Report and Breitbart News have not endorsed any candidate in the 2016 GOP primary.
