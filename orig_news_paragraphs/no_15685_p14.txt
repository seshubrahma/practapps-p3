A recent academic study of polling suggests the email questions dominated all the news about Clinton in the final days of the election and further suggests it did a lot to hurt her. She was unable to shake the scandal and ineffective in explaining why she had the server in the first place. Comey’s actions, witting or not, compounded those problems. Clinton herself has said it was Comey’s decisions, particularly the one in October, that interrupted her momentum and cost her the election.
