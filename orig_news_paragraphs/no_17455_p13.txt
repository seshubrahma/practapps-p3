July 22, 2016   Days before the Democratic National Convention, WikiLeaks posts nearly 20, 000 emails hacked from the DNC server. The documents include notes in which DNC chair Debbie Wasserman Schultz insults staffers from the Bernie Sanders campaign and messages that suggest the organization was favoring Clinton rather than remaining neutral. Wasserman Schultz resigns in the aftermath of the leak.     July 25, 2016
