Cleveland (CNN) Donald Trump has never faced more pressure on the national stage than he will Thursday evening when he accepts the Republican nomination.   This week’s Republican National Convention has been dominated by unexpected fireworks ranging from the plagiarized speech his wife delivered and the way the campaign prolonged the episode to Ted Cruz’s shocking address Wednesday in which he refused to endorse Trump and encouraged Americans to vote their conscience. Cruz’s speech exposed the deep divisions that remain in the party after the fractious primary.
