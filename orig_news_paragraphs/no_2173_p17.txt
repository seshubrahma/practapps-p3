Born: 1962 Position: United States Court of Appeals for the 11th Circuit Background: Judge Pryor was Alabama’s attorney general when he was nominated by President Bush in 2003. That year, he was at the center of a national firestorm when he called for the removal of the state’s chief justice, Roy S. Moore, who had refused to obey a federal court order to remove a Ten Commandments monument from the state’s judicial building.
