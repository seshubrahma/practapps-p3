Also harming Peña Nieto’s popularity this year was his administration ’s decision to increase gas prices as much as 20% in January, as part of a plan to deregulate the energy sector.   The price hike    dubbed ’el gasolinazo’    sparked violent protests across Mexico, resulting in at least 1, 500 arrests and six deaths in the first month of the year. Protests got so out of hand in Tijuana last week that US authorities decided to temporarily close the border.
