Interestingly, about 25 percent of Trump and Clinton voters stated that they don’t find   awards show speeches to be persuasive. The survey also found that 79 percent of Clinton voters plan to watch this year’s Oscars, compared to just 66 of Trump voters. Asked if Trump should be invited to this year’s Oscars, 62 percent of Trump voters said yes, while 84 percent of Clinton supporters said no. Of the major awards shows so far this season, the Golden Globes, the Grammy Awards and the Screen Actors Guild (SAG) Awards all heavily featured   speeches, with most centering on opposition to President Trump and his policies.
