Road to Peace works with organizations around the world to help build alliances between communities in conflict and facilitate specialized medical care for refugee children.   The family had a plan: With the charity’s assistance, Dilbireen and Ajeel would go to the US for the boy’s first round of reconstructive surgeries in October.
