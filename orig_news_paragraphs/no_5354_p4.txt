The Hollywood Reporter solicits opinions from anonymous Oscar voters every year in the week before the ceremony. While the responses of one Academy member are hardly an indication of how the entirety of the 6, 000+ members will ultimately vote, the   "brutally honest" ballots usually offer a refreshing perspective on the process. In addition to her comments on Streep, this year’s anonymous voter said La La Land star Emma Stone’s performance wasn’t "as wonderful" as everyone is saying, the   thriller Arrival "just sucked" and the Denzel   Davis starring Fences underwhelmed because "they just filmed the play.
