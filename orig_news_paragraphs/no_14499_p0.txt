Washington (CNN) Just days before the Democratic National Committee elects its new leader, candidates for the job will hash out their visions for the party at CNN’s "Debate Night: Democratic Leadership Debate" on Wednesday. The debate, moderated by CNN Chief Political Correspondent Dana Bash and "New Day" anchor Chris Cuomo, will air at 10 p. m. ET from the CNN Center in Atlanta. The candidates will lay out strategies for the 2018 midterm elections, rebuilding the Democratic Party and the DNC’s role under the Trump administration.
