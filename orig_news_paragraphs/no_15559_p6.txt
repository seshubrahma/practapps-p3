With Mueller’s mandate this broad, and Trump’s life and cash so complicated, overzealous Democrats needn’t feel compelled to manufacture drama. And yet, it’s almost certain some will. The day the special counsel was announced, for example, Rep. Al Green of Texas took dramatically to the House floor to call for Trump’s impeachment.   The danger here, of course, is that if the investigation reveals no smoking gun, Trump’s supporters will insist    and with some authority, then    that nothing his detractors say should ever be believed.
