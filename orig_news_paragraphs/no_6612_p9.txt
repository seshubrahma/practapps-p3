This week President Donald Trump informed Planned Parenthood it would have to quit performing abortions in order to keep its federal funding for its "other" services. However, Dawn Laguens, executive vice president of Planned Parenthood Federation of America (PPFA) rejected Trump’s proposal. The provision to end Planned Parenthood’s federal funding would redirect it to other federally qualified healthcare centers (FQHCs) that provide more health services, but do not perform abortions.
