The Associated Press (@AP) July 8, 2016,  Update 7:43 a. m.: As officials around the country react, some offer solidarity with Dallas and with police, but some seem to equivocate. As Breitbart California reports: Political leaders and law enforcement officials in Los Angeles have expressed outrage at the murders of five police officers at a Black Lives Matter protest in Dallas. In contrast, officials in San Francisco and Oakland equivocated, or ignored the event.
