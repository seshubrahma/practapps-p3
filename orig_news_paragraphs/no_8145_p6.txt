Hungary, along with neighbours Slovakia, the Czech Republic, and Poland, constitute the Visegrad 4 group, who have been a major bloc in opposition of mass migration within the European Union. The V4 now look to the presidential elections in Austria for another potential ally in the   migration Freedom Party (FPÖ) candidate Norbert Hofer. After meeting with Czech president Miloš Zeman, Hofer related his intentions for Austria to join the group as well.
