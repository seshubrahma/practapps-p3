Appearing in Pittsburgh as Mr. Obama spoke, Hillary Clinton gave a blistering denunciation of her own. She echoed many of the president’s points and even some of his language, assailing Mr. Trump’s temperament, ridiculing his proposals and arguing forcefully that he had failed to meet the gravity of the moment. " History will remember what we do in this moment," she told hundreds of supporters inside a union hall, asking "responsible Republican leaders" to join her in condemning Mr. Trump.
