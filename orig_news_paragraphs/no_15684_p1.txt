During a working lunch in Brussels ahead of the NATO summit, President Trump made it clear to Macron he would not announce a decision on the Paris climate agreement prior to the G7, this official told CNN. Trump told Macron he is under "heavy" pressure back in the US on the climate accord brokered by the Obama administration in 2015. " A lot of people in my country are against this agreement," Trump told Macron during their lunch, according to this person.
