Renowned chef Anthony Bourdain, host of CNN’s travel and food series Parts Unknown, says he’s not at all interested in sitting down and eating with Donald Trump. ["Absolutely fucking not," Bourdain said when asked whether he might interview the Republican presidential candidate.   In an interview with the Wrap, the award winning chef and New York City native said he’s long believed Trump to be a "loathsome" man and would have turned down dinner with the White House hopeful "10 years ago.
