Future First Lady Melania Trump is taking action to remove a video from the Internet that questions whether her    son Barron is autistic   —   and has slammed Rosie O’Donnell as a "bully" for sharing the video on social media.   On November 11, YouTube user James Hunter posted a video entitled, "Is Barron Trump Autistic? #StopTheBullying," which has since been viewed nearly four million times.
