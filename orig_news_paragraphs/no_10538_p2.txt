This is called "originalism," what many   call being a "strict constructionist. " Plain Meaning, There are only two ways to obtain citizenship: automatically at birth, or after birth through some legal process such as immigration and naturalization. The plain meaning of "natural born citizen" is understood to be a person who was a citizen "naturally," referring to the conditions that attended their natural birth into this world, as opposed to undergoing a citizenship process later in life or through immigration. It does not mean —  and has never meant —  that both parents had to be U. S. citizens, or that a person had to be born on U. S. soil.
