Marco Rubio is airing the second most ads of any Republican candidate, so the stakes have been raised for him as well. Finishing a strong third in Iowa, Rubio entered the final stretch before the New Hampshire vote with   momentum. This earned media and his robust advertising in New Hampshire positioned him to run a close second to Trump, with a possibility of competing to win. Polling over the last week, however, has shown very little movement to Rubio.
