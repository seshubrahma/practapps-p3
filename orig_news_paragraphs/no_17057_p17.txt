What about transgender students? The Obama administration’s recommendation to schools to treat transgender students in accordance with their gender identity drew cheers from advocacy groups and criticism from conservatives, including Pence. Trump said transgender people should be protected under the law but rejected the use of federal law to do so, saying it should be left to the states.   Even if Trump rescinds the Obama administration’s guidance, the laws upon which it was based won’t change, said David Stacy, Government Affairs Director of HRC.
