In both countries, where the hosts showered Trump with flattery and warmth, Trump played the role of gracious guest.   But his visit grew more complicated as he arrived in Europe, where he joined heads of state     many wary of his presidency    in groups and where the welcome was far more muted. There, he berated members of the NATO alliance and aggressively jostled the prime minister of miniscule Montenegro to get to the front of a photo line, straightening his jacket with emphasis as he landed on his mark.
