Ashton Eaton of the U. S. won his second consecutive Olympic gold medal in the decathlon. And the track star Usain Bolt of Jamaica earned his third straight gold at 200 meters. There’s one world champion who isn’t competing at the Olympics: Giovanna Petrucci, a slackliner. The sport, which involves performing on a thin strap of nylon or polyester tied to trees, isn’t part of the Games.
