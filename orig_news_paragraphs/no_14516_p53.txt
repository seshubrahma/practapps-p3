White House spokesman Josh Earnest says that the US will be deploying "less than 50" Special Operations forces, who will be sent to   territory in northern Syria. The American troops will help local Kurdish and Arab forces fighting ISIS with logistics and are planning to bolster their efforts. February 26, 2016   A temporary cessation of hostilities goes into effect. The truce calls for the Syrian regime and rebels to give relief organizations access to disputed territories so they can assist civilians.
