Center for Security Policy President Frank Gaffney joined SiriusXM host Matt Boyle on Wednesday’s Breitbart News Daily to reflect on the 75th anniversary of Pearl Harbor. ["As with you, Matt, my thoughts go out to all those who suffered, whose lives were upended, in some cases, actually terminated, on this terrible day 75 years ago   —   and the many, many millions of others who, in the period shortly before that and subsequent to it, perished in the course of World War II," said Gaffney.
