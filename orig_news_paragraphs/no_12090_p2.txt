Of course, these    proclamations and policies are nothing new.   The Progressive Republicans a century ago, including Theodore Roosevelt, William Howard Taft and Herbert Hoover, all embraced them.   Even earlier, they were central to the   People’s Party, which was eventually swallowed by the Democrat Party’s progressive movement.
