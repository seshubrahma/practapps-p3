The butterfly ballot has figured in attempts to delegitimize the 2000 election ever since it was held. For example, Florida Congressman Rob Wexler declared, "I think it’s fair to say Theresa LePore’s mistake resulted in the wrong man becoming President. " Wexler was one of several Democrat heavyweights to work for LePore’s defeat in 2004 others included Gore’s running mate Joe Lieberman and another Democrat presidential contender, Vermont Governor Howard Dean.
