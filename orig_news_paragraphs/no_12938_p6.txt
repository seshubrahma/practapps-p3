While Los Zetas cartel members were murdering innocent victims and terrorizing the state, Coahuila’s Governor Humberto Moreira turned a blind eye and was arrested in Spain for having been a surrogate for the fearsome drug cartel. He has since been released. Federal authorities in the U. S. are also investigating him. Moreira was a key figure in Peña Nieto’s election when he served as the national president for the PRI.
