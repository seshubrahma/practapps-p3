Photos of protesters from a demonstration in Los Angeles on Wednesday night showed many marchers brandishing signs from leftist organizations such as the ANSWER Coalition and the Party for Socialism and Liberation, as shown on KCETLink. The Associated Press reported that at least 185 people have been arrested in Los Angeles out of over 225 people protesting across the country. In Portland on Friday, one person was shot and injured at an   protest.
