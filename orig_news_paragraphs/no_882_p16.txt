At a rally near the United States Embassy, female police officers holding shields stood guard as a women’s group, Gabriela, held a rally. The president of the Philippines, Rodrigo Duterte, who has made inflammatory remarks about sexual assault, gave a speech praising women   —   though he also lashed out at the country’s   female elected official, Vice President Leni Robredo, and at a political rival, Senator Leila de Lima. Another senator, Risa Hontiveros, accused Mr. Duterte of allowing a "pervasive culture of sexism, misogyny and gender bias.
