Washington (CNN) President Donald Trump’s CIA director, Mike Pompeo, was "surprised" by a report of a draft executive order directing the agency to reexamine the use of interrogation techniques that are legally considered torture, a source with knowledge of the director ’s thinking told CNN. The source added that the draft, the contents of which were reported by The New York Times, is in fact an administration document being circulated to various agencies. But the White House said Wednesday that the circulated paper was not an official administration product.
