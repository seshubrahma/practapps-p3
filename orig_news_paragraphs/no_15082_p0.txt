 (CNN) The US ambassador to the United Nations, Nikki Haley, has told CNN that removing Syrian President Bashar   from power is a priority, cementing an extraordinary   in the Trump administration’s stance on the embattled leader. But before Tuesday’s chemical attack on the   town of Khan Sheikhoun, which killed 89 people, Haley had said toppling Assad was not a priority.
