Republicans aren’t oblivious to Trump’s struggles. One   GOP official conceded that some top Republicans begin each day fearing what new   sideshow Trump could create.   Another, pointing to the   furor in the progressive base and the huge turnout at town halls, said that "our guys are getting demolished" by an energized Democratic Party.   Still, very few would openly criticize Trump’s job performance beyond casting controversies like the Comey firing as an unhelpful distraction.
