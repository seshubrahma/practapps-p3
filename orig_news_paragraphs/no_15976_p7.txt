Lewandowski consistently pushed back on widespread reporting that his presence in the Trump campaign had grown toxic, even as he acknowledged that he is a "very intense person," a perfectionist and a "straight shooter"    sometimes to his own detriment. And even without a formal role with the campaign, Lewandowski said he would do whatever he could to help Trump win the White House.   He was also notably unapologetic: "I have no regrets as it comes to this campaign," he said.
