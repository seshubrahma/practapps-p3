From Columbus’s Great Discovery to the War on Terror,   with Michael Allen, was a #1 New York Times bestseller, and captured the interest of the nascent Tea Party movement, which would soon come to fruition at the end of George W. Bush’s presidency and the beginning of Barack Obama’s. How Trump Won similarly captures the mood of the populist insurgency that propelled Trump to power in one of the most unlikely, and most exciting, elections in the history of the United States.
