For Republicans, the possibility of an indictment is a satisfying one. The GOP has pursued the Clintons for 25 years, from the scandals of Bill Clinton’s presidency and his impeachment to Hillary Clinton’s actions leading up to and during the Benghazi attacks. That the FBI could hamstring her presidential ambitions is a bonus for the GOP. Republicans pounce, Republican National Committee Chairman Reince Priebus said in a statement that Clinton is now "the first major party presidential candidate to be interviewed by the FBI as part of a criminal investigation surrounding her reckless conduct."
