Washington (CNN) President Donald Trump is all about the bark    bold, aggressive promises. Whether it’s unfair Chinese trade practices, the North American Free Trade Agreement he’s dubbed a "disaster" or Iran’s destabilizing actions, Trump has kept up much of his bold campaign rhetoric. But when it comes to follow through, at least in his first 100 days in office Trump has come up short on the bite, failing to deliver on his toughest campaign promises.
