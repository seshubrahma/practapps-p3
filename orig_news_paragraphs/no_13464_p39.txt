July 9, 2016,  UPDATE 1:24 A. M. ET, Reports out of Rochester, New York, say a press conference will happen tonight —  in the middle of the night —  suggesting things may have been much worse in the city than previously thought. Earlier reports, citing law enforcement officials, say as many as 40 people may have been arrested there. A local news outlet says one of their reporters was among those arrested. This local reporter says a second one of her reporter colleagues was also arrested.
