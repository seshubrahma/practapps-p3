If the president and his institutions come to see each other as an enemy, trust could be difficult to restore. Susan Hennessey, a former National Security Agency lawyer, and Helen Klein Murillo, a law student, wrote in Lawfare that Mr. Trump’s accusations that career civil servants are partisan agents, along with his administration’s aggressive internal investigations, could be read as "an intention to use the pretense of leak investigations to engage in political retaliation. " This risks deepening    mistrust of Mr. Trump, which Ms. Hennessey and Ms. Murillo believe helped motivate initial leaks.
