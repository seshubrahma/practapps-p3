HBO’s "Real Time" host Bill Maher stated that if he had to choose between GOP presidential candidates Donald Trump and Texas Senator Ted Cruz, he would pick Cruz because "Ted Cruz will be our worst president, but Donald Trump, might well be our last" on Friday.
