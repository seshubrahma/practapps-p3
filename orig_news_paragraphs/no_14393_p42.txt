Nevertheless, it is important to recognize that there are three categories of individuals: (1) noncitizens outside the United States (2) noncitizens inside the United States. and (3) Legal Permanent Residents (LPRs) and citizens.   While noncitizens outside the United States have no constitutional rights, noncitizens inside have a limited modicum of constitutional rights and LPRs and citizens have full constitutional rights.
