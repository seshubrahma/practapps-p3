By the summer of 2009, Shelby was backing away from the issue as an aide explained "while he hasn’t personally seen the President’s birth certificate, he is confident that the matter has been thoroughly examined."   The birther issue receded into the realm of conspiracy theorists and fringe political figures such as the actor Chuck Norris, a   document "expert" named Ron Polarik, and Orly Taitz, a California dentist who circulated a document she claimed showed Obama was born in Kenya. It was labeled a forgery by Kenyan officials.
