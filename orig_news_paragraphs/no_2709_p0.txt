When it comes to predicting how   Donald J. Trump’s administration will affect America’s schools and universities, education experts say they are struggling to read the tea leaves. " The fundamental issue is that nobody really knows what the Trump administration is about" on education, said Frederick M. Hess, a conservative education policy expert. At a panel discussion in Washington last week, he joked that Mr. Trump’s trademark educational achievement thus far, creating the controversial Trump University, placed him in history alongside another president, Thomas Jefferson, the founder of the University of Virginia.
