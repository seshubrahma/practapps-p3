In response to GAO’s findings, the Department of Homeland Security said metrics to assess fencing contributions would be developed, according to the report. The CBP acknowledged the GAO report, and said it would be implementing its recommendations. " As a result of CBP’s tactical infrastructure and technology investments between the ports of entry, the U. S. Border Patrol (USBP) can detect and interdict illegal activity, monitor evolving threat patterns, and strategically deploy assets,"  a spokesman said.
