In one sense, the strange controversy over Trump’s tweets has left the President in a position where he is most comfortable politically   —   at odds with the media and the political and intelligence establishment in Washington. But it has also evolved into a political distraction at a time when Trump is facing fights over Obamacare repeal and the budget that will help define his first 100 days in office. This week, for example, the administration should benefit from favorable coverage of the confirmation hearings of Trump’s respected Supreme Court pick Neil Gorsuch, but the Intelligence Committee hearing has the capacity to overshadow the judge’s testimony.
