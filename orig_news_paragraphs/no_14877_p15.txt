The Chinese foreign ministry statement quoted Tillerson as saying that Trump expected the two leaders to meet "as soon as possible" but did not confirm the April summit. Despite fiery rhetoric on the campaign trail and prior to his inauguration, Trump and his administration have taken a relatively   approach to China so far.
