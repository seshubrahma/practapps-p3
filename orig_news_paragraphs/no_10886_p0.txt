During an interview broadcast on Wednesday’s "CNN Newsroom," author, New York Times opinion writer, and Purdue Associate Professor of English Roxane Gay stated she "would have a hard time" talking to a relative who voted for Donald Trump.   Gay said she would talk politics over Thanksgiving, and that she has a Republican brother who, she joked, "voted for Hillary Clinton, thank goodness. So we can speak to him. He won’t be banished.
