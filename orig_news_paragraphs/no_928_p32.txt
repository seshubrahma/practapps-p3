Quietly, apart from his son, Mr. Winemiller worries about leaving him alone in the farmhouse when his   days in the fields resume. " I hate to say this, but because of his past, I don’t trust him," he said. They pulled into the Clinton County Adult Probation offices for the son’s   drug test, then set out again for the drive to a new treatment center where he gets counseling and doses of buprenorphine, which can help addicts stay off opioids by keeping them from experiencing cravings and withdrawal.
