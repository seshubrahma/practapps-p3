At key moments in the primary, he urged his listeners to fast and pray for Cruz’s victory. But as Trump’s momentum continued, Beck expressed harsh criticism for the Evangelical Christians who threw their support to Cruz’s opponent, declaring at one point that "real Christians" don’t support Donald Trump and that "all throughout the South the Evangelicals are not listening to their God" because Cruz lost the Southern primaries to Trump. The fervor of Beck’s support for Cruz has been matched only by the vehemence of his opposition to Trump, whom he regularly compares to Nazi dictator Adolf Hitler.
