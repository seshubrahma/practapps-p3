The US had ordered 35 officials and their families from the embassy in Washington and the consulate in San Francisco to leave the country by noon Sunday in response to charges that Russians hacked the presidential election and harassed US diplomatic personnel in Russia.   A State Department official told CNN early Sunday evening all of the expelled Russian diplomats have left the US. " We can confirm that the 35 Russian diplomats declared persona non grata have, along with their family members, departed the United States. ," the official said.
