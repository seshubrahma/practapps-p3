Judge Gorsuch argued for a signing statement for several reasons, including that such a statement would help advance the view that the   measure applied to existing lawsuits and not just to future ones   —   a view the Supreme Court later rejected   —   and that it could set the stage for interpreting the torture ban in a limited way.
