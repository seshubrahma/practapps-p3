" A man you can bait with a tweet is not a man we can trust with nuclear weapons," Clinton said in her acceptance speech, capturing in one line mounting fears about Trump’s temperament and preparedness. Trump’s binge of bombast in the wake of the Democratic convention only seemed to authenticate the argument Clinton and others had been making against him. And the furor over his peevish   outbursts led several prominent Republicans to disavow him, perhaps easing the way for others to follow.
