The deployment of armies on the battlefield is the classic manifestation of collective  . If both sides are not convinced of their military superiority, there will be no confrontation rather, those who lack confidence will simply flee the field. Accordingly, the battle is decided the moment the confidence of one side fails. The will to fight ("morale") evaporates, the military formation collapses, and the army seeks salvation in flight or, if it is lucky, in organized retreat.
