President Barack Obama and congressional Republicans claim the middle class should like America’s pending trade deal with 11 other Pacific nations. But there’s one demographic that already loves it: billionaires. Last week, dozens of New York City’s power elite signed a letter to the state’s congressional delegation, urging lawmakers to support the   Partnership now in negotiations. In addition to Rupert Murdoch, other signatories of the letter included the chairman and CEO of Goldman Sachs, the Chairman and CEO of American Express, the Chief Executive Officer of Citigroup, the Chairman and CEO of JPMorgan Chase, the Chairman and CEO of Morgan Stanley, and the Chief Executive Officer of New Corporation.
