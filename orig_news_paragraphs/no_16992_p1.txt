Below are some of Trump’s signature proposals supporters expect him to deliver on when he takes office in January. The wall, Without question, Trump’s central campaign plank was his pledge to build a wall on the   border. Trump made immigration a top focal point from the moment he launched his White House bid in June of 2015, when he controversially claimed that Mexico was sending "rapists" and "criminals" across the border.   The wall, which Trump has promised will be "beautiful," was the centerpiece of his immigration policy.
