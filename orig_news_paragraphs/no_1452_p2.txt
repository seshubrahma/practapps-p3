Switzerland’s unemployment rate was 3. 3 percent in May, lower than that of Britain or the United States, which have both been hovering around 5 percent this year. Switzerland has one of the lowest crime rates in the world, and there have been no terrorist incidents there in the last 30 years. Known for its religious tolerance since a civil war during the Reformation, Switzerland accommodates four national languages and has a higher percentage of Muslims (5 percent) than Britain. Roughly a quarter of Switzerland’s population is made up of foreigners.
