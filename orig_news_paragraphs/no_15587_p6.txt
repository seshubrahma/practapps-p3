Misprision of a felony occurs when: (1) a third party commits an alleged felony and (2) the defendant has full knowledge of the crime (3) he or she fails to notify authorities and, most importantly, (4) helps conceal the crime. If ordering or inducing the FBI chief to back off Flynn, an allegation news reports say was based on a memo Comey drafted after meeting with Trump, amounts to concealing an underlying crime, then this could be a misprision case.
