Following the death of his   son in a car accident, Hafez groomed his second son, Bashar   to be his heir apparent. Bashar took over in 2000 after Hafez’s death.    The North Korean dictatorial dynasty goes back a little further than Syria’s. With the help of the Soviet Union, Kim Il Sung, the grandfather of the Kim dynasty also known as "The Eternal President," became the first premier of the Democratic People’s Republic of Korea in 1948.
