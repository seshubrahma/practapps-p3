American textile companies make technologically advanced fabrics like those for carpeting, parachutes or the steel mesh inside tires. But even if you accept that these trends contribute to higher economic output, and to more   jobs and more competitive companies in the United States, it’s not as if the anxiety about losing jobs that Mr. Trump describes doesn’t have a realistic basis.
