A revealing headline atop a March 2 story in Stat News blares: "Trump’s health care plan takes (another) page from the Democrats. "  In the words of   journalist David Nather, Trump "has embraced an idea to bring down drug costs that’s associated more with Democrats like Hillary Clinton and Bernie Sanders than with the party he’s trying to lead.
