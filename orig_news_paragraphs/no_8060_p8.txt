Despite Trump’s huge victory over Clinton in the Mountaineer State, Manchin remains a favorite to win   in 2018 due to his long history as a political figure in the state and his unique ability to convince voters there he is a conservative Democrat with truly populist instincts. Though a party switch by Manchin between now and the election can not entirely be ruled out, if he remains a Democrat he will likely remain a heavy favorite to win   unless Republicans can attract a strong well financed challenger. On Thursday, Manchin told Fox News Channel’s Brian Kilmeade that he would "absolutely" remain a Democrat.
