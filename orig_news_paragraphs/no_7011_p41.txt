Cardiovascular disease remains the number cause of death in the United States, yet, not one drug to treat primary cardiovascular disease was approved in 2016.   Similarly, there were no drugs approved to treat obesity or Alzheimer’s disease, and just one for diabetes.   These are conditions that are afflicting Americans in epidemic proportions and which cause tremendous morbidity (sickness) and mortality (death). As an aside, we can see, once again, the value of a strategy —  a strategy that prioritizes needed categories of cures.
