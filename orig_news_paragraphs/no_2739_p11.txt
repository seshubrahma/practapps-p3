In the prelude to the party conference, Ms. Merkel had attended a series of regional meetings, occasionally facing a demand to resign, or hostile criticism of her decision to allow migrants free passage in 2015. Little of those critiques surfaced at the briskly managed conference on Tuesday, and Ms. Merkel won a   standing ovation. She was reaffirmed in the party leadership she has held since 2000 by a thumping 89. 5 percent of votes from 994 party delegates.
