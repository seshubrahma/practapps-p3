 (CNN) Donald Trump’s accusation that NATO is "obsolete" has led to "astonishment and agitation" within the alliance, Germany’s foreign minister has said. Speaking in Brussels before a meeting of EU foreign ministers,   Steinmeier suggested the alliance was rattled by the remarks by Trump. In a joint interview with the Times of London and the German publication Bild, Trump signaled that US foreign policy in a range of areas could be turned on its head.
