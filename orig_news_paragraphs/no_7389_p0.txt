Speaker of the House Paul Ryan has refuted Donald Trump’s claims that the upcoming presidential election will be rigged against him, saying that he is "confident" that there will be no foul play in the electoral process. ["Our democracy relies on confidence in election results, and the speaker is fully confident the states will carry out this election with integrity,"  Ryan’s press secretary told BuzzFeed.   This follows Trump’s claims at a rally in North Carolina yesterday that the "whole election is being rigged," adding that "the corrupt media is trying to do everything in their power to stop our movement.
