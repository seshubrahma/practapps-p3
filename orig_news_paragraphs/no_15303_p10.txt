Nevermind that millions of people around the world die each year from diseases linked to air pollution    much of which comes from coal. The administration is reportedly mulling pulling out from the Paris Agreement, an international accord designed to push the planet out of the fossil fuel era. Federal monuments and parks are under review funding for regulators is on the chopping block. All of this is likely to lead to more pollution and therefore more warming    more wildfires, longer droughts, rising seas, mass extinction.
