In his interview with Smerconish, Buchanan also said the "nation divided as it has never been divided" and "you’ve got words like racist, sexist, homophobe" being hurled around on television every day. He said, "We are not one people and one nation the way we were in 1960 under Eisenhower. " Buchanan admitted the country "had some things wrong on the civil rights," but the country in which he grew up "was a good country, and what these fellows see are the same things I’m seeing: we are losing the best of the country that we grew up in. The government here in Washington, A, is incompetent, B, its system is rigged, and C, we are being pulled away from the center.
