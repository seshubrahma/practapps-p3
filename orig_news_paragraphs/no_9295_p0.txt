Sen. Bernie Sanders ( ) delivered a virtual concession in a written statement following his speech on Tuesday evening after losing all but Rhode Island   —    the lone state with an open primary  —   to presumptive Democratic presidential nominee Hillary Clinton. ["I congratulate Secretary Clinton on her victories tonight, and I look forward to   campaigns in the 14 contests to come," Sanders wrote in a statement issued from his campaign’s temporary headquarters in Huntington, West Virginia where he is campaigning.
