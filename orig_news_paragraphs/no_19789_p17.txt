As the squabbling Russian opposition was trying to consolidate into a coalition that might give it a chance in the parliamentary election in September —   the opposition had but a single seat in the Duma —  another videotape dropped. This one was of Mikhail Kasyanov, former prime minister and a controversial member of the opposition, in bed with another activist, trash talking everyone else they worked with. It wasn’t long before the coalition descended into recriminations and gossip, and, predictably, bombed in the parliamentary election.
