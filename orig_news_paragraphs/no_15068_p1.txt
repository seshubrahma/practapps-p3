The latest attacks come after a missile strike early Friday by the United States on a base in western Syria that the United States says was used to launch Tuesday’s chemical attack, which left more than 85 people dead and hundreds more injured. The new strikes came as Russia, the Syrian regime’s main ally, sent a frigate armed with cruise missiles to a port in western Syria in an apparent show of force in response to the US action. Key developments, The Syrian air force resumed flight operations at the base the US struck Friday. Pentagon investigating possible Russian involvement in chemical attack that prompted US strike.
