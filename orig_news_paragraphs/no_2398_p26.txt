It was the Republican ticket’s second stop in   Minnesota in two days, and although few signs point to a Republican upset there, a modest crowd near Mr. Pence’s plane lapped up his attacks on Mrs. Clinton. " It’s almost hard to keep up, all the headlines, all the scandals flowing out of just her years as secretary of state," Mr. Pence said.
