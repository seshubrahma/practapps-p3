After the   Scania truck came to a stop on the Budapest road having just crashed through the Christmas market, officers found the body of its Polish operator and his   clothes. Officers said he had been shot with a   weapon. Identified as    Luke U. the driver had been transporting a a shipment of metal beams from Italy to Berlin for delivery on Monday. The director of the freight shipping company and cousin to Luke U. Ariel Zurawski, has spoken to journalists remarking that he last had contact with his relative on Monday afternoon to tell him unloading of his cargo had been suspended until Tuesday, and that he would have to stay with his truck in Berlin overnight.
