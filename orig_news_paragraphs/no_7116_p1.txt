The Census Bureau has been posting these quarterly reports about the mining industry in the U. S. including data about profits and losses after taxes, since the fourth quarter of 2000. In the first quarter of 2016, U. S. mining corporations with $50 million or more in assets cited a collective $27. 075 billion in losses. The U. S. mining industry had not reported profits since the third quarter of 2014 when it cited profits of  $10. 258 billion.
