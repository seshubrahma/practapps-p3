National frontrunner Donald Trump is second with 27 percent support. Florida Sen. Marco Rubio and neurosurgeon Ben Carson are a distant third and fourth, with 12 and 9 percent support respectively. Like in New Hampshire, Christie is competing with Marco Rubio, Jeb Bush, and John Kasich for the more establishment lane in the nomination fight.
