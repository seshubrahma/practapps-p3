 (CNN) There are many questionable aspects of Donald Trump’s revised child care tax plan, as announced Wednesday in Pennsylvania with his daughter Ivanka by his side, but one point is very easy to make and understand: It’s not a "child care" plan at all. Trump initially announced last month in Detroit a plan to allow families to "fully deduct" the costs of child care from their income taxes, a proposal roundly criticized as overwhelmingly favoring   moms, in high tax brackets, like Ivanka.
