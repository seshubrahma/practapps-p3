In the event that Sessions, the new attorney general, is unable to fulfill his normal duties, Trump’s succession pick, Dana Boente, US attorney for the Eastern District of Virginia, will be prepped to fill the gap. Boente briefly acted as attorney general after Trump fired the former acting head, Sally Yates, after she declined to defend the administration’s executive action on immigration.   Trump’s presidential memorandums, In addition to executive orders, Trump has signed 12 presidential memorandums, which have less legal weight than an executive order and are more important as documents laying out the priorities of his administration. They can have real consequences, however.
