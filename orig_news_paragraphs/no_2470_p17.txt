The space at the Trump International Hotel has been empty since the celebrity chefs José Andrés and Geoffrey Zakarian backed out in protest of Mr. Trump’s incendiary comments about Mexican immigrants during the campaign, prompting legal action by the Trump organization. Now, Nakazawa, a Japanese omakase bar and dining room, is stepping up with a new restaurant, to be opened by next summer. " My decisions are not clouded by political views or what I feel in my heart, right or wrong," Alessandro Borgognone, Nakazawa’s owner, told Washingtonian magazine.
