Shift of Israel policy, The resolution would have marked the biggest international step on the   peace process since the 1993 Oslo Accords, which saw the beginning of direct negotiations between Israel and the PLO. It would also have marked a dramatic departure from decades of US foreign policy, which has held that peace will come only through negotiations. But the peace process has stalled in recent years. The last round of   talks, initiated by Kerry, collapsed in April 2014, with both sides blaming each other.
