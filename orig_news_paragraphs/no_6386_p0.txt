Whitlock says that   sports blog Deadspin caused ESPN to veer left, and that was the seed that caused the network’s downfall. [Deadspin, a popular but   website that forms part of the Gawker chain of sites, has targeted ESPN for political correction for several years with a long list of posts aimed at forcing ESPN to toe the political line of the extreme progressive left.
