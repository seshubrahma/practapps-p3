Further complicating matters for Senate GOP leaders is Sen. Rand Paul,   who announced this week he plans to vote against the Obamacare repeal legislation because the underlying budget measure it is attached to doesn’t balance and adds to the deficit. If Murkowski, Collins and Paul all voted against the budget bill, it would be enough to torpedo the Obamacare repeal legislation. " I’m going to wait and see what happens," Collins told reporters, indicating she thinks it’s too early to decide how she will vote on the bill.
