And in addition, the right has further understood that when governments and corporations get in bed together, the resulting "crony capitalism" can be costly to both consumers and taxpayers. Yet now the right must come to a further realization: Crony capitalism can deprive the people of more than money it can deprive them of their freedom. And in the course of this suppression of liberty, companies can be just as guilty —  or even guiltier —  than the government.   To be sure, it’s almost always the government that has the muscle and the weapons to put the literal iron in the iron fist.
