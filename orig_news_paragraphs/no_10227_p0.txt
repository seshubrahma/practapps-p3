Police in Pittsburgh, PA say a Hillary Clinton supporter lit a flag on fire near a group of Donald Trump supporters and attacked a man Saturday near a   event, according to CBS report.   Per the criminal report, Joshua Sturman was in the area to support Hillary Clinton, who held a rally nearby. Trump supporters gathered across the street, in what police say was a peaceful protest. Sturman walked over to the group of Trump supporters, who were standing    and then pulled out a flag and began to set it on fire.
