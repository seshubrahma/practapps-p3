Cruz’s widely acknowledge success winning delegates through local and state Republican conventions shows deep support within the party’s most energetic activists. Trump and his supporters repeatedly decried his failure to win over local and state delegates as evidence of a "rigged" system. The system isn’t rigged in any meaningful way, but it is dominated by a small number of the most committed Republicans who shoulder almost all the    operations that fuel the party. The activists who regularly attend   meetings and dutifully attend city, county and state conventions regularly are the   base of the Republican party.
