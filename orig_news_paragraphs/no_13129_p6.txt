Unsurprisingly, most polling reflected Trump’s wide lead in the Granite State, although a Gravis Marketing poll seriously underestimated his support, showing him just 11 points up in its final poll. The New Hampshire polls struggled, though, in handicapping the race for second and third. In general, the polls seriously overestimated support for Sen. Marco Rubio and underestimated support of Ohio Governor John Kasich. Kasich’s support was also seriously underestimated in Vermont, which voted on Super Tuesday.
