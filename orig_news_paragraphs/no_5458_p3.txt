ADL’s center on cyberhate, technology, and society will pioneer new strategies in the fight against online abuse, drawing from ADL’s three decades of expertise monitoring, tracking, and combating hate on the public and private web. It will author reports and data provide insights to government and policy makers and expose and stop specific cases of online harassment and cyberbullying.
