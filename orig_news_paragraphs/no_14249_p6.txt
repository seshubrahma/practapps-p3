How long have they been there? According to an Australian government report, the average length of time refugees spend in detention is 469 days, and steadily increasing.   Conditions in the camps are very poor, which has resulted in immense international pressure on successive Australian governments to solve the issue, though domestic support remains high.   In October Amnesty International accused Canberra of turning Nauru into an "  prison," and human rights abuses have been documented at the centers.
