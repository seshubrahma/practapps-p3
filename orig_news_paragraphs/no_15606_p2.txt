He arrived in the Middle East on Saturday and in contrast to the controversies and questions he’s facing in the U. S. President Trump received a royal welcome in Saudi Arabia, and it wasn’t just because he was greeted by the nation’s king, Salman bin Abdulaziz al Saud.   Red carpet, brass bands, American flags, billboard with the two leaders’ faces, images of pomp and respect were all over Riyadh, Saudi Arabia’s capital.
