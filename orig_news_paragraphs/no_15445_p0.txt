 (CNN) Though many individuals likely contributed to the President ’s decision to fire FBI Director James Comey, one notable one may have been Roger Stone, a    close friend of Donald Trump and central figure in the FBI investigation into Trump campaign ties to Russia.   In the middle of the Watergate scandal, Stone, who engaged in dirty tricks during Richard Nixon’s 1972 campaign, was discovered to have hired a Republican operative to infiltrate the George McGovern campaign and was subsequently fired from his job.
