" School choice" is a term to describe the provision of scholarships to poor children who are trapped in inner city public schools where year in and year out nearly half of them fail to graduate and the half who do are functionally illiterate and unable to function in a modern economy. These scholarships or "vouchers" would allow poor children to take the tuition that taxpayers already provide, and use it in a school, private or religious, that would actually teach them.
