Aerial banners bearing the message "Rescue UNborn Children" and a 15’ x 100’ photo of a baby aborted at 15 weeks will be flying over Cleveland, Ohio when the Republican National Convention gathers next week. [The #OperationRNC campaign  —   whose goal is to have the Republican National Convention defund Planned Parenthood in its 2016 platform   —   will fly its banners over downtown Cleveland July 13 to 21 for three hours daily, weather permitting.   Mark Harrington, national director of Created Equal, is leading #OperationRNC.
