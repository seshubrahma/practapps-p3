The S. C. House GOP poll, conducted between February 18 and 19, shows Trump has a   lead over second place Ted Cruz  34 percent to 19 percent. This is a one point drop from the   lead Trump had in the SC House GOP poll conducted on February 17. More significantly, the Augusta Chronicle poll conducted between February 18 and February 19 shows that Trump has only a   lead over second place Marco Rubio, 27 percent to 24 percent.
