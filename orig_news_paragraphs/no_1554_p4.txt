According to the July report, which used Sanitation Department data from 2013, only about a quarter of aluminum cans  —   one of the most valuable recyclable metals  —   makes it into the right bin. And only around 5 percent of our plastic cups, plates and utensils goes to the right place. We do the best job recycling glass, but as it turns out, that’s one of the least valuable types of commodities, Mr. Huber said
