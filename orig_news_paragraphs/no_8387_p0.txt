GOP presidential candidate Sen. Rand Paul ( ) is tweaking fellow candidate Sen. Ted Cruz over whether the Texan is eligible to be President of the United States. Cruz was born in Canada. ["You know, I think without question he is qualified and would make the cut to be prime minister of Canada, absolutely without question, he is qualified and he meets the qualifications," Paul said about Cruz during a radio interview on Kilmeade and Friends.   Cruz was born in Canada, but his mother was a U. S. citizen.
