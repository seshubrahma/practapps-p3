President Trump held a meeting with conservative leaders on February 1 in which he called on NRA Senior Vice President Wayne LaPierre to guide the leaders in a way that secures confirmation of Supreme Court nominee Neil Gorsuch. [Trump’s meeting with LaPierre and leaders from some of the "most influential conservative groups in the nation" came one day after he nominated Gorsuch to fill the seat left vacant by the death of Antonin Scalia.   NRATV’s Grant Stinchfield covered the meeting, wherein Trump thanked the NRA for supporting him during the election, then designated LaPierre to guide both the NRA and the various conservative groups toward confirmation.
