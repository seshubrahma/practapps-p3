The Federal Reserve is often spoken about as if it possesses some divine intelligence. Reminiscent of the old EF Hutton commercials, the CNBC crowd behaves similarly, falling all over themselves to dissect every word for some hidden message or insight —  when, in fact, there isn’t one. Most of the Fed’s statements can be best surmised by Shakespeare: It is a tale. Told by an idiot, full of sound and fury, Signifying nothing.
