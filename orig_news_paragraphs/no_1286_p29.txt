Chivers, The New York Times Mr. Chivers, 52, spent months crafting his   portrait of a young combat veteran haunted by his experiences in Afghanistan, who was imprisoned after a violent fight with a stranger. Unflinching yet empathetic, the reporting by Mr. Chivers  —   himself a former Marine  —    prompted the state of Illinois to vacate the veteran’s jail sentence.
