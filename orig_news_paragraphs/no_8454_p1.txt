[The activists came from as far as Washington State to register their continued support for Sanders   —   and their disgust at the Democratic National Committee (DNC) in the wake of the release of emails suggesting that DNC leaders colluded to help Hillary Clinton defeat Sanders in the primary race.   Sporting "Bernie for President"   signs, and buttons, and carrying signs and American flags, the activists dominated the Trenton line train, with new demonstrators clambering on, to cheers, at each stop along the way.
