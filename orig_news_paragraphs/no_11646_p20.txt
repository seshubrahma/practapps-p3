The White House released the text of Obama’s State of the Union speech. Sen. Jeff Sessions ( ) responds to Obama’s State of the Union speech. President Obama’s policy agenda on trade, crime, immigration, spending and debt all have one common feature: they make life harder for working Americans and put the country at needless risk. With wages down, record numbers not working, and crime rising in cities across the U. S. the next person to occupy the oval office will have to chart a dramatically different course.
