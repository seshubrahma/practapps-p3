In September, Johnson was asked about the the Syrian city of Aleppo where so many have died in fighting between Syrian government forces and insurgents. Johnson was stumped by the question and tried to make light of it by saying in a bemused manner, "And what is Aleppo?" Then a few weeks later, Johnson joked that he was having another "Aleppo moment" when he could not name any world leaders he admired.
