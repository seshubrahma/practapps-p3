Between the midterms’ track record and the polarizing health care legislation, this week’s events would seem to be a recipe for political disaster for the GOP.   Democrats, on the other hand, see a silver lining in the House vote. They are hoping that TrumpCare will become the Republican ObamaCare, producing the same flip of control of the House that Democrats suffered in 2010. According to David Wasserman of the Cook Report, one of the shrewdest political observers around, the vote "guarantees Democrats will have at least one major    vote to exploit in the next elections."
