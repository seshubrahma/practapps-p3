TEL AVIV  —    U. S. Jewish groups hailed President Donald Trump’s opening remarks in his address to Congress slamming the recent spate of   acts of "hate and evil. " ["Recent threats targeting Jewish community centers and vandalism of Jewish cemeteries, as well as last week’s shooting in Kansas City, remind us that while we may be a nation divided on policies, we are a country that stands united in condemning hate and evil in all of its very ugly forms," the president said at the joint session to wide applause.
