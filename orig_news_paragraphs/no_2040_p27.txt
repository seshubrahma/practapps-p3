Linda McMahon, a pro wrestling impresario, put $1 million into Future45, a   "super PAC," in the final stages of the presidential campaign, taking her total contribution to the organization to $7 million. This week, Ms. McMahon  —   who lost twice in recent years as a Republican candidate for the United States Senate in Connecticut   —   was chosen by Mr. Trump to head the Small Business Administration. Peter Thiel, the Silicon Valley billionaire who took down Gawker, gave $1 million to Make America Number 1, an   "super PAC" run by the Mercer family, hedge fund billionaires.
