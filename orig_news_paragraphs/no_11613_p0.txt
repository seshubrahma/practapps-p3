After spending six months in a coordinated effort to grind Donald Trump into dust, the billionaire businessman’s impressive second place showing in Iowa, first place triumph in New Hampshire, and competitiveness with Hillary Clinton in a general election, has apparently resulted in a change of tactics: The media is now moving its attacks from Trump to his supporters.   The fact that three major mainstream publications have engaged in this tactic in less than 24 hours, makes it pretty clear that this new approach is just as coordinated as the previous attacks on Trump himself. Tuesday night, after Trump’s   New Hampshire victory, the Huffington Post lashed out directly at Trump supporters as "racist sexist xenophobes. "
