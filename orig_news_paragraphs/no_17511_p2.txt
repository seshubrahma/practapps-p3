Latinos gathered in social media circles to condemn, plot and retaliate against Trump with such fervor in the past two weeks that they caused three multibillion dollar media companies to back away from him: Univision, NBC and Televisa.   And on Wednesday, Macy’s announced that it was removing Trump merchandising from its stores after 700, 000 people signed a MoveOn. org petition.
