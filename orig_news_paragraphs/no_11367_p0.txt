Hillary Clinton campaigned at an Pennsylvania area church Sunday morning, reminding attendees that the founders’ vision of America was initially imperfect. ["Here in America, when our founders said all men are created equal, well they left out African Americans, they left out women, they left out a lot of us, but that didn’t stop generations of Americans from pushing forward, making clear we were not satisfied with just half measures," she said.   Clinton spoke from the pulpit to a largely African American congregation of Mount Airy Church of God in Christ, in Philadelphia. "
