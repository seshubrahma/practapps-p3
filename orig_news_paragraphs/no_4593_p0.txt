Tech leaders across Silicon Valley have attacked President Trump for his recent decision to pull the U. S. out of the Paris Climate Accord. [Mark Zuckerberg, CEO of social media giant Facebook, was one of many tech elites to speak out against President Trump’s decision to leave the Paris Climate Accord. " Withdrawing from the Paris climate agreement is bad for the environment, bad for the economy, and it puts our children’s future at risk. " wrote the billionaire CEO in a public post on Facebook.
