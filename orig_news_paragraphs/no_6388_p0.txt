Donations for the mentally disabled victim who was tied up, beaten, and tortured during a Facebook Live broadcast in Chicago reached over $80, 000 on the fundraising website GoFundMe. [The GoFundMe campaign, which was called "Let’s show the Chicago victim some love," had raised over $44, 000 Friday morning and reached over $80, 000 from more than 2, 800 donors as of Saturday morning, WXIN reported.   GoFundMe ’s regional communications manager Bartlett Jackson told the Associated Press the account is legitimate and that its creator has been working directly with the victim’s family.
