It rules out the possibility that the TPP could lead to a higher unemployment rate as a result of increased imports displacing U. S. workers. The ITC analysis also failed to include the negative growth effects of stronger and longer copyright and patent protection. Both of these are forms of protection which translate into higher prices for drugs, software, and other protected products. The losses from this increased protectionism can easily exceed the projected gains from the tariff reductions in the TPP.
