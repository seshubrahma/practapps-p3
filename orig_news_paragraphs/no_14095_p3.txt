Here’s what all that means: first, the Trans Pacific Partnership, or TPP, it’s a controversial international trade deal involving 12 countries located around the Pacific Rim.   It’s supposed to encourage more trade, more business between them, partly by getting rid of tariffs, taxes on certain goods that countries import. Former President Barack Obama who helped negotiate the TPP said it’d be good for American workers.   But critics and some U. S. lawmakers disagreed.
