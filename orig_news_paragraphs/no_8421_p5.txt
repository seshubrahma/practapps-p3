Like its counterpart in the U. S. Mexico’s powerful ruling establishment has condemned Mr. Trump’s proposal. The reason is simple: the PRI and Mexico’s lavish insider network of politicians, businessmen, and financiers are one and the same, and their unique partnership has made many Mexican politicians and their favored allies some of the richest men in the world.
