Silverman: ’Ridiculous’ remark was unscripted, But Sanders’ own delegates were as angry as ever Tuesday morning, just hours after he implored them to support Clinton in his   speech. Sanders delegates in California booed down speakers, including Clinton supporter Rep. Xavier Becerra. At one point in Becerra’s brief talk, some delegates hopped on tables with Sanders signs, while other delegates pounded loudly on the tables in unison. As of early Tuesday morning, it did not look like even Sanders would be able to sway his supporters.
