Mr. Spicer, whose tenure as President Trump’s chief spokesman has made him a television sensation and   punch line, calls himself his own worst critic. And while he said "it’s an honor" to serve as press secretary, he also marveled to Ms. Van Susteren about the level of scrutiny that the role entails. " No matter what you do, what you wear, it gets amplified to a degree that you couldn’t imagine," Mr. Spicer said.
