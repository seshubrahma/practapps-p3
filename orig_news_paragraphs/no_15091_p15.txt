Speaking from Lucca on Monday, Johnson said the western powers were working to give Tillerson "the clearest possible mandate" to enter into talks with Russia on Syria from a position of strength. The Trump administration has found itself increasingly at loggerheads with Putin’s government over the past month, as expectations of a second relationship "reset" dim.
