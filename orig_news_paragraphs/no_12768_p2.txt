Weiner was the subject of an infamous 2011 scandal, first broken by Andrew Breitbart, involving his habit of sending racy pictures of himself to women he met online. The story might have died after a few days had Weiner not alleged that he had been "hacked," which implied that Breitbart had done the hacking. Then, in 2013, Weiner abandoned his mayoral campaign  —   which had some real prospect of succeeding   —   when new allegations that he had not quite kicked his social media habits.
