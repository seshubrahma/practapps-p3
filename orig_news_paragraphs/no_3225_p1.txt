Executive Director Bob Dane explains that Trump’s potential   immigration overhaul would mean an end to the current system which favors immigrants who already have family in the U. S.:   Today, the U. S. remains a meritocracy in many respects, but not in one notable area: its immigration system. This is why President Donald Trump’s call for an immigration policy overhaul ― in favor of applicants whose skills and talents are most likely to benefit the country ― is so important.
