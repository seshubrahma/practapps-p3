Texas Mayor Beth Van Duyne announced Monday she accepted the Trump Administration position of Regional Administrator for the U. S. Department of Housing and Urban Development’s (HUD) Southwest Region VI. [The announcement follows much speculation the now former Irving mayor would join the administration. Based out of HUD’s Fort Worth Regional Office, Van Duyne will oversee department programs and operations for Texas, Oklahoma, New Mexico, Arkansas, and Louisiana.   Van Duyne said she was honored to serve in the position, which reports to HUD Secretary Dr. Ben Carson.
