On April 21, media outlets claimed that Donald Trump supported the progressive campaign to eliminate   bathrooms. In fact, Trump backs the current     policy favored by most Americans, in which they willingly tolerate the quiet use of normal bathrooms by people of the opposite sex. " There’s a big move to create new bathrooms   …     for transgender [people]   …     that would be discriminatory in a certain way [and] that would be unbelievably expensive for businesses in the country," Trump said April 21.
