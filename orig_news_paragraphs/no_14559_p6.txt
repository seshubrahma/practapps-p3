Trump’s policies have the potential to massively increase the number of undocumented immigrants held in detention and processed for deportation. One of Trump’s key focuses has been ending what he calls "catch and release," where undocumented immigrants who are apprehended and processed are released into the US on parole pending further court proceedings. It’s a way to alleviate the strain on a backlogged immigration court system and detention centers.   But Trump has vowed to end that policy, meaning the government will need a place to put people.
