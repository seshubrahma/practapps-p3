That none of the Beltway lifers in "Veep" believe in anything is not exactly unrealistic, but it’s also oddly comforting. They may be the worst, but at least they lack all conviction. For the dangers of passionate intensity, watch "Game of Thrones," which returns for its sixth season an hour and a half earlier on Sunday. Beyond its derrières and dragons, "Thrones" has been an astute study of power politics and flexible alliances.
