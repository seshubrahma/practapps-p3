Trump offered the vice presidential slot to Indiana Gov. Mike Pence Wednesday, CNN has learned, and the Indiana governor accepted. Sources said that Trump’s decision to pick Pence over more controversial candidates such as New Jersey Gov. Chris Christie followed a sustained    lobbying push orchestrated from within the family’s inner circle. After building a presidential campaign almost exclusively on the strength of his own persona and often seeming to make decisions on the fly, Trump’s willingness to listen to others while agonizing over his most important political move yet does not just suggest a candidate who is evolving politically. It shows a man who is not used to being told he is wrong and who makes an exception for the opinions of those who are his own   .
