Because we never attempted to write a political manifesto, we were surprised by the book’s popularity among certain crusaders on both the left and the right. When "The Fourth Turning" came out, our biggest partisan fans were Democrats, who saw in our description of an emerging "Millennial generation" (a term we coined) the sort of   optimists who would pull America toward progressive ideals.
