Washington (CNN) The race for the Republican nomination for President is more wide open now than perhaps ever before, according to a new Quinnipiac University poll, with five GOP hopefuls all tied with the same amount of support. Retired neurosurgeon Ben Carson has joined the top tier of Republican aspirants, joining Wisconsin Gov. Scott Walker, Florida Sen. Marco Rubio and former governors Jeb Bush of Florida and Mike Huckabee of Arkansas with 10% of the vote in a national survey released Thursday.
