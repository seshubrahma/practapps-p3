CBP reports that of the 218, 111 OTM apprehensions along the Mexican border, 140, 496 occurred in the Texas Rio Grande Valley Sector. The UAC apprehensions consisted of minors from El Salvador, Guatemala, Honduras, and Mexico. Breitbart Texas reported a Department of Homeland Security (DHS) report showing an over 600 percent increase in Haitian immigrants who are flooding into southern California seeking refugee status. Because of this increase, DHS Secretary Jeh Johnson reinstated the process of removing certain Haitian immigrants.
