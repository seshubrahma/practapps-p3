From his behavior so far, it seems that Trump thinks a big part of his job will be to examine and adjudicate particular companies’ behavior and to decide what’s allowed and what’s fair on a situational basis, not just on a policy one. Without hyperventilating over the term, this means that in one small way he wants to operate like a king. This brings up an important difference between a king’s court and an administration. An administration is made of public policies, and of the politics by which policies live or die.
