If Cruz captures Indiana, however, Trump will face a steep challenge in winning a majority of delegates before the convention. The conservative Club for Growth recently announced a $1. 5 million ad buy in Indiana to oppose Trump’s candidacy. It is likely that other organizations that oppose Trump will also flood the Indiana airwaves in an attempt to block Trump. Most of these organizations largely are ignoring next week’s primaries, since they are being held in a region where Trump has run very strong.
