The Running Man didn’t predict the truths of 2017 —  far from it. What it did do, though, is anticipate the fact that truth itself might be a matter of political controversy in the United States. The film understood the ways that celebrity and politics would mingle and muddle this year, in ways both harmlessly entertaining and potentially dangerous for democracy. In 1987’s version of 2017, the president has a theatrical agent, and the state exerts control with the help of a reality show.
