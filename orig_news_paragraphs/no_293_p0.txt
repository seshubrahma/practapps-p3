We’re resurfacing this article in light of President Trump’s recent comments on Nafta. After calling it the "worst trade deal," he seems to be softening his stance.   It was a deal that created a deeply intertwined economy stretching from the Arctic to Central America and a generation of mostly warm relations between the United States and its two neighbors. It has also contributed to the loss of manufacturing jobs in the United States and added to   discontent in the communities that lost them.
