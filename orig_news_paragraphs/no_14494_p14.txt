Electronic warfare, such as attacks on computer systems and internet communications, is a tool of growing importance, and has been used to great effect in Ukraine.   Russia realizes that the Western militaries have a substantial technological edge, but in the words of the Chief of the Russian General Staff Valery Gerasimov, the enemy "will always have vulnerabilities. " In 2013, Gerasimov said the use of "political, economic, informational, humanitarian, and other   measures" now exceeded the power of weapons in some cases.
