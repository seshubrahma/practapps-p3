Soon afterward, on April 7, 1999, Putin went on TV himself to claim the tape authentic —   that the "man who looked like" Skuratov was indeed Skuratov —  and called not only for Skuratov’s resignation, but for a more robust criminal investigation. All this is noteworthy not only because this was one of Putin’s key steps toward the presidential throne, but because this dark and convoluted chapter of contemporary Russian history is also, however amazingly, now relevant reading for understanding contemporary American history. Now that Buzzfeed has released a dossier compiled by a private intelligence company, with unverified allegations that the FSB has a video of Donald Trump with prostitutes in the Moscow Ritz Carlton in 2013, America has entered uniquely Russian territory.
