The four years of the Pence administration in Indiana    a package of tax cuts, expansion of Medicaid, conservative education reforms and more    have all had Karen Pence’s influence at varying levels. " It was pretty customary for Mike to get kind of down to the end and say, ’I’m gonna sleep on it, pray about it and speak to Karen,’ " said one ally, who described her integral role in shaping policy. The Pence ally spoke on condition of anonymity because few people talk openly about her role.
