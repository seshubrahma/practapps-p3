Drain the swamp or reward the donors?                  Rewarding big campaign donors with plum ambassador slots is a   bipartisan tradition. And despite his "drain the swamp" campaign mantra about shaking up Washington, there are early indications that Trump and his administration will follow the norm.                  Julie Pace of The Associated Press shares reporting about how big GOP donors, including some who were at first reluctant to join Team Trump, are now angling for an overseas stint in, say, Paris, Rome or London.
