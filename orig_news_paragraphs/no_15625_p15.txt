The injection of terror into Trump’s first foreign trip is an echo of several episodes during President Barack Obama’s international travels, when attacks regularly intruded into the message he was hoping to bring abroad. Obama was just departing for a    swing through Turkey and Asia when the 2015 terror spree in Paris unfolded. He visited the French capital weeks later for a climate summit and laid a white rose at the Bataclan theater, where one of the massacres occurred. A few months later, a bomb attack at the Brussels airport interrupted Obama’s historic visit to Cuba.
