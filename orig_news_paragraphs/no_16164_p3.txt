In his quintessential style, Trump diverted repeatedly from his speech introducing Pence to hail his own achievements in winning the Republican nomination. He seemed to spend as much time crowing about his own qualities    including predictions that there would be more terror attacks in Europe for instance    as about the record of Pence himself and his suitability to be one heartbeat away from the presidency. The event took place after a chaotic vice presidential selection process, that included an   period of late night second guessing by Trump on Thursday about whether he had made the right choice, multiple sources have told CNN. Trump sought to refute such reporting.
