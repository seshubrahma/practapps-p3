White House Press Secretary Sean Spicer continued fielding questions about President Donald Trump’s Twitter account, reminding the press that it was an effective tool for Trump during his campaign for president. ["Does he sometimes cross the line where his tweets become the news, become the agenda, and actually impedes the ability to get things done?" asked Fox News White House correspondent John Roberts during Tuesday’s White House press briefing, pointing out that "using it and using it wisely are two different things.
