New hopes for Keystone?   Like Dakota Access, the Keystone XL Pipeline had been the subject of environmental concern from activists, residents and indigenous tribes who worried that the pipeline would pollute as many as 2, 500 aquifers.   But pipeline supporters touted the jobs it would create and other economic benefits.   The $8 billion Keystone XL Pipeline was proposed to stretch nearly 1, 200 miles across six states, shuttling   petroleum from Canada to the Gulf Coast.
