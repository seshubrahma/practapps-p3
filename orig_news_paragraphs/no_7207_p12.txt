As Breitbart Tech revealed in February, Twitter deliberately manipulates its algorithm to favour a trusted "whitelist" of news sources, while pushing sources on a disfavoured "blacklist" down users’ search results. It’s hard to imagine that Facebook, whose CEO’s rabid progressivism easily matches that of Twitter’s Jack Dorsey, won’t one day go down the same route. After all, as UCLA law professor Eugene Volokh reminded Gizmodo, there are no legal obstacles in Zuckerberg’s way. He could, if he wished, be as biased as he liked in his management of Facebook.
