Then Friday, a more welcome surprise for refugee groups: A federal judge in Washington reversed several key provisions of the executive order, paving the way some refugees to enter the country.   Amid the celebration, however, there is also concern and trepidation about how long the ruling will hold, and how many refugees will actually be admitted. " I feel like our whole world has been turned " Danielle Drake, community relations manager at the Cleveland nonprofit US Together, told CNN Saturday.
