Exactly what is the New START Treaty? Russia and the US signed the New Strategic Arms Reduction Treaty in 2010. The agreement, an upgrade on the 1991 START, commits both countries to scaling back their nuclear warheads by 2018. Both countries are required to restrict the number of deployed warheads to 1, 550 across 700 delivery systems, including intercontinental ballistic missiles,   ballistic missiles and bombers.
