The bill, titled the Synthetics Trafficking and Overdose Prevention, or the "STOP Act," is being championed by Ohio Republican Sen. Rob Portman. Ridge, a senior advisor with the   group "Americans for Securing All Packages" (ASAP) is working to halt the spread of the deadly opioid epidemic ravaging American families. Fentanyl is usually 50 to 100 times stronger than heroin —  dousing the body’s opioid receptors in the brain stem, spine, and gut all at once, obliterating pain and potentially triggering an overdose. One quarter of a milligram of fentanyl can kill a person.
