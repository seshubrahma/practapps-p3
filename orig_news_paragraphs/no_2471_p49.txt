An opponent, Gary Clisham, told the student newspaper that Mr. Bannon had "immense charisma" but predicted that he would get nothing done. After graduation, Mr. Bannon joined the Navy, attended Officer Candidate School and served two deployments aboard the Foster, a destroyer that took him all over Asia and into the Persian Gulf. His basketball style  —   running the length of the court without passing   —   earned him a sarcastic nickname,    said Mr. Masso, his shipmate.
