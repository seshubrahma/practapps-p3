Boston Herald:   Amid Trump Surge, 20, 000 Massachusetts Democrats Left Party, Became Independents or Republicans: Nearly 20, 000 Bay State Democrats have fled the party this winter, with thousands doing so to join the Republican ranks, according to the state ’s top elections official. Secretary of State William Galvin said more than 16, 300 Democrats have shed their party affiliation and become independent voters since Jan. 1, while nearly 3, 500 more shifted to the MassGOP ahead of tomorrow’s "Super Tuesday" presidential primary.
