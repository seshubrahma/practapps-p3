Not long after the family moved to Beverly Hills, Calif. when Ms. Haskell was 13, her father died of a heart attack and her mother went to work. Ms. Haskell soldiered on. At Beverly Hills High School, she was head cheerleader and was voted "Biggest Flirt" in her senior year. In the Los Angeles of the 1960s, she won a twist contest judged by Bob Hope and Joan Collins.
