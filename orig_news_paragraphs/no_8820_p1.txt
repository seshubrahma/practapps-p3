Since the first regular day of the New Year, January 4th, Bush has climbed more than 3 points while Rubio has lost 1 point.   Donald Trump continues to lead the GOP field with 39 percent support, down slightly for the week, but still far ahead of the other Republican candidates. Ted Cruz remains in second with 14. 5 percent support, up three points since the first of the year. Ben Carson is 4th with 9. 6 percent support, continuing his drop from the end of 2015.
