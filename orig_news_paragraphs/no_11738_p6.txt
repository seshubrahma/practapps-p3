The Fake   Cap, The media almost universally reports that the federal government has set a 65, 000 or 85, 000 annual cap on the annual number of incoming     professionals. Here’s the secret  —   the   visas given to university hires don’t count against the 85, 000 annual cap, according to a 2006 memo approved by George W. Bush’s administration. Basically, universities are free to hire as many   as they like, anytime in the year, for any job that requires a college degree.
