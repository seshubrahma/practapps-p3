Yes, the Republicans won big in the 2010 and 2014 midterm elections on an   message.   Yet at the same time, Barack Obama and the Democrats won big in 2008 on a     platform, and in 2012, Obama won big a second time, defending, of course, his 2010 enactment of Obamacare. Yes, it’s true that after the 2016 elections, Republicans could claim they had a national mandate to repeal Obama’s signature program.   And yet at the same time, Democrats could just as rightly claim that they still had their mandate to protect Obamacare.
