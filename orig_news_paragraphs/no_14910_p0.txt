Austin, Texas (CNN ) CNN caught up with RB duo Chloe x Halle at the SXSW music festival in Austin, Texas, where the Atlanta natives opened up about their passion for women’s rights, the power of music and their relationship with their mentor, Beyoncé. Describing music as a "universal language," Chloe Bailey, 18, said that regardless of the color of a person’s skin or their gender, "everyone understands feelings and emotion. "
