According to CBP data, the 40% drop in illegal Southwest border crossings from January to February is far outside normal seasonal trends. Typically, the January to February change is actually an increase of 10% to 20%. The drop breaks a nearly   trend, as CBP data going back to 2000 shows an uptick in apprehensions every February.   The number of apprehensions and inadmissible individuals presenting at the border was 18, 762 people in February, down from 31, 578 in January.
