The first thing to note about this statement is that it’s technically an impossible commitment for Facebook to make. The company has long switched from   newsfeeds (where ordinary people choose what information they come into contact with) to an algorithmic model, in which Facebook controls what content gets pushed to its users (and when). If Facebook’s algorithms think a news story is interesting to you, they’ll make sure you see it.
