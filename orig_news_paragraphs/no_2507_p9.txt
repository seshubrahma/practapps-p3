Though Mr. Trump prevailed in the Electoral College, he did so thanks to the slimmest of margins in three states   —   Michigan, Pennsylvania and Wisconsin   —   and he lost the popular vote nationally by nearly three million votes. With the country growing more urban and racially diverse, Democrats like their chances in the 2020 presidential race, when nonwhite voters are likely to make up more than 30 percent of the electorate for the first time in history.
