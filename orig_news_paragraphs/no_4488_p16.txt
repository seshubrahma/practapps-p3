Normally you’d expect an organization set up to support a politician and his agenda to close up shop after that candidate leaves office, but not Obama’s OFA. Rather, it’s gearing up for battle, with a growing war chest and more than 250 offices across the country. Meanwhile, Politico recently profiled the   Indivisible, reporting the group has been aided by MoveOn. org and the ACLU. The associations between the   Indivisible and the   MoveOn. org and ACLU may be telling since the latter two groups have been central to recent efforts to stop Trump.
