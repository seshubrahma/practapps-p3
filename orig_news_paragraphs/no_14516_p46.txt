The Organization for the Prohibition of Chemical Weapons announces that Syria has destroyed all its declared chemical weapons production facilities.   November 25, 2013    The UN announces that starting January 22 in Geneva, Switzerland, the Syrian government and an unknown number of opposition groups will meet at a "Geneva II" conference meant to broker an end to the Syrian civil war. December 2, 2013   UN High Commissioner for Human Rights Navi Pillay says that a UN   team has found "massive evidence" that the highest levels of the Syrian government are responsible for war crimes.
