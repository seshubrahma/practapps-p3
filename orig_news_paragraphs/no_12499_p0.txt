An Arizona Republican senator, who was most adamantly opposed to   Donald J. Trump, is in for a tough reelection campaign in 2018, according to a Remington Research Group poll. The poll was conducted Nov. 15 and Nov. 16 with 1, 122 likely Republican primary voters in that state and carries a 2. 93 percent margin of error.
