COLUMBIA, South Carolina  —    The campaign of Sen. Ted Cruz ( ) issued a blistering rebuke at Sen. Marco Rubio ( ) the     frontrunner here in South Carolina, after it has come to light Rubio repeatedly skipped vote after vote after vote on important bills related to national security and defense policy.
