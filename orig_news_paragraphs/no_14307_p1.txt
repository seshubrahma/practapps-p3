Here’s how it unfolded: January 27: Trump signs an executive order banning nationals from seven   nations, including Iran, from entering the United States for 90 days and suspending all refugee admission for 120 days. January 28: Iran’s Foreign Ministry slams the ban as "an obvious insult to the Islamic world and in particular to the great nation of Iran" and says that it "will be recorded in history as a big gift to extremists and their supporters. "  Iran threatens reciprocal measures through legal, consular and diplomatic actions.
