In FY 2016, more than 12, 500 Syrian refugees were resettled in the United States. In the previous four fiscal years, about 2, 300 Syrian refugees were resettled in the country. California led the way with 40 Syrian refugees resettled in the first five days of FY 2017, followed by Michigan (39) Texas (33) Arizona (3O) Maryland (21) and New York (20) Georgia (14) and Nevada (12).
