The actors’ comments were, on the one hand, extremely typical of awards shows, whose rituals tend to merge Hollywood’s sense of its own cultural significance with a passing awareness of the struggles of the world beyond Wilshire Boulevard. Talk of politics —   Sean Penn on Proposition 8, Jared Leto on Ukraine, Patricia Arquette on equal pay —  is extremely common on the stages of those shows, as celebrities use their cultural influence to effect political change.
