Handling people with   conditions, Trump and Republican members have repeatedly promised that they would continue to protect people with   conditions. It’s among Obamacare’s most popular provisions, as protesters reminded lawmakers in town halls around the country. This protection, however, is also one of the main reasons why premiums rose under Obamacare. That’s why conservative House Freedom Caucus members pushed to weaken key provisions that require insurers to provide comprehensive coverage and ban them from charging consumers based on their medical histories.
