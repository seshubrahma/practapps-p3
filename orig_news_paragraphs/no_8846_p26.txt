There is no better answer than the flourishing of opportunity, which has been artificially suppressed by creeping statism for far too long.   Voluntary cooperation, in the pursuit of mutual profit, and vigorous competition have a way of dissolving prejudice.   It takes time, but patience is rewarded. Look at it this way: the Obama presidency brought us the ultimate example of the opposite viewpoint, which holds that imperfect citizens must be molded to virtue by the strong hand of an   State.
