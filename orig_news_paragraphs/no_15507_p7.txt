Asked about criticism that Republicans aren’t standing up to Trump, Scott disagreed and said they are "absolutely" putting country ahead of party at times. As an example, he brought up his joint statement with Sen. Marco Rubio against Trump’s initial travel ban executive order in January. The two Republican senators said they were "uneasy" with the ban, which barred citizens of seven   countries    Iraq, Syria, Iran, Libya, Somalia, Sudan and Yemen    from entering the US for 90 days, all refugees for 120 days and refugees from Syria indefinitely.
