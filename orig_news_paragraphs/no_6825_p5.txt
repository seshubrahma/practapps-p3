Interestingly, Hawaiians seem to regard homeless squatters from the mainland the way some Americans see illegal immigrants: the Hawaiian legislature perennially debates proposals to pay the homeless to leave the state permanently. Hawaii faces the same threat from the six designated   countries as any other U. S. point of entry.
