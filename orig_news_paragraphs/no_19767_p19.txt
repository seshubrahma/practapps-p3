Because of his role running his family’s   company, Kushner is deeply enmeshed with a variety of business interests that could conflict with his role in policymaking. A deeply reported Times story on Sunday looked at the Kushner companies and how they intersect with, among other things, Chinese national interests. Despite Trump’s belligerent words about China, a joint   venture in New Jersey was financed in part with a maneuver that allows major overseas investors, in this case many of them Chinese, to get U. S. visas. These connections could cause problems for Kushner.
