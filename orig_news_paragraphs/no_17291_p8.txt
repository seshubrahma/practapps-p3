Most Americans     57% of those surveyed    say they see "a lot" of discrimination against Muslims in society today. That’s higher than the 43% who see a lot of discrimination against gays and lesbians, 41% who see it against blacks, 32% who see it against Hispanics, 23% who see it against women, 15% who see it against evangelical Christians, 14% who see a lot of discrimination against whites and 13% who see it against Jews. Vice   Mike Pence’s favorability rating is underwater, with 39% saying they view him favorably compared to 42% unfavorably.
