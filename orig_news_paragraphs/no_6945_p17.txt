In his Senate testimony this month, Comey confirmed Lynch’s tarmac meeting with Clinton "capped" his decision to make public the results of the FBI probe into Clinton’s emails. Referring to the tarmac meet, Comey stated, "In an ultimately conclusive way, that’s the thing that capped it for me that I had to do something separately to protect the credibility of the investigation which meant both the FBI and the justice department.
