The signage outside the front of a West Texas plant and nursery store does not mince its words when it comes to how the business owner feels about the presumptive presidential Democrat nominee Hillary Rodham Clinton. It reads: "Crooked Hillary Would Be a Fucking Disaster! " [No asterisks, no redacting, no euphemistic use of the pretend word "effing" to mask where Slide Plant Market owner Mark Lee stands on the possibility that "Crooked Hillary" could be elected as the next president of the United States.   Since the sign went up Thursday, residents of the small unincorporated community of Slide, located in South Lubbock County, reacted strongly but not because Lee used the term "Crooked Hillary," coined by the presumptive GOP presidential nominee Donald Trump.
