Washington (CNN) Hillary Clinton’s odds of winning the presidency rose from 78% last week to 91% Monday before Election Day, according to CNN’s Political Prediction Market. Clinton’s odds have always been much greater than her opponent, Republican nominee Donald Trump, according to the prediction market, although they had dropped last week.   Her odds stood at 95% on October 20, but on October 26 dipped to 90% and then continued to decline daily until October 31.
