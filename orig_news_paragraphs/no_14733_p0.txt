 (CNN) Social conservatives opposed to women’s health, rights and autonomy now control the White House, both houses of Congress and, at the state level, most governors’ houses and legislatures. Even as these policymakers continue their assault on abortion rights, they are also poised to enact policy that will undercut US women’s access to family planning services.     The groundwork for this onslaught against   affordable contraceptive care is being laid in Congress and the Trump administration.
