" The 114th Congress returns to Washington this week for one big order of business   —   besides keeping the government’s lights on. In one of its final and most significant acts, the House on Wednesday is set to pass the 21st Century Cures Act, an ambitious and pricey initiative that would deregulate some forms of drugs and medical devices and would fund President Obama’s cancer "moonshot," Alzheimer’s research, regenerative medicine and efforts to address opioid addiction.
