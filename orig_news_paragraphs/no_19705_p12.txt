Chasing them around the state are Texas Rangers Marcus (Jeff Bridges) and Alberto (Gil Birmingham) a grouchy odd couple who gently, but persistently mock each other (Alberto ribs Marcus’s age, and Marcus makes casually, horribly racist jokes about Alberto’s Mexican and Native American heritage). The "wit" on display is often painful, but that’s part of Sheridan ’s point —  that the country’s divisions are drawn so deeply, Marcus’s insults are one of the only ways he can relate to his partner.
