Afghanistan War ’at its lowest point’ The war in Afghanistan has largely slipped from public consciousness, replaced by the conflict in Syria and ongoing tensions over North Korea, but there are still around 8, 400 US troops in the country engaged in counterterrorism operations.   Those troops are separate from a wider   effort to train, advise and assist the Afghan army and police force.   Testifying before the Senate Armed Services Committee in February, Nicholson, the commander, warned of a stalemate in Afghanistan.
