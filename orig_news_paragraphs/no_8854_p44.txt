Yet even now, the drug industry —  besmirched, bedraggled, and besieged as it is —  continues to render forth medical miracles.   In 2015, the Food and Drug Administration approved 45 new medicines, treating everything from cancer to cholesterol to clots to cystic fibrosis. Yes, each of these   counts as good news for some set of patients indeed, each is good news for the whole of the   body politic. Yet for all the wonders that pharma can create, we can still, nevertheless, observe that it’s not enough.
