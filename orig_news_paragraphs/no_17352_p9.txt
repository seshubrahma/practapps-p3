By the end of his career, Moon held almost every high school rushing record in the state of Georgia. As a senior in 1973, he rushed for 2, 501 yards, a record he held until it was broken by Herschel Walker, who later won the Heisman Trophy. " I’m not kidding when I say back in ’72 and ’73, people would come from all over just to see him play," said Lamb, who is now retired.
