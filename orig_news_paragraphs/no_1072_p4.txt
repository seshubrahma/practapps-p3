Mr. Schmidt said the White House would have to be "delusional" not to recognize the depths of the challenges for the new president. The question for Mr. Trump, he added, is: "Does he learn lessons about Washington   from this debacle? " The answer to that question is not yet clear. Since Inauguration Day, Mr. Trump has fired his national security adviser for lying, watched as courts twice blocked his travel bans on some   countries and fumed as the F. B. I. announced that it was investigating his associates for any ties to Russian meddling in the 2016 campaign.
