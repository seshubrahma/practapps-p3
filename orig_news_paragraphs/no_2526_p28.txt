Recipes are like heirlooms for many families there and flavors include chocolate and banana. Flour derived from orchid bulbs binds sahlab, a creamy Middle Eastern beverage. In Lithuania, poppy milk, made from poppy seeds steeped in water and honey, is served on Christmas Eve as part of the Twelve Dishes feast. The British tradition of wassailing in exchange for a bowl of hot mulled cider dates back centuries, as does eggnog.
