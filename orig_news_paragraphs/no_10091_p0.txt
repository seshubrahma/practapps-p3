Politico claimed Tuesday that presumptive Republican presidential nominee Donald Trump is not expanding the Republican party, despite record turnout in party primaries in 2016. [Politico’s Shane Goldmacher argues that despite Trump’s claim to have brought "millions and millions" of new voters into the GOP fold, "it’s just not true.
