The New York Times delegate count released on Wednesday, which does not yet include delegate allocations from Tuesday’s Missouri and Illinois primaries, shows Donald Trump with 621 of the 1, 237 delegates needed to win a majority of the 2, 472 first ballot votes to win the nomination at the convention.
