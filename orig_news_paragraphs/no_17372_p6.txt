In fact, acting as attorney general of Oklahoma, Pruitt is suing the EPA    the agency he soon could head    over Obama’s Clean Power Plan, which aims to reduce pollution deaths, clean up the air and prevent catastrophic warming. That plan is now in legal limbo. And Pruitt has described it as "an unlawful attempt to expand federal bureaucrats’ authority over states’ energy economies in order to shutter   power plants and eventually other sources of   generated electricity," according to the Tulsa World. He’s tried to make this a states’ rights issue.
