William Kristol, editor of The Weekly Standard, said the hostility toward Mr. Trump could be damaging if the Republican convention this summer gets out of hand or if his unusually high negative ratings end up hurting other Republicans on the ticket. " I think people are underestimating the degree to which you could see a crisis in the Republican Party," he said. Still, in some quarters, reconciliation between Mr. Trump and his onetime critics is underway.
