Dr. Leonid Petrov, Visiting Fellow at the Australian National University, notes that while Syria and North Korea are not ideologically aligned, both have "hereditary dynastical lines which control their respective countries with fear and repression. " Hafez   seized power in 1970 in a bloodless coup by military leaders, and for 29 years he dominated Syrian life and politics.
