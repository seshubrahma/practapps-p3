SARASOTA, Florida  —   Republican presidential nominee Donald Trump will spend the last day before Election Day campaigning in five key battleground states, using every moment and every last bit of strength remaining in an effort to win the 2016 election. [FLORIDA  —    Trump will begin on Thursday morning with a rally in Sarasota at the Sarasota Fairgrounds at 11:00 a. m Eastern Standard Time (EST).
