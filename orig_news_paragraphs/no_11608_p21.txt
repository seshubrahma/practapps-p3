—   Or Minnesota Man?   —    Convicted on Terrorism Charges, Guled Ali Omar, a Somalian born in a refugee camp in Kenya but later admitted into the United States as a refugee and subsequently granted citizenship by the U. S. government, was one of three "Minnesota Men" convicted in June of conspiring to join the Islamic State and "commit murder in Syria. " "Guled Ali Omar, Abdurahman Yasin Daud and Mohamed Abdihamid Farah were convicted by a federal jury today of conspiring to commit murder in Syria on behalf of the Islamic State of Iraq and the Levant (ISIL) and to provide material support to the designated foreign terrorist organization," the Justice Department announced on June 3.
