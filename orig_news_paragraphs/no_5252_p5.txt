Though not as dramatic a split, the two parties are also divided on whether the media favors one side in its political coverage, on how much trust they have in national news media, and whether or not national news organizations are doing a good job of keeping them informed. After Trump won the 2016 election, Pew surveyed voters about what news source they relied on and found: "When voters were asked to write in their ‘main source’ for election news,    Trump voters named Fox News. The next   main source among Trump voters, CNN, was named by only 8% of his voters. "
