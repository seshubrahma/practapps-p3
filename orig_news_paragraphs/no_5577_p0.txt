(AP) VATICAN CITY  —    Palestinian Authority President Mahmoud Abbas warned Saturday that peace could suffer if the incoming Trump administration goes ahead with plans to move the US embassy to Israel from Tel Aviv to Jerusalem. [Abbas made the comments as he inaugurated the Palestinian embassy to the Holy See following an audience with Pope Francis.   Speaking through an Italian translator, Abbas said he had only heard of the proposal by US   Donald Trump to move the embassy to Jerusalem, and couldn’t comment officially unless and until it happens.
