Polls in Virginia closed at 7:00 pm eastern time. On Election Day morning, Real Clear Politics (RCP) listed Virginia as a tossup, though Hillary Clinton led the RCP average of polls by 5 percent, with 47. 2 percent compared to Trump’s 42. 2 percent. In its "no tossup" electoral college map as of Election Day morning, RCP gave Virginia’s 13 electoral college votes to Hillary Clinton. 270 electoral college votes are required to win the presidency.
