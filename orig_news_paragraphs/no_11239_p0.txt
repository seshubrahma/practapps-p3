The days of Mexican drug cartels being able to operate with impunity on both sides of the southern border are numbered, said Republican Presidential Nominee Donald Trump. ["I have a message for the drug dealers, for the gang members and the criminal cartels, your days are numbered" Trump said during a boisterous rally in Ocala, Florida today.    "When we allow ICE and the Border Patrol agents and when we give them a big beautiful wall with a door in it, so people can come, we want them to come in, but they have to come in legally," Trump said.
