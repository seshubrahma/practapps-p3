Second, on trade. One of the hallmarks of Pence’s governorship has been to create vocational training in high school and for high school graduates to learn a trade or vocation leading to   manufacturing and   jobs. His trade missions to Japan and other nations have been to convince them to do business with Indiana and provide Hoosiers with   jobs. All this is consonant with Trump’s approach of focusing on American workers and seeking a resurgence of   goods.
