But there’s a bit of a problem: That "swamp" Mr. Trump liked to talk about on the campaign trail? It’s still undrained. And now the dwellers of that   swamp are mobilizing to protect their patch of the bog, with armies of corporate lobbyists scrambling to protect their favorite provisions, exceptions and deductions before it is too late.
