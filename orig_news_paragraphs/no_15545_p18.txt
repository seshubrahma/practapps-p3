McCants and other experts on Islam also warned against projecting Saudi Arabia’s strict theocracy as a model for "moderate Muslims."   While the country is the custodian of Islam’s "two holy mosques," it has also spread a toxic theology, Salafism, that has inspired extremists like al Qaeda and ISIS. What’s more, the country has a horrible human rights record, according to international watchdogs, particularly on religious freedom.
