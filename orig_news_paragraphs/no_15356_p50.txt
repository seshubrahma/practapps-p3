Anna Malik, who’s from Hungary, doesn’t just know how to spin a basketball, she can spin one on the tip of her guitar and she can play the guitar while the basketball spins, and she can sing while she plays the guitar while the basketball spins. For doing all that for 13. 23 seconds, Malik holds a Guinness World Record. Maybe one day she’ll record a record, maybe about setting a record, maybe while setting a record.
