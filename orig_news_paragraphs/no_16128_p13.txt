House Speaker Paul Ryan said Tuesday he has the "highest regard" for Pence and he’s "just as anxious" as everyone to hear who Trump will choose as his running mate. Pence’s allies grew pessimistic over the weekend as reports emerged that Trump was considering two retired generals, Michael Flynn and Stanley McChrystal.
