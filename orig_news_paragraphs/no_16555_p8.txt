The fact sheet the White House put out at the conclusion of the G20 touted the economic progress under the eight years of the Obama presidency. But that bright spot aside, his trip to China has offered reminders that since Obama took office in 2009    and especially since Xi emerged as a nationalist leader after taking office in 2012      relations have worsened.
