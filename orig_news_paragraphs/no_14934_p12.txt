September 10, 2011    Two Afghan civilians are killed and 77 US troops are wounded during a   improvised explosive device (SVBIED) attack at the entrance of Combat Outpost Sayed Abad, an ISAF base in Afghanistan’s Wardak province. The Taliban claim responsibility. September 13, 2011   Taliban militants open fire on the US embassy and NATO’s International Security Assistance Force headquarters in central Kabul.
