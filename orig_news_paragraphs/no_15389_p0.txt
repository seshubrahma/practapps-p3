Paris (CNN) After one of the most contentious elections in modern French history, Emmanuel Macron is the new President of France    and he has a whole lot to worry about.   Europe and the United States now face the prospect of a young and untested leader with some strong ideas but little political muscle to carry them out.   Above all, Macron must take on what his foe, leader of the   Marine Le Pen, has identified as "the survival of France."
