Candid photos from SAG Awards 2017, Legendary comedic actress and "Grace and Frankie" star Lily Tomlin received a standing ovation after her "9 to 5" costar Dolly Parton presented her with the Lifetime Achievement Award. Tomlin kept the audience in stitches with her acceptance speech, including her advice to young people in the industry.   " Behind every failure is an opportunity someone wishes they had missed," Tomlin said.
