May 27, 2017,   On learning more details of Friday’s attack, Pope Francis declared that the victims were "martyrs," telling thousands of pilgrims gathered in St. Peter’s Square on Sunday that the murdered Christians, amongst whom were a number of children, "were killed after having refused to renounce their Christian faith. " Reiterating his closeness to the whole Egyptian nation that two days ago suffered "another act of ferocious violence," the Pope prayed that the Lord might "welcome these courageous witnesses, these martyrs, in his peace and convert the hearts of the terrorists.
