George Clooney, "There’s not going to be a President Donald Trump. " That’s what Clooney told a roomful of reporters at the Cannes Film Festival in France in May during a press junket for his film Money Monster. Clooney railed against Trump all year, including vowing that the U. S. would never build a wall as Trump promised, and also variously called him a "xenophobic fascist" and an opportunist.
