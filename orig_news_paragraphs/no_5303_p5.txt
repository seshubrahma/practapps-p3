In August 2016, Facebook fired its entire Trending News team, formerly responsible for curating the platform’s "Trending News" list, after Breitbart News and others reported accusations of progressive, liberal biases. At the beginning of this year, Facebook launched their own features in Germany to counter fake news after authorities had claimed such stories could help populists in this year’s elections and threatened to sue unless unfavourable content was deleted quickly.
