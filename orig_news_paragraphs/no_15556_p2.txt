The   Reagan, a   carrier, has a crew of 4, 539 and is equipped with roughly 60 aircraft, according to the Navy. It was commissioned in 2003 and cost about $8. 5 billion. The USS Carl Vinson, also a   carrier, arrived at the Korean Peninsula late last month as a show of force in advance of a   sixth nuclear test from the North Korean government. While North Korea has yet to carry out that nuclear test, it did launch a   missile that achieved an altitude of more than 2, 100 kilometers (1, 300 miles) on Sunday, according to the North Koreans, the highest launch to date in their missile test program.
