" The show is part of Shakespeare in the Park, a longstanding summer tradition created by the Public Theater, which was formed about 60 years ago. Familiar faces have graced the stages. To name a few: Meryl Streep in "The Taming of the Shrew," Liev Schreiber in "Macbeth," Anne Hathaway in "Twelfth Night," and Al Pacino in "The Merchant of Venice. "
