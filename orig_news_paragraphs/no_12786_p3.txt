On the campaign trail, Trump frequently criticizes Obama’s trade deals with foreign countries and says China continues to take advantage of the United States. Earnest also compared Trump and Sanders to the GOP’s House Freedom Caucus, and told reporters that Obama won a June 2015 debate over the Trade Promotion Authority bill in the House of Representatives, despite the caucus’ opposition to the legislation. That TPA debate established debate rules which make it easier for the Congress to approve the pending TPP   deal late this year or in 2017.
