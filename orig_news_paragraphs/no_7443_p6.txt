Many pundits have predicted that, if other more mainstream candidates like Chris Christie, Jeb Bush or John Kasich dropped out, Rubio could consolidate their supporters and topple Trump or Cruz from the leading positions. That seems increasingly like magical thinking on the part of pundits. Even if a Republican voter isn’t supporting Cruz or Trump as their first choice, they are more comfortable supporting either of them, clear outsiders, as the party’s nominee than one of the other candidates.
