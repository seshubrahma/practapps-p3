The complex itinerary will take Trump to the capitals of three great world religions and introduce him to the grueling pace of presidential foreign travel. Trump departed Washington as controversies swirled, all centering on the alleged ties between his campaign and Russia. He wasn’t able to completely escape the storm in Riyadh: at an evening news conference, his Secretary of State Rex Tillerson was forced to answer a question about the matter. But for the most part, Trump’s first day on foreign soil proceeded without major hiccups.
