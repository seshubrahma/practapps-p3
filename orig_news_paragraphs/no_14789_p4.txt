He targeted cyber hacking groups LulzSec and Anonymous, according to his profile on the Southern District’s website. Bharara’s team of   prosecutors also went after international narcotics traffickers, corruption in local and state government, including in Albany, and took down gang kingpins and mobsters in the Bronx, Yonkers and other areas. His office extradited and sent to jail arms trafficker Viktor Bout and put Faisal Shahzad, the Times Square bomber, behind bars for life. Given New York’s international profile, many of Bharara’s cases went global.
