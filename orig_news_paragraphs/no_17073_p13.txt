The 2016 political maelstrom that engulfed Comey and the FBI is exactly what Comey tried to avoid, people close to the FBI director say. The FBI worked to complete the Clinton investigation before the July Democratic convention. No one could have counted on the intrusion of Anthony Weiner, the disgraced congressman and estranged husband of Clinton’s longtime aide Huma Abedin. FBI investigators looking into allegations that Weiner sent sexually explicit messages to an underage girl stumbled on a batch of Abedin’s emails that appeared to relate to the Clinton probe.
