Uber driver verbally assaulted, A motorist in a white SUV unleashed this hateful rant last week at an Uber driver in New York City. The Uber driver, a Muslim and a US citizen originally from Morocco, captured the incident on video. It occurred in the Astoria section of Queens on November 17. The Uber driver told CNN the motorist cut him off, yelled at him and continued to follow him for a few blocks.
