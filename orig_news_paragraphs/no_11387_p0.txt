GOP presidential candidate Donald Trump said that George W. Bush and President Obama were "pretty equal" in reducing the US’ standing in the world and that he would move the US’ embassy from Tel Aviv to Jerusalem in an interview broadcast on CNN’s "Situation Room" on Monday.   Trump, after being played a clip of prior comments about the US’ standing in the world stated that "I think Bush did a bad job, and I think Obama carried it out. He continued to do a bad job. "
