Scranton’s population has declined 25 percent since 1970. " People who’ve lived here their entire lives think things aren’t as good as they used to be, so there’s a significant number of people Trump can tap into based on ‘make America great again,’ " said Michael Allison, a political scientist at the University of Scranton. One of those voters, George Colburn, 74, runs a business making machine parts in the Scranton borough of Old Forge.
