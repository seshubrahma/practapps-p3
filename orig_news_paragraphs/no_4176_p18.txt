Here’s how the 44th president expressed himself in his first inaugural address, back in 2009:   As the world grows smaller, our common humanity shall reveal itself and that America must play its role in ushering in a new era of peace.    That might be a nice thought to have in some academic ivory tower, but in the real world, there’s precious little evidence that the polyglot peoples of the planet agree on much of anything, let alone how to usher in a new era of peace.   Heck, even Obama, having received a Nobel Peace Prize for making nice speeches, immediately escalated that forlorn war in Afghanistan.
