" My answer is that Trump didn’t win the election, the Democratic Party lost the election," Sanders said, reviving his own past criticisms.   It’s a line he’s used before, but this was a night for the old hits, and Sanders moved through his laundry list of issues over the course of nearly 55 minutes alone on stage before being joined by wife Jane Sanders and RoseAnn DeMoro, the executive director of National Nurses United, the event’s union organizers, for a      session. Sanders was interrupted repeatedly throughout his remarks by applause and standing ovations, but also by supporters in red waving "Draft Bernie" banners and placards.
