Dressed in tuxedos, they sang "Happy Birthday" to Texas royalty   —    former Gov. John B. Connally and his wife, Nellie, whose birthdays were a few days apart  —   as Nixon played the tune on a white baby grand piano. They dined at Tony’s, the "21" Club of Houston, and Nixon was so fond of the cannelloni pasta that he asked the owner, Tony Vallone, to write the recipe for him on a yellow legal pad.
