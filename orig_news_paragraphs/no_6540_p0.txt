California Governor Jerry Brown referred to taxpayers as "freeloaders" last week for objecting to his new gas tax and car fee hikes. ["The freeloaders  —   I’ve had enough of them   …      They have a president that doesn’t tell the truth and they’re following suit," he said. Brown was speaking in Orange County, defending State Assembly newcomer Josh Newman ( ) who is facing a recall effort after voting for Brown’s new transportation taxes in April.
