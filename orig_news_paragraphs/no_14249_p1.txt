Turnbull, for his part, has been attempting to secure reassurance that Trump will honor the deal, signed by the Barack Obama administration.   Sources told CNN Trump described the agreements as "a very bad deal" and complained that Australia was trying to send "the next Boston bomber" to the US.   What is the deal?   Under the arrangement, agreed by Obama and Turnbull in November within days of Trump’s election, Australia would transfer around 1, 250 refugees currently held in offshore detention centers on the Pacific Island nation of Nauru and Papua New Guinea’s Manus Island to the US.
