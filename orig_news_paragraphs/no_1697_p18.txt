Mr. Trump’s comments were a variation on criticisms made by the National Rifle Association, perhaps Mr. Trump’s closest political ally in the presidential race. The group has publicly criticized Secret Service protection for Mr. Obama’s two daughters, which is required under federal law, as hypocritical. In 2013, the group released an advertisement calling Mr. Obama "an elitist hypocrite" for allowing the service to protect his family   —   which, like other first families, has faced multiple death threats
