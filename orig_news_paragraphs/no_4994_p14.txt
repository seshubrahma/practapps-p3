Voice of America was created in 1942 during World War II to send   news across Europe, as it aimed to counter Nazi and Japanese propaganda. The agency has since evolved into a more traditional news operation, while still pushing out the virtues of democracy worldwide," Politico reported. The problem in recent years is that Voice of America, Radio Free Europe, and the other broadcasting entities run by BBG have shifted from their traditional mission of promoting classic American values and interests to the rest of the world, and have become mirror images of mainstream media outlets like CNN, ABC, NBC, and CBS. Current CEO John Lansing, a veteran cable industry executive,  took over the reins in 2015 and has done nothing to stop that trend.
