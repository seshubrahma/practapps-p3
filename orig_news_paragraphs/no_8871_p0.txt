Actress Sharon Stone admits that she’s ready for Hillary, but is afraid for America’s future if White House hopeful Donald Trump is elected president  —   which she says could lead to another Holocaust. ["We have leaders and people who are trying to be leaders who are running campaigns based on fear, and that’s a contagion. It’s horrible.
