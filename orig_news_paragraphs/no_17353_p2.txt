Preparing to depart office, Obama is undergoing a farewell tour of interviews, many focused on his experience and legacy as the nation’s first   president. It’s a topic he’s grown more comfortable discussing candidly as his term winds to a close. And it’s one that’s only gained more prominence since Donald Trump    who flamed the racially tinged birther theory    was elected President. Obama hopes to provide a positive summation of his time in office while acknowledging   racial tension was never going to be resolved by his historic election.
