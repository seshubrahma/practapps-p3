It is not about terror. There is no great fear of terrorism from refugees. Of the 784, 000 refugees resettled in the United States over the last 15 years, exactly three have been arrested for planning terrorist activities, according to a Cato Institute report by Alex Nowrasteh. Of the 3, 252, 493 refugees admitted into the country from 1975 to 2016, 20 of them took part in terrorism.
