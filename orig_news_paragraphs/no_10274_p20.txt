Even at the height of Billy’s power, he was known as "the corrupt midget" in Carr’s columns and on the radio show. Johnny Depp played Whitey Bulger in the film "Black Mass" with Benedict Cumberpatch playing Billy. Jack Nicholson’s character in "The Departed" was based on Whitey. When Whitey was finally brought to trial, he named Carr as a defense witness just to keep the columnist from sitting in on the trial as a spectator.
