After voting in 2012 and 2014, she moved to Fort Worth’s Tarrant County in 2015, where she registered to vote again   —   this time, ticking the box that indicated she was not a citizen. When her registration was rejected, she called elections officials, telling them that she had voted in Dallas. Told that people who checked the noncitizen box were ineligible to vote, she reapplied, this time indicating that she was a citizen.
