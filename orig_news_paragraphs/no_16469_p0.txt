Austin, Texas  (CNN) Donald Trump launched his presidential campaign by promising to build a "great, great wall" on the US southern border    and make Mexico pay for it    while painting undocumented Mexican immigrants as criminals and rapists. More than a year later, Trump, now the Republican nominee, is preparing to wade back into the heated immigration debate. This time, though, he appears poised to lay out a more nuanced immigration policy    one that could roll back some of the unapologetically blunt proposals that helped carry him to victory in the GOP primaries.
