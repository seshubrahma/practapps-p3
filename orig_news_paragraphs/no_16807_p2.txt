The GOP lead has been shrinking in recent months     it was roughly 12, 000 in August and 2, 700 in September.    Republicans have enjoyed an edge in voter registration in Colorado since 1986, according to an analysis of decades of publicly available voter registration records. The numbers fluctuate on a monthly basis, but the GOP advantage was about 177, 000 in November 2004, when George W. Bush carried the state.
