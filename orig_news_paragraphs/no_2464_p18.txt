As premiums rise, property values fall, a trend already hurting home prices in places like Atlantic City, Norfolk, Va. and St. Petersburg, Fla. according to local real estate agents. Roy and Carol Baker, who now live in Sarasota, Fla. recalled trying for several months to sell their home in nearby Siesta Key in 2014. Interested buyers kept backing out of the purchase when they found out that the annual flood insurance premium was roughly $7, 000, they said.
