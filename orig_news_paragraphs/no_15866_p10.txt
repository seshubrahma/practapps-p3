North Korea has fired 16 missiles this year. Experts say each launch gets the country closer to its stated goal of developing a   weapon capable of reaching the United States.   North Korea believes its missile program is the only way to deter Washington from trying to overthrow the Kim regime. US Secretary of State Rex Tillerson denied that charge last month, saying the US is only seeking denuclearization, but the Trump White House has publicly abandoned the   strategy of strategic patience.
