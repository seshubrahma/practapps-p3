The word of the year, xiao mubiao, or "small goal," comes courtesy of China’s richest man, Wang Jianlin. In an interview earlier this year, he offered a piece of advice: "Set a small goal first, for example, earn 100 million yuan to start with!" (100 million yuan is roughly $14 million.)
