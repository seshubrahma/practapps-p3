Former U. N. ambassador John Bolton took stock of President Trump’s foreign policy on Thursday’s Breitbart News Daily with SiriusXM host Alex Marlow. ["The president, during the campaign, always talked about an America First foreign policy. What he said expressly he meant by that was that American national interests have to dominate what a president does  —   not abstract ideas about the way the world order should look, but concentrating on threats to America and dealing with them," Bolton recalled.
