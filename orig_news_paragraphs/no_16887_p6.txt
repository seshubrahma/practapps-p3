More than four in 10 votes were cast early or absentee in Iowa in 2012, along with a third of votes in Ohio and a quarter in Michigan. Battlegrounds closer to the northeast, which tend to have more restrictive policies for voting early or absentee, including Virginia (12%) New Hampshire (10%) and Pennsylvania (5%) are less likely to have large blocks of votes cast before Election Day.
