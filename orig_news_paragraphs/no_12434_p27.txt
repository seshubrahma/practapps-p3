Rubio also has seen his campaign crippled by broad opposition to his 2013 immigration bill. That bill would have allowed companies  —   such as   based in his home state   —   to hire many more foreign   workers, and it also allowed an unlimited hiring of foreigners who get college degrees at U. S. universities. The bill would also have amnestied all illegal immigrants, and would have doubled legal immigration to 2 million people per year.
