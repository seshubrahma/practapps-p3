The immigration issue has defined the rise of Trump, as Americans continue to be on his side. Some 80 percent of Americans said they disapproved of sanctuary city policies, as Breitbart Texas reported, with 52 percent of voters saying they supported Trump’s border wall plan. Even when it comes to other immigration issues, like the number of refugees entering the U. S. every year, some 52 percent of likely voters said they supported Trump’s ban on refugees for a few months, as Breitbart Texas reported.
