Netanyahu has made it exceedingly clear that he’s ready to work with Trump, especially as the relationship with President Obama deteriorated rapidly in its final weeks, CNN’s Oren Liebermann reports.   Israeli leaders are already expecting Trump to follow through on one of his biggest campaign promises    to move the US Embassy from Tel Aviv to Jerusalem and recognize Jerusalem as the capital of Israel.   Israel’s ambassador to the UN, Danny Danon, said shortly after Trump’s inauguration: "We look forward to your visit to Jerusalem, the capital of Israel. "
