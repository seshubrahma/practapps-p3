Twenty percent of the respondents declined to pick either of the two leaders in the poll of 1, 397 likely voters, which was concluded Aug . 29.   Trump’s support among Republicans climbed from 71 percent up to 78. 3 percent. Clinton’s support among Democrats dipped slightly from 81. 6 percent to 78. 5 percent.   The poll reflects a general trend, in which Trump has managed to close much of the gap that Clinton opened in early August.
