Lie #3: The   protests in Washington, D. C. were important. The protests were nothing more than the venting of outrage at Trump’s election. For all the talk of "women’s rights," there was nothing particular to point to that Trump had done about anything relating to women. The demonstration was large, but also disorganized, as well as vulgar, and protesters left heaps of trash over the various routes they took, including protest signs abandoned at Union Station as they left the capital.
