As promised earlier in the day, the Brandenburg Gate  —   a rare survivor of 19th century Berlin   —   has been illuminated in the    tricolour of the 1919 Bundesflagge in memory of those who were killed last night. This morning Berlin mayor Michael Müller said at a press conference: "The gate has been lit in the colours of places affected by terrorism in the past, but today it will be lit in our own colours".
