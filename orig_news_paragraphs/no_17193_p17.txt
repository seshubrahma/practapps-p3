November 15, 2016   Carson declines an offer from   Donald Trump to join his cabinet as Secretary of Health and Human Services. A close Carson adviser tells CNN Carson declined the position in part because running a federal agency would be "a lot to ask" of a "neophyte" like Carson who has never before worked in government.   December 5, 2016    The Trump transition team announces Carson will be nominated as the next secretary of the Department of Housing and Urban Development.
