Mr. Trump, who was rising in national polls before the debate, has also narrowed the gap in important battleground states. A   poll released Monday showed him running about even with Mrs. Clinton among likely voters in Pennsylvania, a state Democrats regard as a blue wall barricading Mr. Trump’s path to the White House. But even among Republican and independent women who do not support Mrs. Clinton, Mr. Trump’s debate performance  —   frequent interjections of "Wrong" and a thin command of policy details   —   failed to allay doubts.
