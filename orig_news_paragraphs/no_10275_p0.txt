A leading #NeverTrump radio host is pulling the plug on his Wisconsin show, citing age and retirement plans to explain his departure from the airwaves by end of the year. ["This morning, I announced that I am stepping down from my daily radio show on WTMJ at the end of this year," Charlie Sykes said on his website.    I know that lot of people will assume that my decision has something to do with this current campaign and the rise of Trumpism. But, the reality (as my friends and family know) is that I made this decision a long time ago.
