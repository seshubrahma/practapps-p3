A Pew Research Center poll of 1, 502 adults found that 35 percent of Americans thought Mr. Trump would be a good or great president 18 percent said he would be average while 38 percent said he would be poor or terrible. That is better than in October, when just 25 percent of the public said Mr. Trump would make a good or great president, while 57 percent said he would be poor or terrible.
