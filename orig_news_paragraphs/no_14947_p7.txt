Under Trump’s executive order, Pruitt is to review three of the rules in the plan and decide how to deal with them.   Moratorium on federal coal program, The Obama administration placed a moratorium on coal leasing on federal lands in 2016. The move didn’t affect leases already in effect but paused new applications.   Environmentalists had long criticized the practice, saying coal companies paid so little for their leases that the US government and taxpayers were subsidizing coal production, which harmed health and the environment.
