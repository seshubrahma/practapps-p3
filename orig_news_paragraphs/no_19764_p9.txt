More than 50, 000 badged employees report to work there each day, many with direct access to the airfield —  and thus to the vulnerable aircraft waiting upon it. More than 100, 000 passenger vehicles use the airport’s roads and parking lots every day, and, in 2015 alone, LAX hosted 75 million passengers in combined departures and arrivals. LAX is also policed like a city. The airport has its own SWAT team and employs roughly 500 sworn police officers, double the number of cops in the   city of Pasadena and more than the total number of state police in all of Rhode Island.   …
