 (CNN) The President’s latest Twitter attack on the special counsel investigation by Robert Mueller is likely to claim a new victim: Deputy Attorney General Rod Rosenstein, the lawyer at least nominally in charge of the  investigation.    Rosenstein was the Justice Department official who drafted a memo to the President alleging numerous  deficiencies in the performance of   Director James Comey. The memo included stinging criticism of the FBI director’s handling of the investigation of Hillary Clinton’s email.
