Until this year, the highest monthly total of refugees resettled during the month of October was 6, 352, the number that came in during October 2014, the first month of FY 2015. This October, the Obama administration is on a record path to double that number. At a rate of 411 refugees per day, 12, 741 refugees will be resettled in October 2016, the first month of FY 2017.
