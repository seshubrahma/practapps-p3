One week into the administration, Google and other tech companies began to push back, criticizing Mr. Trump’s executive order on immigration bans. The company said an estimated 187 employees were affected by the order and it urged any of those employees who were abroad to work with the company to return safely to the United States. " It’s painful to see the personal cost of this executive order on our colleagues," Sundar Pichai, Google’s chief executive, said in a memo to employees over the weekend.
