Plank Eight: Free Love, or at least, deregulated love. Billionaires naturally don’t like to throw stones at each other’s glass houses. Stern sexual morality has been in decline across the whole of society for decades, but glass houses tend to get glassier as one moves up the income ladder. And because the billionaires don’t want to hurt each other, and tend to frown on judgmentalism of any kind, the political class and pundit classes, too, have tended to stay away from any judgment on any sort of behavior.
