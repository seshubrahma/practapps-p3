Republican voters would trust Donald Trump to lead the GOP by a wide margin over House Speaker Paul Ryan, according to a new survey from NBC News. [While Trump and Ryan have begun a "process" to unify the party ahead of the November elections, Republican voters nationwide have already made a clear choice.   A strong majority of Republicans, 58 percent, say they trust Donald Trump to lead the party, almost 20 points more than the 39 percent who would pick Paul Ryan to lead the party. As the presumptive party nominee, Donald Trump is strengthening his grip on the Republican party.
