Immediately following the U. S. presidential election on November 8,   Trump demonstrations sprang up in New York, San Francisco, and Los Angeles —  cities that vote overwhelmingly Democratic. The protesters felt robbed: Hillary Clinton had won the majority of voters nationwide.
