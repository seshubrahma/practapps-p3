Washington (CNN)"Buckle up" was the advice Vice   Mike Pence gave Hill Republicans about the hectic pace ahead for Capitol Hill ’s first 100 days in 2017.   The   Congress returns Tuesday to begin an aggressive push on a range of policies that their new partner      Donald Trump    can help turn into law.   The focus in January will be to start the wheels in motion to repeal the Affordable Care Act, also known as Obamacare, and vote to confirm many of Trump’s Cabinet nominees.
