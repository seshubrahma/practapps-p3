A Syrian activist group based in the UK said indications were there were pockets of violence, but large areas of the country were calm. " Most areas in Syria are quiet since the start of the ceasefire, but violent clashes were reported less than two hours after it took effect in the Hama suburbs between regime forces and rebel fighters in Tal Beejo in northern Aleppo causing to regime forces to withdraw," said Rami Abdulrahman of the   Syrian Observatory for Human Rights. Neither SANA nor Russian state media has reported episodes of violence Friday morning.
