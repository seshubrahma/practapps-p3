Washington (CNN)   Donald Trump appeared open Friday to compromising on his   pledge to repeal and replace Obamacare    citing a conversation with none other than President Barack Obama himself.   But the openness was complicated by a shift in the official positions listed on his website. The comments, made in an interview with the Wall Street Journal, signal less of a policy shift for Trump than a change from the rhetoric that helped win him the presidency just three days ago and could set up a fight with conservatives.
