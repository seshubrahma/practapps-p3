"  Fillon, 62, is a lawyer who served as prime minister between 2007 and 2012 under Sarkozy.   He’s a social conservative who has talked of ending France’s famed   work week and getting tough with the country’s powerful trade unions. He has also spoken of cutting public spending, abolishing the wealth tax, reducing immigration and investing billions in security, defense and justice. A hard line on immigration has also bolstered Le Pen, whose   stance is gaining popularity among French voters.
