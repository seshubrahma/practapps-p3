An American wrestler devised the evilest, most notorious, and meanest persona south of the border to ensure his certain success in Mexico’s professional wrestling league —   Donald Trump. [Sam Adonis says that the idea came to him during the blazing heat of the 2016 presidential campaign.   He observed that "people were so angry," and thought that if he donned a pair of wrestling tights with an image of Donald Trump on them he would become an instant villain for the Mexican fans who watch the Consejo Mundial de Lucha Libre (CMLL) professional wrestling promotion.
