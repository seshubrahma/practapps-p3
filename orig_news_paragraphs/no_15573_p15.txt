Rouhani promises change in Iran, The key issues   The President has had a tough time defending the 2015 nuclear deal and his opponents have accused him of not making good on his promises. Debates have largely centered on this issue.   Rouhani billed the deal as one that would thrust open the gates of economic opportunity, bring the country out of its isolation and create millions of jobs for Iranians. The agreement has brought a string of billion dollar deals with Western firms for airplanes and oil exploration in Iran.
