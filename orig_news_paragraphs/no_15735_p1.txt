Spectators at the Senate hearing will probably be treated to a gossipy inside look at the quirky interactions between these two unpredictable men.   Indeed, if Comey wishes to maintain any semblance of the reputation for integrity he so scrupulously cultivated before he blew up the presidential election, he will decline to reveal things that would compromise special counsel Robert Mueller’s probe into contacts between Trump campaign aides and Russia. Comey will also be boxed into a tight corner by his previous proclamations that he can’t answer some questions because of national security and the need to maintain the integrity of ongoing investigations. Additionally, he may be impeded in answering questions about the conversations everyone really wants to know about: those that Comey had with the President.
