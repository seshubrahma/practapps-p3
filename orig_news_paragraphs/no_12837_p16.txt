Without a doubt, the Founding Fathers would be horrified at what’s happening to America in the 21st century. After all, the Preamble of their revered document, the U. S. Constitution, is decisive on this point: The new federal government will "insure domestic tranquility" and "provide for the common defense. " And here’s Alexander Hamilton, going further, arguing that national unity is the key to national security: The safety of a republic depends essentially on the energy of a common National sentiment on a uniformity of principles and habits on the exemption of the citizens from foreign bias, and prejudice and on that love of country which will almost invariably be found to be closely connected with birth, education and family.
