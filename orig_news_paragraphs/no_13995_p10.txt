Taking it beyond marches, For President Trump’s opponents to succeed, the inauguration marches can only be a first step. Two new publications point to a path forward for his Democratic opponents. In "Indivisible: A Practical Guide for Resisting the Trump Agenda,"  former congressional staffers turn to the tactics that Tea Party Republicans used against President Obama as a model for opposing Trump.   The online booklet outlines a defensive strategy to stop Trump’s agenda.
