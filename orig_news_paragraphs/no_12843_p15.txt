Since the hot Broadway show debuted, every liberal politician has struggled for a piece of the show and Miranda’s celebrity. President Barack Obama and First Lady Michelle Obama filmed a video lavishing praise on the show at the Tony Awards. Manuel, who visited the White House in 2009 to perform beat poetry for the Obamas, also performed a   music video with Obama at the White House. It’s no surprise, therefore, that Manuel chose to insert his politics into his hit Broadway musical.
