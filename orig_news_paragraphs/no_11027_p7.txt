Buchanan has warned of the Balkanization that will ensue as a result of the immigration agenda of Bush Republicanism —   writing in 2008 that Bush’s immigration policies "may turn this republic into a Tower of Babel. " According to a 2009 Wall Street Journal report, under George W. Bush’s presidency, the U. S. created three million new jobs. Yet at the same time, under Bush’s presidency, 10. 5 million new immigrants (legal and illegal) settled in the United States  —   meaning that Bush brought in three immigrants for every one job he created. During that time, the number of     Americans not working exploded.
