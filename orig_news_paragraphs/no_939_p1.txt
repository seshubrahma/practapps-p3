In the     world of online news, 84 minutes struck some journalists as an awfully long time to wait. The White House took advantage, releasing a   statement that detailed Mr. Trump’s tax figures from 2005 before MSNBC had a chance to air its own report. The Daily Beast and other news outlets ran items as well. Ms. Maddow, who is enjoying the biggest viewership of her show’s   run, did not appear to mind.
