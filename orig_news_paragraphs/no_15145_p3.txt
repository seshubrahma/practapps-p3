On that score, therefore, Trump gave Xi what he wanted most from this first meeting: vivid images of respect for China as a great nation and for him as a great leader. The moment at which the Trump show likely captured Xi came when Trump’s    granddaughter, Arabella, performed for Xi and his wife Peng Liyuan, a famous Chinese folk singer. Arabella  sang "Jasmine Flower," a song Peng is famous for performing, and followed up by reciting a poem by Li Bai, one of China’s most acclaimed poets and a personal favorite of Xi’s. Moreover, she did this all in Mandarin.
