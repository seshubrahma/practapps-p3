Hundreds of thousands of civilians are still in western Mosul, often exploited by ISIS as human shields.   But even with the heavy assault, the Trump administration is largely settling down and following the same slow, deliberate approach of the Obama administration. The battle for Mosul has taken more than half a year and may take many more months. In neighboring Syria, there are nearly a thousand US boots on the ground, backing a mixed   force that aims at overrunning the city of Raqqa, the de facto capital of ISIS.
