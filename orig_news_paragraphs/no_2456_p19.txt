Mr. Trump did not dispute reports that he had used a meeting last week with Nigel Farage, the U. K. Independence Party leader, to raise his opposition to offshore wind farms. Mr. Trump has long complained that wind farms would mar the view from his golf course in Aberdeenshire, Scotland. " I might have brought it up," Mr. Trump said, then argued he had done so because of policy concerns about wind farms rather than any personal interest.
