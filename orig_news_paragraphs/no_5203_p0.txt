A plan by President Donald Trump’s administration to "extreme vet’ foreign refugees entering the U. S. in the hundreds every week could include an ideological test. [The Trump Administration is looking at a number of ways to vet foreigners entering the U. S. through a more rigorous, extensive process that would include asking whether or not an individual has the same values as American citizens, according to a report by the Wall Street Journal.   Officials working on the vetting process also said foreigners could be asked to disclose their social media passwords, past financial records and cell phone data —  coupled with questions on ideology.
