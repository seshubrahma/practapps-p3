Populist parties lost recent elections in the Netherlands and Austria, but they have nonetheless risen from the fringes and are shaking up the debate. Here’s a look at how    and why    some of Europe’s populist parties have swept into mainstream politics.    France, France will hold its second and final round of votes in its presidential election on Sunday that pits a     euroskeptic against a centrist former banker promoting unity with the European Union.
