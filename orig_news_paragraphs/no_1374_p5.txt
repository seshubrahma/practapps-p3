The point of a modern American presidential campaign is to inspire and turn out as many of your voters as possible while avoiding inspiring similar passion for your opponent. In 2008 and 2012, Barack Obama’s campaign pioneered some of the most effective ways to use social media and   organizing to win political campaigns. The Obama operation turned unfocused online passion into a          operation whose effectiveness surprised longtime political observers. Mr. Trump has eschewed many of the Obama team’s     efforts.
