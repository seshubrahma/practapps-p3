Virtually everything about these captives was classified until a Senate Intelligence Committee report in 2014 disclosed grisly details about torture. " You just weren’t allowed to talk about those things, even with them," said Dr. Michael Fahey Traver, an Army psychiatrist at Guantánamo in 2013 and 2014. He was assigned to treat only   detainees kept in Camp 7, Guantánamo’s most restricted area, so that he did not inadvertently pass sensitive information to other prisoners.
