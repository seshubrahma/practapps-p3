Joshua Green of Bloomberg writes that Breitbart News has hired John Carney, a veteran finance journalist, to run a news vertical on the economy. [From Bloomberg:  In a bid to expand its influence in the era of Donald Trump, Breitbart News, the crusading   website that was an early champion of the incoming president, has hired veteran financial journalist John Carney of The Wall Street Journal to lead a new finance and economics section set to launch soon after the Jan. 20th presidential inauguration.   " Breitbart was way ahead of the curve on politics and the rise of Trump," Carney said in a telephone interview.
