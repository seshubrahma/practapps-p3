Pop icon Madonna waded back into the political jet stream last weekend when, during the Women’s March on Washington protest rally, she told thousands of demonstrators that she has often thought of "blowing up the White House" following Donald Trump becoming the 45th President of the United States. ["Yes, I’m angry. Yes, I’m outraged. Yes, I have thought an awful lot about blowing up the White House, but I know that this won’t change anything," the    "Rebel Heart" singer said, to applause from the protesters.
