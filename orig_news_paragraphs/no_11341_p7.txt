California, with a population of 39 million, is the state that has resettled the most Syrian refugees so far in Fiscal Year 2016, 1, 300 of the 11, 469 total. Michigan, with a population of 9. 9 million, is the state with the second highest number of resettled Syrian refugees so far in this fiscal year, with 1, 217. Ten states, including Delaware, which Vice President Joe Biden represented in the United States Senate for more than three decades, have resettled no Syrian refugees in Fiscal Year 2016.
