In the first major address given by Trump since former Breitbart News Executive Chairman Stephen K. Bannon took the reins of the Trump campaign, The Donald showed that you can be passionate about America, compassionate about people, but still    honest about what it’s going to take to restore America’s greatness. This interview with law and order Sheriff Joe Arpaio, done as part of the filming for my upcoming movie   The Bloody Road to Philadelphia, illustrates a great deal about who the real Donald Trump is  —   and it’s far from the super villain caricature that the establishment is desperately trying to draw. In it, Arpaio reveals how Trump dealt with both the sheriff and Arpaio’s wife, who is stricken with cancer.
