Servers don white gloves to facilitate tastings of wine "by the crystal spoon," poured out in   portions, and sipped through the handle, per Hungarian tradition. The least expensive option runs $15 for the ounce.   For a more festive evening, bartenders will roll up a cart of champagne and teach guests to saber the lid with a large knife.
