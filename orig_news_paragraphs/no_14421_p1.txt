If they like each other, Trump will be more likely to accept the case for Canada as the most important friend of the United States, with a century and a half of shared history, geography and commerce.   Trudeau is trying to protect Canada from the harshest elements of Trump’s commitment to "America First": his protectionist, nativist and   vision of the United States, which could be disastrous for Canada. Young, optimistic and handsome, Trudeau is the son of celebrated former Prime Minister Pierre Trudeau. Since he led his Liberal Party to a surprising win over the Conservatives in 2015, Trudeau’s "sunny ways" and charisma have made him a darling of progressives and an international celebrity.
