The cups are labeled with campaign logos she has copied when they are available, or designs she has created when they are not. Professional pollsters have used computers since the days when they were primitive, but at the Monogram Shop, the daily running total is kept on a handwritten piece of paper. It is updated at closing time, and the numbers are posted on the store window, just as in 2004, 2008 and 2012. The final counts from those races are elusive.
