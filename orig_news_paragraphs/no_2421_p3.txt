The purpose of the   event, which broke down the Obama presidency into topics including inequality, counterterrorism, immigration, the Supreme Court and race, was to offer what Mr. Zelizer called a historical "first cut," by scholars who had "lived and felt" the events of President Obama’s terms in office.
