The unique nature of the action at Standing Rock combined with the honesty, pain and humor of all the conversations    including one with a mother and father on Pine Ridge whose son had been killed only days before I talked to them    took my breath away and also somehow inspired me.   I talked with actor and activist Adam Beach about the challenges of being a native actor in Hollywood, an industry that as he put it wants his people permanently "back in the 1800s. " I spoke with activist and former congressional candidate Chase Iron Eyes about how he and his wife made the choice to move their family back to the Standing Rock Reservation, even though they had to downsize and sacrifice many of the   comforts to do so.
