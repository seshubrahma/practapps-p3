 (CNN) The   Joint Strike Fighter, the most expensive and one of the most controversial weapons systems in US history, is on its way to Japan to be "the cornerstone" of US defense in the Pacific. The first of 16 US Marine Corps   left Arizona Monday on the initial overseas deployment for the stealth jets.   The planes are heading for Marine Corps Air Station Iwakuni, not far from Hiroshima, on the Japanese main island of Honshu.
