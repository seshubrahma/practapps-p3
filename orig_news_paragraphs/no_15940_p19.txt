North Korea has been quite wily in recent years in trying to evade the UN Security Council resolutions . .. using diplomatic cover for some of its activities," Murphy said. To that end, one partner the United States says it’s pursuing to deal with North Korea is Philippine President Rodrigo Duterte, who’s serving as the chair of the Association of Southeast Nations (ASEAN).
