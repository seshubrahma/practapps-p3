A day after her narrow confirmation as education secretary, Betsy DeVos toured the department she now leads and delivered a speech to staff members and reporters. She began with a joke: "This confirmation process and the drama it engendered have been a bit of a bear," she said, a reference to her   comment during her Senate hearing that schools might need guns to protect students from "grizzlies. " " We’ve just come through one of the most bruising, divisive elections in modern times, and that’s O. K.," she said.
