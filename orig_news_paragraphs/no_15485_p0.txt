 (CNN) North Korea would hold talks with the United States "under the right conditions," a senior North Korean diplomat said Saturday, South Korea’s Yonhap news agency reported. Choe Son Hui,   of the North America bureau chief of North Korea’s Foreign Ministry, spoke to reporters Saturday at the Beijing Capital International Airport. She made the remarks more than a week after President Donald Trump said he would be willing to meet with North Korean leader Kim Jong Un "under the right circumstances.
