Tillerson’s performance at last week’s G20 in Bonn offered a hint of his approach going forward. It’s the kind of large gathering where leaders often engage in a form of foreign policy   holding quick    meetings on the sidelines, each one marked by an appearance before the cameras and often a comment or two to the press. But Tillerson mostly stayed silent. As TV crews and reporters were ushered out of the room during an appearance with UK Foreign Minister Boris Johnson, the British minister asked, "Are we still being recorded?"
