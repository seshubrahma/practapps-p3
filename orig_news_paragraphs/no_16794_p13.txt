The extent to which Trump’s tax controversies will damage him could also take time to play out. But there is evidence that a majority of voters believe it is important that candidates for president release their tax records. In a Monmouth University poll last month for instance, 62% of those asked thought it was very important or somewhat important for candidates to show their tax records. Trump’s decision not to release his tax returns is a complicated issue politically, and the controversy shows the complications of someone like Trump, with a vast business empire, running for president.
