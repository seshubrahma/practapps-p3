What you need to know about the Dutch election, Rise of populism, The Netherlands vote will be studied for insight into a wider populist trend in Europe and elsewhere. Close to a dozen European countries will hold elections this year with   many buoyed by US President Donald Trump’s victory, riding high in the polls. A strong showing for them could put the European Union, already rocked by Britain’s impending exit, under greater strain. In France, Marine Le Pen    who wants France to drop the euro and has threatened, like Wilders, to hold a   referendum on quitting the European Union    has made her   National Front party a leading contender in April’s presidential ballot.
