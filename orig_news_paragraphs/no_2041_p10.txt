Democrats’ threat to shut down the government   —   the sort of intransigence adopted by Republicans in the recent past   —   indicates their willingness to use the few tools available to them to block legislation next year, when possible, and to bludgeon Republicans with populist issues on which they share common ground with Mr. Trump. Pressing his case on Thursday, Mr. Manchin said that senators from both parties had campaigned on giving a "fair shake" to working people.
