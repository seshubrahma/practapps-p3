In Vista, Calif. on Sunday, Mr. Sanders told a cheering crowd that he was the strongest Democratic candidate to defeat Mr. Trump and that he hoped to have 50 percent of the total pledged delegates by the end of voting. He also stressed that his campaign had more "enthusiasm" and "energy" than his rival’s. " Our vision of social justice, of economic justice, of racial justice and of environmental justice  —   our vision is the future of this country," Mr. Sanders said.
