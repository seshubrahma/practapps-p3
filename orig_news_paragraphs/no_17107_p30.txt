Sessions’ positions on civil rights issues have not won him any fans at the NAACP. He’s received consistent "Fs" on the group’s legislative report cards for the past decade and a half. Cornell William Brooks, the group’s president, blasted Sessions’ nomination, citing what he called a "very disturbing and recurring theme of hostility to and toward civil rights" exhibited over his career. He said Sessions nomination along with a top post being awarded to Stephen Bannon were deeply troubling from a civil rights perspective.
