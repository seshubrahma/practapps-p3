The notion of recruiting Arab countries to help forge an agreement between Israelis and Palestinians   —   known as the " " approach  —   is not a new one. As secretary of state under President George Bush, James A. Baker III organized the first regional conference in 1991 at which Arab leaders sat down with Israel’s prime minister. President George W. Bush invited Arab leaders to a summit meeting with Israel in Annapolis, Md. in 2007.
