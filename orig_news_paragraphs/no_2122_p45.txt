The Conservative Party argues that the country is taking in more refugees than it can provide for, but supports accepting Syrians. Some Canadians complain that the country should take care of its own first, and new chapters of the Soldiers of Odin, a European   group, have cropped up in recent months. A few incidents targeting Syrians  —   graffiti reading "Syrians go home and die" at a Calgary school, a pepper spray attack at an event welcoming refugees   —   drew widespread condemnation.
