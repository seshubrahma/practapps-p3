Which is to say, the number of Americans today who could have had any adult participation in the war is small —  and rapidly getting smaller, as old age takes its toll. Yes, it’s painful to think that all the heroism of that era is no longer with us, at least not in a personal way.    To be sure, there are many museum displays, history books, movies, and TV shows about World War II.
