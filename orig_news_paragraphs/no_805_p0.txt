NEWPORT NEWS, Va.   —   President Trump on Thursday used the country’s most advanced,   aircraft carrier as the    backdrop for his push to rebuild America’s armed forces with a $54 billion increase in military spending. Speaking in the hangar bay of the   Gerald R. Ford, which this year will become the nation’s largest and most powerful warship, Mr. Trump said the Navy  —   and other military services   —   must grow and modernize.
