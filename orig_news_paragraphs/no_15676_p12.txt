The ’steeple’ Politicians, actors and pretty much all public figures have their own   "rest position," and Trump is no exception. For Trump it’s "the steeple," a hand position where he uses his fingers and fingertips touch to form a triangle.   It transmits power, confidence and precision thinking, experts said. Trump rests his hands on this position when he’s seated and a very similar position called victory "O" when he’s left standing.
