Senior Fellow at the National Center for Policy Analysis David Grantham joined Whatever It Takes host Curt Schilling Friday to lay out the changes Latin American countries are making to their own immigration in response to the election of President Donald Trump and his policy to tighten immigration policy. ["Governments are using it as a good segue to begin enacting their own types of immigration reform," Grantham said to Schilling, who hosts Whatever It Takes, broadcast weekdays from 9 a. m. to 11 a. m. at Breitbart.
