As of Friday morning, the livestream had gone dark and was replaced with the words, "The Museum Has Abandoned Us. " The   exhibit was just the latest piece of performance art from LaBeouf, who has conceived and executed some unusual public art pieces in recent years with frequent collaborators Nastja Sade Ronkko and Luke Turner. Other recent art installations from the actor included intentionally getting himself stuck in an elevator for 24 hours, conducting a marathon screening of all of his movies, setting up a public call center for the public to "touch his soul," and live streaming his heartbeat over the Internet.
