So that young Chicago mother in Wicker Park, wheeling her child on the way to pick up a prescription, is now left to wonder what those young men standing in her path a few yards ahead might have in mind. We can observe, as an aside, that it’s possible to imagine many ways in which new technology could make that mother —  and all Chicagoans —  more safe, such as additional security cameras and maybe even drones.
