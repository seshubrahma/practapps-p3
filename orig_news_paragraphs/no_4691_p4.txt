Kellogg’s has been struggling over the last few years, so much so that by 2016 customer confidence in its brand name had fallen from 60th to 84th place. The company’s fall from grace coincided with Kellogg’s decision last year to cease advertising with Breitbart News, thereby snubbing Breitbart’s 45, 000, 000 readers. In November of last year, Kellogg noted that the conservative readers at Breitbart News are not "aligned with our values as a company. "
