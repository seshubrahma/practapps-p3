April 29, 2015   King Salman, in a surprise   move, appoints Interior Minister Mohammed bin Nayef as crown prince, replacing his   Prince Muqrin as his successor, and Defense Minister Mohammed bin Salman, his son, as deputy crown prince.   December 14, 2015   At least 17 women are elected to public office in the first elections in which women in the country are allowed to vote and to run for office.   January 2, 2016   Saudi Arabia announces it has executed 47 prisoners who had been convicted of terrorism over the last decade    most prominently, a Shiite cleric named Nimr   who had spoken out against the ruling Al Saud family.
