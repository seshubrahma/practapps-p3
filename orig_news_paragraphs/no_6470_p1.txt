Martin was joined by two dozen volunteers holding signs outside the Hart Senate Office Building, while Gorsuch spent the day listening to senators on the Senate Judiciary Committee make opening remarks before making his own   statement. Gorsuch was nominated by President Donald Trump to fill the seat on the high court made vacant with the passing of Justice Antonin Scalia. When the nominee walked into the hearing, he was mobbed by senators,   and at least 30 photographers. The shutter clicks from the still cameras rose and fell like the waves of sleet upon a tin roof.
