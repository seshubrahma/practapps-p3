According to the report, a bounty of about $200 a head has been placed on "drug suspects, rapists, pickpockets, swindlers, gang members, alcoholics, and other ‘troublemakers. ’" These claims are still being evaluated by the Philippines Commission on Human Rights. Reuters notes there is little documentary evidence in the report for its most explosive allegations, and it segues into a political document criticizing Duterte for Communist ties, portraying Duterte’s "social cleansing" of drug addicts to Chairman Mao’s murderous purges in China.
