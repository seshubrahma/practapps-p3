Building apartments and office complexes that attract no tenants, and entire ghost cities, count in China’s GDP tally but add little to productivity. Wasteful outlays have boosted debt to 260 percent of GDP. Nervous about a looming credit crisis, Chinese investors are heading for the doors —  selling yuan for dollars to invest in overseas real estate and securities.
