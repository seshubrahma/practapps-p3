Cities have also seen lots of benefits from networks   —   the Compact of Mayors, which has been signed by more than 120 American cities, C40 Cities Climate Leadership Group, Climate Mayors and others   —   most notably, information sharing and solutions, and reaffirming commitments to each other and to their citizens, as many mayors did in a recent letter to the  . Depending on their state, however, cities are somewhat limited in what they can do from a legislative or regulatory standpoint.
