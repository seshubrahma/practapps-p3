With great controversy surrounding the Republican presidential primary process in this tumultuous 2016 election cycle, the Wyoming GOP posted a description of how they select national delegates. As in all states, one delegate slot goes to the state chairperson, one to the state’s national committeewoman, and a third goes to the state’s national committeeman. Twelve delegates and 12 alternates are selected from among 12 delegate districts in Wyoming. County conventions held for each took place on March 12.
