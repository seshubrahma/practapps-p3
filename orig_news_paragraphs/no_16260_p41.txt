Nevertheless, I don’t believe that her achievements will much change the plight of the average woman struggling to balance work and family.    Although many women have, in recent years, attained high levels of professional success, we’re still subject to the outdated notion that women are primarily caregivers.   As such, we’re expected to balance work and family in ways not required of men. When I was a little girl, for example, I wasn’t allowed to call my father at work unless it was an emergency.
