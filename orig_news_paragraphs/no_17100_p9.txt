Even Angela Merkel, the German chancellor upon whom Obama piled praise, will face a challenge from the right if she decides to run for a fourth term. Obama left little doubt where he stood on her reelection prospects Thursday, saying he’d vote for her if he could. Merkel, in her fashion, offered a tightly constrained view of the road ahead. She said she approached Trump’s presidency with an "open mind," leaving the unknown details about the future of the relationship unsaid.
