Los Angeles (CNN) Macy Gray is known for her edgy, enigmatic voice and her soulful music about love and life, but in her 10th studio release, which will be out this summer, the   singer is getting political in a way she hasn’t before. " For me, being a black female, that puts me right in the thick of all the racism that’s going on and all the misogyny," the RB singer told CNN.
