During World War Two, we were, as a nation, All In.   And Big Business, which had gotten such a black eye during the Depression, now had a chance to redeem itself —  by showing the country, and the world, what American ingenuity and industry could produce. In fact, during World War Two, we produced an astonishing 314, 000 military aircraft —  more than six times FDR’s   hope, and more than 100 times the annual production that Marshall cited in 1939.
