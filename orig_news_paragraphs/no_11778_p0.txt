Eli Stokols writes in Politico on the recent reports that former President George H. W. Bush and other members of the Bush family will be voting for Democratic nominee Hillary Clinton. According to one source close to the Bush family, the Clintons and Bushes have a "mutual regard" for each other, as well as a "shared disgust for Trump as someone so far outside of that club" of presidential families.
