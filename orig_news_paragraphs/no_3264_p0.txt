Sunday on CNN’s "State of the Union," Sen. Cory Booker ( ) commented on Hillary Clinton saying in her commencement speech at Wellesley College that President Donald Trump’s presidency would end in impeachment for obstruction of justice.   Booker praised Clinton’s speech, but said he is not pushing for Trump’s impeachment just yet. " I just wanted to tell you, I’m not going to rush to impeachment," Booker stated.
