As confusion continued to swirl about the pending nomination of Trump, Republican National Committee Chairman Reince Priebus announced Friday the selection of four party loyalists who will fill vital posts in the convention in Cleveland.   Priebus tapped RNC vice chairwoman Enid Mickelsen and former George W. Bush political director Ron Kaufman to   the convention Rules Committee. And he also tapped former RNC Chairman Haley Barbour    a trusted power player among establishment Republicans    to lead the convention’s Committee on Permanent Organization with Wisconsin RNC Committeewoman Mary Buestrin.
