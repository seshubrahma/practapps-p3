Our country faces real threats, but we must not let legitimate    but narrow    security concerns desecrate the historic values that have helped to define our nation for more than two centuries.   We can do this better. Our immigration system is broken and, at the same time, many American workers are feeling insecure about their economic future, which makes many of them understandably less hospitable than has been our tradition.    But the approach we are seeing from Washington these days resembles a sledgehammer aimed at the immigration issue, when what we need is a scalpel.
