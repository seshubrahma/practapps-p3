For the first time in more than 100 years, the Metropolitan Opera in New York is performing a work composed by a woman. The opera, "L’Amour de Loin" ("Love From Afar") by Kaija Saariaho, above, debuted this week with a female conductor, Susanna Malkki, as well. Ms. Malkki became only the fourth woman to take the podium in the company’s history. _
