For each and every President, it was their temperament that led to their success. Like it or not, the Keirsey Presidential Evaluation reveals that Trump has the same temperament as these arguably successful past presidents. Scrutinizing the temperaments and characteristics of past presidents shows every one of them thrived on action, risks, challenges, and change. While Franklin Roosevelt was not a   strategist, he was a prodigal tactician with a very clever ability to influence people and immediate events with an extraordinary sense of timing.
