God’s word says we must be "born again"  —   a concept that is very hard to explain since it is, in one sense, one size fits all, but radically different in the particulars for every single person. In John chapter 3, before the famous sixteenth verse, Jesus discusses this new kingdom with a prominent Pharisee named Nicodemus.
