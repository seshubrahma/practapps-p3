But let’s look at what he would actually do. Recently, Trump proposed an ideology test on new immigrants that would require those coming into the United States to show they are, among other things, tolerant of LGBTQ people. Putting aside the fact that he and Mike Pence would apparently fail their own ideology test on several fronts, the very existence of such a vetting policy would create another obstacle for refugees   —  including LGBTQ people fleeing countries where they can literally be put to death simply because of who they are.
