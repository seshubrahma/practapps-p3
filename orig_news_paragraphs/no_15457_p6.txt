Investigators found that the mechanics "failed to observe explicit warnings concerning cleanliness" while working on the oxygen system    one of three major factors identified as leading to the contamination.   The incident report also noted that Boeing failed to exercise adequate oversight over the repair procedures, and the mechanics failed to learn the information taught during oxygen system training.   While Boeing would not comment on whether the mechanics mentioned in the report remain employed by the company, spokesman Ben Davis did tell CNN that they have "completed multiple investigations and corrective action plans so that we ensure stellar performance in the future.
