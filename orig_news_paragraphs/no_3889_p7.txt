18 USC 1621 ands 18 USC 1001. Section 1621 requires a person "willfully and contrary" to a sworn oath "subscribe a material matter" which is both false and the person knows to be false. Section 1001 is basically the same, without certain tribunal prerequisites: it also requires the government prove a person willfully made a materially false statement. This requires three elements: first, a false statement second, the false statement be "material" and third, the false statement be made "knowingly" and "willfully.
