Oregon voters support Democratic nominee for president Hillary R. Clinton over her GOP rival Donald J. Trump, 47 percent to his 39 percent, according to the Oct. 4   poll of 1, 248 registered voters. ["Oregon is a solidly Democratic state, but it has also been the site of political turmoil and unrest against its party establishment," said Doug Kaplan, the managing partner of Gravis Marketing, the   polling firm that conducted the poll.
