Of course, it is always the poor and vulnerable who are impacted most. These facts and figures are no abstractions for me. In February 2007 I was in Jakarta, Indonesia’s capital, as the city was paralyzed by severe flooding      the worst in its history    that inundated about 70 percent of the city, killed a number of people, cut off the highway connecting to the country’s major airport and sent about 450, 000 fleeing their homes.
