Strategic messages’ The official conceded that "Trump is right when he says we have to pay a larger portion" of NATO’s funding. Allies are supposed to spend at least 2% of GDP of defense, but only five nations    the US, Greece, the UK, Estonia and Poland    meet that target.   According to NATO statistics, the US spent an estimated $650 billion on defense in 2015    more than double the amount spent by the other 27 NATO allies put together.
