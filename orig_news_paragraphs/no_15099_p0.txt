 (CNN) Gov. Robert Bentley’s resignation came Monday, a   if   climax to a saga that had dogged him and Alabama for years.   It began with Bentley’s alleged dalliances with political adviser Rebekah Caldwell Mason, but over the years it turned into a maelstrom, sucking in everything around it: the state’s top cop, the state attorney general’s office, a US Senate seat, multiple prosecutors, the Legislature and the Alabama Ethics Commission.
