Riyadh, Saudi Arabia (CNN) Saudi Arabia announced more than $55 billion in deals with US companies Saturday, coinciding with President Donald Trump’s arrival here for a   visit. The Saudis announced they had reached preliminary agreements with US defense companies, industrial manufacturers and oil and gas companies following a   CEO Forum in Riyadh that will help Saudis make progress on their Vision 2030 plan to modernize and diversify the country ’s economy.
