A recent report found that over 15 percent of people scavenge as a means of survival. Those who eat what they find have developed the skill of finding edible bits of food where they can. In an interview with Colombia’s El Tiempo, for example, one woman noted that some neighborhoods are known for having the "good" garbage  —   the kind with food in it that can be recooked and consumed, as opposed to   waste.   A woman and her teenage daughter, who can be seen wearing a school backpack, search for food in garbage bags.
