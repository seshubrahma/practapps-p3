Details of the executive order,   On Friday, Trump signed an order banning travel from seven   countries for 90 days and suspending all refugee admission for 120 days.   The countries affected are Iran, Iraq, Syria, Sudan, Libya, Yemen and Somalia, according to a White House official. It also caps the total number of refugees admitted into the United States during the 2017 fiscal year at 50, 000, down more than half from the current level of 110, 000.   "
