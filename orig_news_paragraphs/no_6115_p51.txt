A war is happening, and one of the unforeseen casualties is the Yemeni mind. Symptoms of   stress disorder, trauma and anxiety are becoming rampant in the different corners of the country where drones are active. " Drones hover over an area for hours, sometimes days and weeks," said Rooj Alwazir, a     activist and cofounder of Support Yemen, a media collective raising awareness about issues afflicting the country.
