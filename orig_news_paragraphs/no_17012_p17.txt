For parents like Marie Stroughter, who voted for Trump, there are also important conversations to have with her three children, ages 13, 15 and 18. She said she and her children saw the "gamut of reactions" about Trump’s victory on social media, with a lot of "ugly comments" and people unfriending or threatening to unfriend her on Facebook. " We talked about how emotions are high and how we felt the last two times when things didn’t go the way we had hoped," said Stroughter,   and host of   Conservatives, referring to the 2008 and 2012 presidential elections, when a Republican lost to Barack Obama.
