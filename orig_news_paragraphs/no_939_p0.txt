Rachel Maddow had a big scoop, and she handled it her way. With a single tweet on Tuesday, Ms. Maddow, the MSNBC anchor, set the political world ablaze, announcing at 7:36 p. m. that she was poised to reveal previously unseen tax records from President Trump on her 9 p. m. program. ("Seriously," Ms. Maddow added.) In the     world of online news, 84 minutes struck some journalists as an awfully long time to wait.
