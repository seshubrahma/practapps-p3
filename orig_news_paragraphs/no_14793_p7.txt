Support for Wilders’ Party for Freedom seems to be peeling away to the more moderate conservative Christian Democrats on one side and, curiously, to the   Socialists.   Much of today’s European political spectrum is less a straight   line. It’s an oval track with candidates running around it in the direction of the popular will at the moment, with both extremes connecting as they round the circle. The challenge for any political candidate, but especially those at the extremes, is to snatch the checkered flag before someone else crosses the finish line going the other way.
