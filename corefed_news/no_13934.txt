Washington ( CNN ) Former CIA Director Leon Panetta said Thursday that Rep. Mike Pompeo , whom    Donald Trump has picked to lead the agency , is up to the task and supports Donald Trump nomination . " Donald Trump ’s somebody who understands the intelligence agencies , is smart and I think will be a good director , " Former CIA Director Leon Panetta told CNN ’s Erin Burnett on " OutFront . " Panetta , who later served as President Barack Obama ’s secretary of defense , has made similarly positive comments about defense secretary nominee James Mattis . Pompeo at Pompeo Senate confirmation hearing earlier on Thursday said it would be Pompeo mission to deliver unvarnished facts to the President and pledged to shield the agency from political influence at a time when Trump has been critical of the intelligence community . Pompeo , a Kansas Republican , also said Pompeo would not restart the CIA ’s use of enhanced interrogation techniques if Pompeo were approved for the position .
