Roll Call columnist Jonathan Allen , during Saturday ’s " Who Won the Week " segment on MSNBC ’s " AM Joy , " awarded Hillary Clinton ’s running mate Sen. Tim Kaine ( ) as his winner of the week after Kaine compared Donald Trump and Kaine supporters to slave owners at a Florida AM University rally Friday .   Allen noted how Kaine brought up abolitionist Frederick Douglass’ newspaper , The North Star , and said Kaine was comparing Donald Trump and Kaine supporters to slave owners . " Kaine was comparing Donald trump and his supporters to slave owners , which you would think the RNC and Donald Trump would be even more angry about had they been the RNC and Donald Trump to hear it , " Allen stated .
