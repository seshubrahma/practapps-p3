The traditional men ’s sword dance is known as the ardah , according to the Saudi Embassy in the United States . The performance combines dance , drumming and chanting poetry , and The performance signifies the start of notable occasions like religious holidays or weddings , according to the United Nations . Other world leaders have similarly participated in the ardah .
