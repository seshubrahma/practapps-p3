Belt and Road Forum , Beijing ( CNN )China ’s leaders are ringing in what China’s leaders hope is a new world order at a major international conference in Beijing Sunday .   The Belt and Road Forum is China’s answer to Davos or the G20 , centered around the colossal One Belt , One Road ( OBOR ) trade initiative , which takes its inspiration from the ancient Silk Road trading route .   Speaking at the opening ceremony , Chinese President Xi Jinping emphasized OBOR ’s international credentials in the face of criticism that the project will be dominated by Beijing .
