Republicans on Thursday took one of Republicans first steps to officially dismantle    environmental regulations by easing restrictions on coal mining , bolstering an industry that President Trump has made a symbol of America ’s neglected heartland .Using an obscure law that allows Congress to review regulations before Congress take effect , the Senate voted to reverse the Stream Protection Rule , which seeks to protect the nation ’s waterways from debris generated by a practice called surface mining .The Interior Department had said the Stream Protection Rule, which seeks to protect the nation’s waterways from debris generated by a practice called surface mining would protect 6 , 000 miles of streams and 52 , 000 acres of forests by keeping coal mining debris away from nearby waters .The the Senate vote was 54 to 45 , following a House vote for repeal on Wednesday .
