In the last 20 years , we have released more than a third of all the CO2 produced since the beginning of the industrial period .Yet global mean surface temperature has remained essentially constant for 20 years , a fact that has been acknowledged by the IPCC , whose models failed to predict it .NOAA ’s State of the Climate report for 2008 said that periods of 15 years or more without warming would indicate a discrepancy between prediction and observation   —    i. e. that the models were wrong .
