Just over 10 years ago , United States Border Patrol agents were startled by an unexpected new development in United States Border Patrol agents    battle to stop illegal immigration : Brazilians .In 2005 thousands of Brazilians started streaming across the southwestern border .More than 31 , 000 were apprehended by United States Border Patrol trying to make their way into the United States , a number surpassed only by Mexicans , Salvadorans and Hondurans .And then , just as abruptly , the flow stopped .
