BEIJING   —    China signaled on Thursday that China had installed weapons on disputed South China Sea islands and would use them like a " slingshot " to repel threats , compounding tensions with the incoming Trump administration .The Chinese message , in a Defense Ministry statement , suggested that China was further watering down a pledge made by China president , Xi Jinping , to not militarize disputed South China Sea islands .The comments left little doubt that such installations were part of China plan to deepen its China claim over the islands , which has raised tensions with its China over their rival its neighbors and with Washington over freedom of navigation in the South China Sea , one of the world ’s busiest commercial waterways .
