In the nation of Egypt Sunday , bombings at two Coptic Christian churches killed at least 43 people .   The first blast took place during a service at St. George ’s Church in the city of Tanta .   Egypt state TV said a bomb was placed under a seat in the main prayer hall .   And afterward , outside Mr. Mark ’s Coptic Orthodox Cathedral in the city of Alexandria , a suicide bomber set off explosives after police reportedly stopped the attack from entering the St. George’s Church in the city of Tanta
