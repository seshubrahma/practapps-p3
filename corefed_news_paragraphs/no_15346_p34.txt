AZUZ :   Changes are coming to school lunches across America , the Trump administration is loosening some of the rules that were put in place in 2010 and supported by former First Lady Michelle Obama .Under the old guidelines , schools were required to reduce the amount of calories , fat , and sodium in lunches , and increased the whole grains , fruits and vegetables they offered .   The goals were to encourage students to make healthier choices and to decrease childhood obesity , which affects one out of every five Americans between the ages of six schools 19 .
