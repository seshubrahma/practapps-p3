Washington ( CNN )US investigators believe Russian hackers breached Qatar ’s state news agency and planted a fake news report that contributed to a crisis among the US’ closest Gulf allies , according to US officials briefed on the investigation .The FBI recently sent a team of investigators to Doha to help the Qatari government investigate the alleged hacking incident , Qatari and US government officials say .   Intelligence gathered by the US security agencies indicates that Russian hackers were behind the intrusion first reported by the Qatari government two weeks ago , US officials say .
