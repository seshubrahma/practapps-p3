Washington ( CNN )Three Boeing mechanics caused $ 4 million of damage and could have sparked a    fire while repairing one of the planes in the Air Force One fleet , a federal investigation has found .   The oxygen system aboard one of the two    aircraft available to President Donald Trump was contaminated while the plane was undergoing maintenance at a Boeing plant in San Antonio , Texas between April 1 and April 10 , 2016 , according to an incident report released by Air Force investigators this week .The mechanics " caused the mishap by supplying and using    clean tools , parts , components , a regulator , and an unauthorized cleaning procedure while performing oxygen system leak checks , " according to an incident report released by Air Force investigators this week .
