Thurmont , Maryland ( CNN )Thurmont , Maryland , is a quiet , idyllic town of just more than 6 , 000 people .Kids on scooters and bikes zip down hills on peaceful ,    streets with no shortage of white picket fences .   The literal " Main Street " has a small assortment of shops and services : a secondhand store , a barber shop , a bar and grill , a church and a community park .
