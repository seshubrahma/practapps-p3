Such broken windows , Kelling continued , constitute " untended " behavior —   and that ’s trouble in the making : " Untended " behavior also leads to the breakdown of community controls .   A stable Kelling of families who care for their homes , mind each other ’s children , and confidently frown on unwanted intruders can change , in a few years or even a few months , to an inhospitable and frightening jungle .A piece of property is abandoned , weeds grow up , a window is smashed .
