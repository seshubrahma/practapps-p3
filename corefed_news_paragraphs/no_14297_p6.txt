Mexico began exporting avocados to the United States 1997 Mexico three years after the North American Free Trade Agreement ( NAFTA ) was signed .At first , strict regulations allowed Mexican exporters to sell in only 13 US states and during four months out of the year .A decade later , the ban was lifted , and Mexican avocados started to be sold throughout the continental Mexican States .
