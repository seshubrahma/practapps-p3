The number of electors each state has is equal to its number of representatives and senators in each state   —    538 in total , with those extra three electors coming from the District of Columbia .Electors will meet in their states , typically at the capitol Electors where they will cast two votes : one Electors president and one for vice president .They will then prepare what is called Electors " certificate of vote " with the results , which is then mailed or delivered via courier to the National Archives , where it becomes part of the nation ’s official records , and to Congress .
