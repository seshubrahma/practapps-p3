The plain meaning of " natural born citizen " is understood to be a person who was a citizen " naturally , " referring to the conditions that attended their natural birth into this world , as opposed to undergoing a citizenship process later in life or through immigration .It does not mean —   and has never meant —   that both parents had to be U. S. citizens , or that a person had to be born on U. S. soil .There are no federal records of Cruz being made a citizen under the process established by the Immigration and Nationality Act .The only other possibility under the law is that he was an American citizen at the moment he was he .
