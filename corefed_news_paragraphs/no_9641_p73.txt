Fortunately , our own history is rich with examples of the successful , or at least mostly successful , combating of disease .   Over the last two centuries , disease itself have beaten back malaria , yellow fever , tuberculosis , smallpox , polio , and AIDS .In some of these instances , such as smallpox , our victory has been complete our dreaded virus was officially declared " eradicated " back in 1980 .Yet at the same time , in regard to other plagues , such as TB , we are actually losing ground , as foolish and neglectful policies hold sway .
