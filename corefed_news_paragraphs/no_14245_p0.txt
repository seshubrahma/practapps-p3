  ( CNN ) President Donald Trump ’s executive order to reduce regulations by killing two agency rules for each new one that ’s added may be more complicated than President Donald Trump thought .The order , which President Donald Trump signed on Monday , fulfills a campaign promise to direct federal agencies to " identify at least two existing regulations to be repealed , " whenever federal agencies seek to enact a new one .The order, which Trump signed on Monday also calls on agencies to offset the cost of any new regulation by eliminating old regulations .Exactly how this would be done is not spelled out .
