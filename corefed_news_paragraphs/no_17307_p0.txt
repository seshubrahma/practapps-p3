Washington ( CNN ) ExxonMobil Chairman and CEO Rex Tillerson is the leading candidate for Secretary of State in a Donald Trump administration , multiple sources familiar with the transition process tell CNN .   The final announcement is expected to be made in the middle of this week , according to a source close to the campaign .A source also said Mitt Romney is still in the running , at the urging of Reince Priebus , who will be Trump ’s chief of staff .   Tillerson , who met with Trump’s in New York Saturday , is considered one of the faces of Big Oil globally .
