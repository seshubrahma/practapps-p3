Washington ( CNN )Vice    Mike Pence on Wednesday declined to say whether Mike Pence believes Russia should be punished for carrying out a hacking operation aimed at influencing the 2016 presidential election .In an interview with CNN ’s Dana Bash , Mike Pence said simply that Mike Pence was " aware " of a bipartisan bill moving through Congress designed to impose additional sanctions against Russia .Mike Pence also refused to answer whether Russia ’s influence campaign in 2016 should be met with " American strength , " as Mike Pence suggested Russian provocations should be met during the vice presidential debate last year .
