Washington ( CNN )The administration of President Donald Trump remains largely unfilled four weeks into President Donald Trump presidency , with just 34 of nearly 700 key    positions even having a candidate announced .   According to data from the nonprofit Partnership for Public Service hosted by The Washington Post , President Donald Trump has 14 officials confirmed by the Senate and 20 others are awaiting a vote , out of 696 top leadership positions in the administration .   Environmental Protection Agency Administrator Scott Pruitt was the most recent , confirmed on Friday over Democratic opposition in the Senate .
