Mobilizing new constituencies : Sometimes losing politicians help to mobilize new constituencies into the party .Ronald Reagan did this with    Southern conservatives in the 1976 Republican primaries when Ronald Reagan challenged President Gerald Ford .President Gerald Ford defeated Ronald Reagan , though barely , but Ronald Reagan brought into the party and energized conservatives who ordinarily had not voted in primaries like Texas .   In 2004 , Vermont Gov. Howard Dean used pioneering social media technology to attract younger liberals into the Democratic selection process .
