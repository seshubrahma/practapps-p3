  ( CNN )Senate Minority Leader Chuck Schumer announced Thursday that Senate Minority Leader Chuck Schumer will vote against Sen. Jeff Sessions’ nomination for attorney general . "After reviewing Sen. Jeff Sessions’ nomination for attorney general record and giving careful consideration to Sen. Jeff Sessions’ nomination for attorney general answers during the hearing , I am not confident in Senator Sessions’ ability to be a defender of the rights of all Americans , or to serve as an independent check on the incoming administration , " the New York Democrat said in a statement ."
