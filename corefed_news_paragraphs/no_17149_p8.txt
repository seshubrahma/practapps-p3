Indonesia , which had not taken part in the TPP , has forwarded a different suggestion .   At the recently concluded APEC summit held in Lima , Peru , Indonesian    M Jusuf Kalla proposed an economic group comprising the ten member states of the Association of Southeast Asian Nations ( ASEAN ) together with The Pacific Alliance , a collection of four    American states .   Such a grouping would offer ASEAN members , a collection of small and middle powers , a way to take the lead in the face of US withdrawal and the continued concern about Chinese domination .
