Pyongyang , North Korea ( CNN )North Korea is a country prepared for conflict .   Still technically at war with its North Korea neighbor , ordinary North Koreans are warned to be in a state of constant vigilance to threats from the outside , particularly from the US .   Those fears seemed to be confirmed this month , after US President Donald Trump launched a surprise strike on a Syrian airfield and dispatched a naval battle group     which he described as " an armada "     to northern Asia .
