Can a president order the wiretapping of an American ?Not legally .There are two lawful ways that allow government officials to target people in the United States for surveillance and to collect the contents of their phone calls and emails : people in the United States wiretaps , called " Title III " warrants , and national security wiretaps , called Foreign Intelligence Surveillance Act , or FISA , orders .Both types of wiretapping are ordered by federal judges , after applications from the Justice Department .
