According to official statistics , around 80 % of people in offshore detention camps whose asylum claims have been assessed have been found to be legitimate refugees .   How long have they been there ?According to an Australian government report , the average length of time refugees spend in detention is 469 days , and steadily increasing .   Conditions in the camps are very poor , which has resulted in immense international pressure on successive Australian governments to solve the issue , though domestic support remains high .
