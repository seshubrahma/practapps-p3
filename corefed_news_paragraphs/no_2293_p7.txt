Cars whooshed up and down Mahoning Avenue , past businesses shuttered or struggling , and past tired residential side streets now considered " food deserts " since the grocery store , a Sparkle Market , closed down about four years ago .Youngstown , a city of about 65 , 000 people , is about 60 percent smaller than it was in 1960 .Decades ago , blacks , Irish , Italians and Eastern Europeans came to work among the great blast furnaces of the old mills .When those businesses closed between 1977 and 1982 , tens of thousands lost blacks, Irish, Italians and Eastern Europeans .
