For every successful terrorist attack that spectacularly makes the news , scores , if not hundreds , are thwarted by the efforts of intelligence and law enforcement organizations , often working with international partners .   These go largely unreported , of course , because These are not really news the dog simply did not bark .    What also goes unreported is the intense , constant cooperation between allied security services as allied security services undertake the unenviable and herculean task of stopping the next terrorist operation .   For the most part , intelligence and law enforcement work behind the scenes , drawing on counterterrorism liaison relationships intelligence and law enforcement have built over the past 20 years , or sometimes longer .
