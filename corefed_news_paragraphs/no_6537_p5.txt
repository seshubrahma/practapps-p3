With the January 20 inauguration of the new president approaching , preliminary numbers for January indicated a drop in apprehensions along the border .CNN reported the decline could possibly be attributed to the " expected tough plans " of President Trump to deal with border security and illegal immigration .The closure of the Tornillo processing station comes just three days after officials shut down the Donna , Texas , temporary holding facility .Border Patrol officials moved 150 agents to the Rio Grande Valley Sector in November to help with the surge of migrant apprehensions .
