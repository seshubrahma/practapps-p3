The Demographia International Housing Hong Kong, Sydney, Australia, and Vancouver British Columbia Survey comes out every year .   What it does is compare The Demographia International Housing Affordability Survey with median household incomes in a given area .   The more than house prices exceed incomes The Demographia International Housing Affordability Survey the more unaffordable a place is considered .    For the ninth year straight , Hong Kong came in as the most unaffordable place to live on a list of 406 major city markets .
