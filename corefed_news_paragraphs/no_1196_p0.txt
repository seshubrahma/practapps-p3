WASHINGTON   —    A lawyer who represented Florida State University in an explosive sexual assault case and another lawyer who during the 2016 presidential campaign accused Hillary Clinton of enabling sexual predators have been chosen for key roles in the Department of Education , raising fears that the agency could pull back from enforcing civil rights in schools and on college campuses .President Trump will nominate Carlos G.
