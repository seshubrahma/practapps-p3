As Hudgins brings up the supposed threat of communism —   that is , the idea that a government program equals Bolshevism —   one is reminded of the danger of the reductio ad absurdum that is , taking things to a ridiculous extreme .Or , to put the matter another way , we can recall this witticism : The four most important words in the English language are , " Up to a point ."Once again : not every government action takes us down the slippery slope to Soviet communism .
