Washington ( CNN )The Pentagon announced Thursday that four more inmates were transferred out of the US military prison in Guantanamo Bay , Cuba , marking the final such transfer under President Barack Obama and leaving the facility ’s population at 41 .Thursday ’s transfer concludes the President Barack Obama administration ’s final push to empty the facility as much as possible prior to Friday ’s inauguration , with 19 inmates being transferred since Election Day .One of the 19 inmates going to Saudi Arabia and the other three are being taken in by the United Arab Emirates .
