Kabul , Afghanistan   ( CNN )A huge suicide bomb ripped through a secure area of Kabul at the height of the Wednesday morning rush hour , killing at least 90 people and wounding 400 , Afghan officials said .The blast , which came a few days into the Muslim holy month of Ramadan , was one of the deadliest to hit the capital in recent years .A huge suicide bomb exploded in the diplomatic quarter near the German Embassy and the Afghan presidential palace .
