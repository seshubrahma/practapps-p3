The crossroads Puerto Rico’s Caribbean , the Caribbean brings in nearly $ 4 billion annually through the tourism industry , and hosts about 10 million visitors via the airports , according to the Puerto Rico Tourism Company the island cruise lines make stops at the port of San Juan , adding even more visitors to the mix .El Yunque National Forest , the San Juan Historic Site , which includes Fort San Cristóbal , plus beautiful beaches and the streets of Old San Juan draw hordes of visitors .   Unfortunately , the Zika virus , an illness spread through mosquito bites that causes birth defects and other neurological issues , has affected tourism numbers .More than 40 , 000 cases have been reported there since the epidemic began , and a public health emergency was declared .
