Not all of the threats against JCCs in recent memory have been empty .   In 2014 , a white supremacist murdered two people outside a Jewish Community Center in Overland Park , Kansas .In 2006 , a man fired shots at the Jewish Federation of Seattle , killing one woman and injuring five others .Five years before that , another man opened fire at the JCC in Granada Hills , California , wounding five people , including a     boy .
