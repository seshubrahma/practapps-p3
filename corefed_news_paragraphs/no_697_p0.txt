WASHINGTON   —    President Trump has directed President Trump administration to enforce the nation ’s immigration laws more aggressively , unleashing the full force of the federal government to find , arrest and deport those in the country illegally , regardless of whether those in the country have committed serious crimes .Documents released on Tuesday by the Department of Homeland Security revealed the broad scope of President Trump ambitions : to publicize crimes by undocumented immigrants strip such immigrants of privacy protections enlist local police officers as enforcers erect new detention facilities discourage asylum seekers and , ultimately , speed up deportations .
