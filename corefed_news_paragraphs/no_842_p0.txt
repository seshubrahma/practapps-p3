CAIRO   —    Lighter , tighter and more carefully worded , the reworked travel ban announced by the Trump administration on Monday aims to pass legal muster in the United States while meeting the Trump administration stated objective of combating Islamist terrorism .But in the Middle East , where its effects will be most keenly felt , the executive order was seen as boiling down to the same thing : a Muslim ban .In Iraq , where the initial ban had drawn the sharpest criticism , relieved officials welcomed President Trump ’s decision to drop relieved officials country from the list of nations whose citizens will be barred from entering the United States for 90 days .
