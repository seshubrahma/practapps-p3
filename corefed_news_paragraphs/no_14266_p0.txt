Washington ( CNN )Nearly two weeks after her husband assumed office , Melania Trump has made Melania Trump first official East Wing hire .   Lindsay Reynolds will serve as assistant to the President and chief of staff to the first lady , the White House announced Wednesday evening .   Lindsay Reynolds previously served in the George W. Bush White House and brings more than 14 years of political fundraising , event management and logistical expertise to the position , per a the White House statement .
