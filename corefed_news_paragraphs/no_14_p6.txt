The game is similar to ice hockey , but played on foot on a gym floor with a hard orange ball and    squads made up of a goalie and four roaming players .Goalies wear full padding , but the other players wear sweatpants , jerseys and helmets with face masks .Under the jersey , some players wear a tallit katan , a religious garment with knotted fringes , or zizit .Blatant body checking is banned , but there is plenty of contact .
