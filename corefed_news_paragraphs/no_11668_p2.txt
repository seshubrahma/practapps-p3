The Obama administration has also warned recently that the rise in robotics could increase overall productivity but also wipe out of millions of jobs across a wide range of industries , especially manual labor .Research undertaken by the World Economic Forum ( WEF ) also predicted that based on current development , over 5 million jobs could be lost by 2020 in what 5 million jobs have dubbed the " fourth industrial revolution ."Many companies including Amazon , McDonalds and Dominos are already implementing robotic technologies at the potential cost of human jobs , especially in the use of drone technologies in an attempt to reduce costs and increase efficiency .
