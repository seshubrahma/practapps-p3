Washington ( CNN )The political tumult that rocked the world in 2016 might be an appetizer for 2017 .Crucial elections loom this year in France and Germany , where the same    backlash that produced Donald Trump and Brexit could offer an opening to nationalist leaders who oppose Muslim immigration and further erode the European unity that has been a signature of the    War II era .
