Deceiving , subverting , destabilizing : all of these tactics are cheaper and more deniable than sending in tanks or firing missiles .Electronic warfare , such as attacks on computer systems and internet communications , is a tool of growing importance , and has been used to great effect in Ukraine .   Russia realizes that the Western militaries have a substantial technological edge , but in the words of the Chief of the Russian General Staff Valery Gerasimov , the enemy " will always have vulnerabilities ."
