Miami has been the epicenter of the Cuban exile community for decades .More than 260 , 000 Cubans left in a    airlift between 1965 and 1973 .In 1980 , Castro let another 125 , 000 leave in the chaotic Mariel boatlift .At other times , desperate Cubans have fled the island nation in makeshift boats across the treacherous Straits of Florida .
