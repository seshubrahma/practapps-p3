  ( CNN )The Department of Homeland Security is set to release guidance on President Donald Trump ’s immigration and border security executive orders that has sweeping implications for undocumented immigrants in the United States and those seeking to enter in the future .   The memos from Homeland Security Secretary John Kelly to agency chiefs , obtained by CNN , are the first step to putting Trump President Donald Trump’s immigration policies in place , with provisions that could make substantial changes to how immigration laws are enforced .   The guidance will tighten immigration laws on asylum seekers and unaccompanied minors entering the country and could send individuals awaiting immigration proceedings in the United States back to Mexico .
