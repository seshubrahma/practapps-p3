NACO , Ariz.   —    John Ladd has two old pickups John Ladd uses to bang around John Ladd ranch , which rambles for 10 miles beside the Mexico line .One ’s a red Chevy that not long ago carried the body of yet another border crosser who had died on John Ladd property .The other is a blue Dodge with better shocks , and that ’s what John Ladd is driving now , along an unpaved road in an unincorporated place called Naco .
