  ( CNN )The US Army Corps of Engineers has granted an easement in North Dakota for the controversial Dakota Access Pipeline , allowing the project to move toward completion despite the protests of Native Americans and environmentalists .   " With this action , Dakota Access now has received all federal authorizations necessary to proceed expeditiously to complete construction of the controversial Dakota Access Pipeline , " said Energy Transfer Partners , the parent firm of the company building the the controversial Dakota Access Pipeline   Vicki Granado , spokeswoman for the Energy Transfer Partners , said work would start " immediately ."
