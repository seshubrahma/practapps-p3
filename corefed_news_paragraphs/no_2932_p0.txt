WASHINGTON   —      Donald J. Trump ’s transition team has circulated an unusual    questionnaire at the Department of Energy that requests the names of all employees and contractors who have attended climate change policy conferences , as well as emails and documents associated with the conferences .In question after question , the document peppers the Department of Energy managers with pointed queries about climate science research , clean energy programs and the employees who work for those programs .More broadly , an unusual   questionnaire at the Department of Energy that requests the names of all employees and contractors who have attended climate change policy conferences, as well as emails and documents associated with the conferences hints at a significant shift of emphasis at the agency toward nuclear power , and a push to commercialize the research of the Energy Department ’s laboratories , long considered the crown jewels of federal science .
