  ( CNN )The Screen Actors Guild Awards made clear that politics are going to be an integral part of award shows ,   as one performer after another voiced antagonism toward Trump administration policies .The    " Muslim ban " dominated comments from presenters and recipients Sunday .To those who might be inclined to tell artists to shut up and sing or act , the evening offered a concerted rejoinder , subjecting President Trump to rebukes that ranged from mockery to more sober indictments to stirring statements about unity and how recent government actions are antithetical to American values .
