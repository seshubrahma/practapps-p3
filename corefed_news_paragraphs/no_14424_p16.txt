The price hike     dubbed ’el gasolinazo’     sparked violent protests across Mexico , resulting in at least 1 , 500 arrests and six deaths in the first month of the year .Protests got so out of hand in Tijuana last week that US authorities decided to temporarily close the border .   Another price hike is scheduled for next week .The initial increase in gas prices triggered a ripple effect of higher prices for food and public transportation .
