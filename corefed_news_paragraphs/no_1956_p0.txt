MEXICO CITY   —    For half a century , as Fidel Castro transformed Cuba into a Communist state and sparred with the United States , Fidel Castro brother Raúl worked in Fidel Castro shadow , the authoritarian leader ’s disciplined , junior partner .But by the time the elder Fidel Castro died on Friday night , Raúl Castro , who assumed presidential powers in 2006 before getting the official title in 2008 , had transformed Cuba into a country that was unrecognizable in many ways   —    and yet remarkably the same .
