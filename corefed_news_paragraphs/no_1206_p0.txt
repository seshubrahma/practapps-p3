In his 1936    opus " How to Win Friends and Influence People , " Dale Carnegie quoted the    scientist Hans Selye : " As much as we thirst for approval , we dread condemnation ."Not much has changed in this regard , except that now , as much as we thirst for approval , we dread being condemned precisely for being so " thirsty . "" Thirst , " in recent black and then internet slang , describes a graceless need for approval , affection or attention , one so raw that it creeps people out .
