WASHINGTON   —    The Trump administration defended on Sunday a reorganization of the National Security Council that elevates the president ’s chief strategist , Stephen K. Bannon   —    a political adviser with no direct national security role   —    to full membership and downgrades the director of national intelligence and the chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff .The alteration was contained in a memorandum issued late Saturday defining the organization of the National Security Council and the Homeland Security Council , the top    bodies inside the White House on everything from diplomacy to counterterrorism to crisis management to nuclear and cyberpolicy .
