Friday on his nationally syndicated radio show , Rush Limbaugh questioned the motives of the numerous investigations into President Donald Trump and his alleged ties to the Russian government .   Rush Limbaugh said despite the lack of evidence of such ties , there are the numerous investigations into President Donald Trump and his alleged ties to the Russian government underway and that it goes beyond incompetence , which Rush Limbaugh called a " silent coup ."Rush Limbaugh went on to criticize former FBI Director James Comey , who Rush Limbaugh suggested exhibited odd behavior regarding Rush Limbaugh interactions with Trump .
