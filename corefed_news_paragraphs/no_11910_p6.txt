What is Hillary Clinton talking about ?In October 2015 Breitbart News reported that Hillary Clinton pledged gun control by executive action .The controls Hillary Clinton wants include extending the instant background checks indefinitely by changing the amount of time the FBI has to carry out a check .Hillary Clinton also wants to place new regulations on sales at gun shows and for guns sold online , and Hillary Clinton wants to expand the definition of domestic violence to include dating relationships , then use that expanded definition to ban individuals from owning guns .
