The idea was that the agency would work to remedy the effects of past discrimination .It was a precursor to a series of programs that what would later be labeled " affirmative action " policies .   At its creation the MBDA the MBDA was tasked with the mobilization of federal and local resources for the promotion of minority business efforts in the United States with a focus on Black , Hispanic , and Native Americans .In more recent years , this group was expanded to include Hasidic Jews , Asian Americans , and Pacific Islanders .
