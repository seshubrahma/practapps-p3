Susan Estrich is known for being first .A celebrated feminist legal scholar , Ms. Estrich was the first female president of The Harvard Law Review .Working for Michael S. Dukakis in 1988 , Susan Estrich was the first woman to manage a presidential campaign .And in 1991 Susan Estrich coined the term " nuts and sluts " to describe the campaign Clarence Thomas supporters waged to discredit Anita Hill .
