TEHRAN   —    The Iranian director Asghar Farhadi , whose film " The Salesman " is nominated for an Academy Award for best    movie , said on Sunday that Mr. Farhadi would not attend the Oscars ceremony next month even if Mr. Farhadi were granted an exception to President Trump ’s visa ban for citizens from Iran and several other predominantly Muslim countries .Mr. Farhadi said Mr. Farhadi had planned to attend the Feb. 26 ceremony in Los Angeles and while there bring attention to a decision Mr. Farhadi called " unjust ."
