During his first 11 days in office , President Trump has provided news outlets with plenty of material , at all hours of the morning and night .But his maelstrom of activity   —    the bold executive orders , the fiery Twitter posts , the brazen speeches   —    has also exposed , and perhaps exacerbated , ideological differences .For those devouring news about the administration , the choice of narratives has become starker , with brighter lines drawn around the content .For the readers and viewers , it ’s follow the narrative of your choice , and be wary of the great chasm between .
