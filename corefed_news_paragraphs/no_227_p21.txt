A tradition since Thomas Jefferson ’s inauguration in 1801 , the parade is one of the inauguration’s highlights a parade on Pennsylvania Avenue from the Capitol draws large crowds and TV viewers around the country .More than 8 , 000 people will march behind the president and vice president , according to the Presidential Inaugural Committee , including representatives of each branch of the American armed services , as well as high school , college and police bands that have volunteered to perform .This year ’s parade is expected to be considerably shorter   —    about 90 minutes   —    than those celebrating Mr. Trump ’s recent predecessors .
