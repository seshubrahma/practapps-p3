FDR ’s    vision —   political liberty , religious tolerance , social compassion , and national security —   was an immediate success , resonating deeply with the American public .In 1943 , in the pages The Saturday Evening Post , the brilliant illustrator Norman Rockwell immortalized the Four Freedoms in a quartet of memorable paintings .In fact , during World War Two , the actual artworks themselves went on a nationwide tour to sell victory bonds .Moreover , FDR ’s larger message punched through around the planet , and ultimately , 26 countries joined the Allied side in the war .
