WASHINGTON   —    For much of the summer , the F. B. I. pursued a widening investigation into a Russian role in the American presidential campaign .Agents scrutinized advisers close to Donald J. Trump , looked for financial connections with Russian financial figures , searched for those involved in hacking the computers of Democrats , and even chased a lead   —    which those involved in hacking the computers of Democrats ultimately came to doubt   —    about a possible secret channel of email communication from the Trump Organization to a Russian bank .
