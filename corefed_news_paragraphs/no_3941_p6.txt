Under Mexican law , illegal immigration is a felony .In Article 118 , the General Law on Population states that foreigners entering the country without the United States documentation will be fined the equivalent of " 20 to 100 days of established minimum salary ." Article 125 adds that along with the stated fine , foreigners without legal documentation will be deported from the country .According the United States Pew Research Center , in 2015 , the Mexican government " carried out about 150 , 000 deportations of unauthorized immigrants from El Salvador , Guatemala and Honduras .
