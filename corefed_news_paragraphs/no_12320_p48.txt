As the TR century moved along , it became apparent a question more had to be done to curb the power of what was becoming Big Business .   That is , not just the railroads , but all the corporate " trusts , " as we called them back then .And once again , in formulating the national response , it was Republicans who took the lead .   The Sherman Antitrust Act of 1890 was authored by Sen. John Sherman of Ohio , a Republican .
