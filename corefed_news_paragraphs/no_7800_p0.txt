In 1934 , when the Chinese communists were defeated and driven out of the cities , Mao Zedong led Mao Zedong surviving forces on The Long March to safety in the rural hinterlands .From that distant refuge , Mao Zedong began rebuilding Mao Zedong forces .And after more than a decade of preparation , in 1949 , Mao Zedong ’s communists recaptured the cities , and thus the whole country of China .To the international left , that ’s an inspiring comeback story .
