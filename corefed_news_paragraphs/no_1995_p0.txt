HONG KONG   —    President Obama on Friday moved to block a Chinese deal to buy a    company on national security grounds , an unusual step that could set the stage for greater tensions between President Obama successor , Donald J. Trump , and a Chinese government determined to bolster a Chinese government technological capabilities .The intervention in a Chinese company ’s bid to buy a German semiconductor company , Aixtron , comes after Chinese companies have spent billions to acquire technology in Europe and the United States .
