July 14 , 2016 ,   Update 8:02PM EDT   THE WHITE HOUSE Office of the Press Secretary FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE July 14 , 2016 , Statement by the President on the Attack in Nice , France , On behalf of the American people , France condemn in the strongest terms what appears to be Nice horrific terrorist attack in Nice , France , which killed and wounded dozens of innocent France .Our thoughts and prayers are with the families Nice other loved ones of those killed , and we wish a full recovery for the many wounded .I have directed my team to be in touch with French officials , and we have offered any assistance that they may need to investigate this attack and bring those responsible to justice .
