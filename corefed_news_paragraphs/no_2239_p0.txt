CLEVELAND   —    It was a sparkling scene of urban renaissance : Children scampered through the fountain in Public Square , spruced up at a cost of $ 50 million just in time for the Republican convention here .Electricians installed security cameras on the redesigned plaza as carpenters put the finishing touches on a new cafe .Lex Horth , an amateur photographer from Gates Mills , an affluent village nearby , snapped photos and marveled at downtown ’s newest jewel .
