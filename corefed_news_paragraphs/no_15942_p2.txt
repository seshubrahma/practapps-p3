The winner of Tuesday ’s special election will be granted a relatively brief lease on the seat , about 18 months , most of themselves unfolding in the muck of yet another campaign season .   And still , this contest     the most expensive in congressional history     has very real meaning to both parties .It is , and has been for months , the foremost contest of the Donald Trump era and the first that Democrats and Republicans seem to agree should be taken as a referendum on the White House and a bellwether for what ’s to come in the 2018 midterm elections .
