The cable directs embassies worldwide to identify criteria for visa applicants embassies get increased scrutiny related to potential terrorism , espionage , proliferation , or other ineligibilities .Any applicants who would ordinarily be eligible for a visa , but now meet the new local criteria , have to go through the increased screening .     Questions include the applicants travel history , addresses and jobs over the last 15 years the names of siblings , children or former spouses and all phone numbers and " " social media accounts used in the last five years .
