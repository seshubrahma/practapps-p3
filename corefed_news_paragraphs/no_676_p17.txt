With the loss of manual jobs has come a transformation in the job force , with demand growing for more data analysts , math scientists , communications specialists and robotic design engineers .In the last two years , ABB , the Swiss technology company , has opened two plants in Houston for assembling and packaging robotics and integrating advanced instrumentation into oil field operations .GE Oil and Gas opened a technology center in Oklahoma City in October to place scientists closer to the oil fields to research and apply new digital industrial technologies for exploration and production .Among its many projects is an experiment to use drones to inspect GE Oil and Gas and identify methane leaks on oil sites .
